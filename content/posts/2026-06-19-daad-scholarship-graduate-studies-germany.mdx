## Introduction to DAAD Scholarship for Migrant and Refugee Students
The DAAD Scholarship is a prestigious award that provides financial support to migrant and refugee students pursuing graduate studies in Germany. If you are a migrant or refugee student looking to further your education in Germany, the DAAD Scholarship is an excellent opportunity to consider. With various scholarships available, you can find one that suits your needs and supports your Master's or PhD studies in Germany.

## Who Qualifies for the DAAD Scholarship
To be eligible for the DAAD Scholarship, you must fall under one of the following visa categories:
* Subsidiary protection
* Refugee status
* Asylum seeker
* Temporary residence permit
* Humanitarian residence permit
* Other residence permits for humanitarian reasons

## Available Scholarships for Migrant and Refugee Students
### DAAD Scholarship for Master's Studies
The DAAD Scholarship for Master's Studies provides a monthly stipend of 750 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel allowance. To be eligible, you must have a Bachelor's degree and meet the language requirements of the university. The deadline for application is usually in October of each year, and you can find more information on the [official DAAD website](https://www.daad.de/en/).

### Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung Scholarship
The Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung Scholarship offers a monthly stipend of 1,000 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel allowance. To be eligible, you must have a strong academic record and be pursuing a Master's or PhD degree in a field related to politics, economics, or law. The deadline for application is usually in July of each year, and you can find more information on the [official KAS website](https://www.kas.de/en/).

### Friedrich-Ebert-Stiftung Scholarship
The Friedrich-Ebert-Stiftung Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel allowance. To be eligible, you must have a strong academic record and be pursuing a Master's or PhD degree in a field related to social sciences, politics, or economics. The deadline for application is usually in November of each year, and you can find more information on the [official FES website](https://www.fes.de/en/).

### Heinrich-Böll-Stiftung Scholarship
The Heinrich-Böll-Stiftung Scholarship offers a monthly stipend of 1,000 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel allowance. To be eligible, you must have a strong academic record and be pursuing a Master's or PhD degree in a field related to politics, economics, or law. The deadline for application is usually in March of each year, and you can find more information on the [official HBS website](https://www.boell.de/en).

### Deutschlandstipendium Scholarship
The Deutschlandstipendium Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 300 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel allowance. To be eligible, you must have a strong academic record and be pursuing a Master's or PhD degree at a German university. The deadline for application is usually in July of each year, and you can find more information on the [official DS website](https://www.deutschlandstipendium.de/en/).

1. Check the eligibility criteria for the DAAD Scholarship and ensure you meet the requirements.
