## Turkish Government Scholarships for Asylum Seekers: A Comprehensive Guide
The Turkish Government Scholarships are available for asylum seekers who are currently residing in Turkey with a valid visa, offering them a chance to pursue higher education in the country. 
Asylum seekers who qualify for these scholarships include those with the following visa categories:
* International Protection Applicant Visa
* International Protection Status Holder Visa
* Temporary Protection Identity Document
* Humanitarian Residence Permit
* Refugee Visa

### 2026 Research Scholarship 
The 2026 Research Scholarship provides a coverage amount of up to 1,500 TL per month, and is eligible for asylum seekers who are currently enrolled in a PhD program in Turkey. The deadline for application is 15 February 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/en.

### Turkiye Burslari Scholarship 
The Turkiye Burslari Scholarship offers a coverage amount of up to 2,600 TL per month for undergraduate students, 2,800 TL per month for master's students, and 3,000 TL per month for PhD students, and is eligible for asylum seekers who have been accepted into a Turkish university. The deadline for application is 20 November 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/en.

### Higher Education Scholarship 
The Higher Education Scholarship provides a coverage amount of up to 1,800 TL per month, and is eligible for asylum seekers who are currently enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate program in Turkey. The deadline for application is 31 January 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.yok.gov.tr/en.

### Postgraduate Scholarship 
The Postgraduate Scholarship offers a coverage amount of up to 2,200 TL per month, and is eligible for asylum seekers who have been accepted into a postgraduate program in Turkey. The deadline for application is 15 March 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/en.

### PhD Scholarship 
The PhD Scholarship provides a coverage amount of up to 2,500 TL per month, and is eligible for asylum seekers who have been accepted into a PhD program in Turkey. The deadline for application is 30 April 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.yok.gov.tr/en.

## Application Process
To apply for the Turkish Government Scholarships, asylum seekers must follow these steps:
1. Check the eligibility criteria for each scholarship to ensure they qualify.
2. Gather all the required documents, including a valid passport, visa, and academic transcripts.
3. Fill out the online application form on the official website of the scholarship.
4. Upload all the required documents to the online application portal.
5. Submit the application form before the deadline.
6. Wait for the application to be reviewed and processed.
7. Receive an email notification if the application is successful.
8. Follow the instructions in the email to complete the scholarship acceptance process.

## Required Documents
Asylum seekers must submit the following documents as part of their scholarship application:
* A valid passport
* A valid visa or residence permit
* Academic transcripts and diplomas
* Language proficiency test results (e.g. TOEFL or IELTS)
* A motivation letter
* A recommendation letter from a academic supervisor or employer
