If you are a migrant student looking to study in Germany, you may be wondering if you qualify for DAAD scholarships. The answer is yes, DAAD scholarships are available for migrant students who meet certain eligibility criteria, including having a strong academic record, being enrolled in a German university, and demonstrating financial need. Who qualifies for DAAD scholarships? Migrant students who have been accepted into a German university, have a strong academic record, and demonstrate financial need are eligible to apply for DAAD scholarships. Here are some of the DAAD scholarships available for migrant students in Germany: 1. DAAD Scholarship for Master's Studies: This scholarship covers a monthly stipend of 750 euros, health insurance, and travel costs. Deadline: October 31st, 2026. 2. DAAD Scholarship for PhD Studies: This scholarship covers a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros, health insurance, and travel costs. Deadline: October 31st, 2026. 3. DAAD Helmut-Schmidt-Programme (HSP): This scholarship covers a monthly stipend of 750 euros, health insurance, and travel costs. Deadline: July 31st, 2026. 4. DAAD Scholarship for Language Course: This scholarship covers the cost of a language course, accommodation, and living expenses. Deadline: April 30th, 2026. 5. DAAD-EPOS Scholarship: This scholarship covers a monthly stipend of 750 euros, health insurance, and travel costs. Deadline: October 31st, 2026. To apply for a DAAD scholarship, follow these steps: Step 1: Choose the scholarship you want to apply for and check the eligibility criteria. Step 2: Prepare the required documents, including a motivational letter, CV, transcripts, and language proficiency certificate. Step 3: Submit your application through the DAAD online portal. Step 4: Wait for the selection committee to review your application. Step 5: If selected, receive your scholarship award and begin your studies in Germany. Documents checklist: - Motivational letter - CV - Transcripts - Language proficiency certificate - Letter of acceptance from a German university - Proof of financial need. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs): Q: What is the deadline for DAAD scholarship applications? A: The deadline varies depending on the scholarship, but most applications are due by October 31st, 2026. Q: Can I apply for multiple DAAD scholarships? A: Yes, you can apply for multiple DAAD scholarships, but you can only receive one award. Q: What language proficiency is required for DAAD scholarships? A: English language proficiency is required, with a minimum score of 6.5 in IELTS or 95 in TOEFL. Q: Can I apply for a DAAD scholarship if I am already studying in Germany? A: Yes, you can apply for a DAAD scholarship if you are already studying in Germany, but you must meet the eligibility criteria. Q: How long does it take to receive a DAAD scholarship award? A: The selection committee reviews applications and makes awards within 2-3 months after the deadline. Q: Can I renew my DAAD scholarship? A: Yes, you can renew your DAAD scholarship for up to two years, but you must meet the renewal criteria and submit a new application.
