## German DAAD Scholarships for Migrant and Refugee Students: A Fully Funded Opportunity
The German DAAD Scholarships are available for migrant and refugee students to pursue master's and Ph.D. studies in Germany, providing a fully funded opportunity for individuals with a refugee or migrant background. 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the German DAAD Scholarships, migrant and refugee students must meet specific visa category requirements, including:
* Visa category "humanitarian" or "refugee"
* Visa category "family reunification"
* Visa category "asylum"
* Visa category "subsidary protection"
* Visa category "tolerated stay"

## Available Scholarships
The German DAAD Scholarships offer a range of programs for migrant and refugee students, including:
### Helmut-Schmidt-Programme
The Helmut-Schmidt-Programme provides a monthly stipend of 934 euros, plus additional allowances for health insurance, travel, and family members, to students pursuing master's studies in Germany. Eligible students must hold a bachelor's degree and have a strong academic record. The deadline for application is November 2026, and more information can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Development-Related Postgraduate Courses
The Development-Related Postgraduate Courses scholarship provides full tuition fees, a monthly stipend of 934 euros, and additional allowances for master's and Ph.D. studies in development-related fields. Eligible students must hold a bachelor's or master's degree and have a strong academic record. The deadline for application is October 2026, and more information can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Graduate School Scholarship Programme
The Graduate School Scholarship Programme provides a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros, plus additional allowances for health insurance, travel, and family members, to students pursuing Ph.D. studies in Germany. Eligible students must hold a master's degree and have a strong academic record. The deadline for application is February 2027, and more information can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Public Policy and Good Governance
The Public Policy and Good Governance scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 934 euros, plus additional allowances for health insurance, travel, and family members, to students pursuing master's studies in public policy and good governance. Eligible students must hold a bachelor's degree and have a strong academic record. The deadline for application is August 2026, and more information can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### EPOS Programme

Eligible students must hold a bachelor's or master's degree and have a strong academic record. The deadline for application is September 2026, and more information can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
