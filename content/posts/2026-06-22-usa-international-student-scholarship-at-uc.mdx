## Introduction to USA's International Student Scholarship at the University of California for Asylum Seekers
The University of California offers various international student scholarships to asylum seekers pursuing undergraduate and graduate studies in the USA. These scholarships provide financial assistance to help asylum seekers achieve their academic goals. As an asylum seeker, you can apply for these scholarships to support your education in the United States.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the University of California's international student scholarships, you must be an asylum seeker with a valid visa category, including:
* Asylum status (AS)
* Refugee status (RS)
* Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
* Deferred Enforced Departure (DED)
* Special Immigrant Visa (SIV)
* Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA)
* Withholding of Removal (WOR)
* Convention Against Torture (CAT)

## Available Scholarships
The University of California offers various scholarships to asylum seekers, including:
### The University of California President's Scholarship
The University of California President's Scholarship provides $10,000 to $20,000 per year to undergraduate and graduate students. To be eligible, you must be an incoming freshman or transfer student with a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline to apply is January 15th. For more information, visit [https://admission.universityofcalifornia.edu/](https://admission.universityofcalifornia.edu/).
### The UC Berkeley Scholarship
The UC Berkeley Scholarship provides $5,000 to $15,000 per year to undergraduate and graduate students. To be eligible, you must be a Berkeley student with a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline to apply is March 1st. For more information, visit [https://financialaid.berkeley.edu/](https://financialaid.berkeley.edu/).
### The UCLA Scholarship
The UCLA Scholarship provides $2,000 to $10,000 per year to undergraduate and graduate students. To be eligible, you must be a UCLA student with a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline to apply is April 1st. For more information, visit [https://financialaid.ucla.edu/](https://financialaid.ucla.edu/).
### The UC Davis Scholarship
The UC Davis Scholarship provides $1,500 to $8,000 per year to undergraduate and graduate students. To be eligible, you must be a UC Davis student with a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline to apply is May 1st. For more information, visit [https://financialaid.ucdavis.edu/](https://financialaid.ucdavis.edu/).
### The UC San Diego Scholarship
The UC San Diego Scholarship provides $1,000 to $6,000 per year to undergraduate and graduate students. To be eligible, you must be a UC San Diego student with a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline to apply is June 1st. For more information, visit [https://financialaid.ucsd.edu/](https://financialaid.ucsd.edu/).
