# Women Funding
The [Australia Awards Scholarships](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/) exist and qualify migrant women from participating countries with up to AUD 30,000 per year for living expenses for the duration of their studies.

## Who Qualifies
Migrant women who hold an Australian visa such as a Student visa (subclass 500), a Bridging visa, or a Protection visa may be eligible for women-only emergency funding scholarships at Australian universities. To qualify, applicants must meet the eligibility criteria for each scholarship, which may include requirements such as academic performance, language proficiency, and financial need. Women from diverse backgrounds, including those with refugee status or seeking asylum, may also be eligible for [scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees).

## Available Scholarships
The following five scholarships are available to migrant women in Australia:
### Australian Government Research Training Program
* Amount: up to AUD 28,597 per year
* Eligibility: migrant women enrolled in a PhD or research master's degree at an Australian university
* Deadline: varies depending on the university
* Official URL: [https://www.education.gov.au/research-training-program](https://www.education.gov.au/research-training-program)
### Westpac Future Leaders Scholarship
* Amount: up to AUD 120,000 over 2-3 years
* Eligibility: migrant women who are Australian citizens or permanent residents, and are enrolled in a postgraduate degree at an Australian university
* Deadline: 31 August 2026
* Official URL: [https://www.westpac.com.au/about-westpac/scholarships/future-leaders/](https://www.westpac.com.au/about-westpac/scholarships/future-leaders/)
### Women in Engineering Scholarship
* Amount: AUD 5,000 per year
* Eligibility: migrant women enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate degree in engineering at an Australian university
* Deadline: 30 June 2026
* Official URL: [https://www.engineersaustralia.org.au/Engineering-Profession/Women-in-Engineering](https://www.engineersaustralia.org.au/Engineering-Profession/Women-in-Engineering)
### Equity Scholarship
* Amount: up to AUD 5,000 per year
* Eligibility: migrant women who are enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate degree at an Australian university, and can demonstrate financial need
* Deadline: 31 March 2026
* Official URL: [https://www.studyassist.gov.au/scholarships](https://www.studyassist.gov.au/scholarships)
### Australian Awards Leadership Program
* Amount: up to AUD 10,000 per year
* Eligibility: migrant women from participating countries who are enrolled in a postgraduate degree at an Australian university
* Deadline: 30 April 2026
* Official URL: [https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/leadership/](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/leadership/)

These scholarships can help migrant women pursue their academic goals, including [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) and [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded).
