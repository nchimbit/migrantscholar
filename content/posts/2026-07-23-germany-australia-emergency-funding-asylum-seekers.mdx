The Albert Einstein German Academic Refugee Initiative (DAFI) scholarship exists for asylum seekers and refugees, providing funding of up to €750 per month. 

## Who Qualifies
Asylum seekers in Germany and Australia may be eligible for emergency funding through various scholarships and grants. To qualify, applicants typically need to hold a specific visa category, such as a temporary protection visa or a permanent humanitarian visa. In Germany, applicants may hold a residence permit for humanitarian reasons, while in Australia, they may hold a bridging visa or a permanent protection visa.

## Available Scholarships
### DAFI Scholarship
The DAFI scholarship provides up to €750 per month for undergraduate studies. Eligible applicants include refugees and asylum seekers who have been granted a residence permit in Germany. Deadline: varies, [official URL](https://www.unhcr.org/dafi-scholarship-programme).
### Australia Awards Scholarship
The Australia Awards Scholarship provides full funding for undergraduate and postgraduate studies. Eligible applicants include citizens of participating countries, including those holding a permanent humanitarian visa in Australia. Deadline: April 30, [official URL](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au).
### Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung Scholarship
The Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung scholarship provides up to €1,200 per month for postgraduate studies. Eligible applicants include international students, including asylum seekers and refugees, who are studying in Germany. Deadline: varies, [official URL](https://www.kas.de/en).
### Deutschlandstipendium
The Deutschlandstipendium provides up to €300 per month for undergraduate and postgraduate studies. Eligible applicants include international students, including asylum seekers and refugees, who are studying in Germany. Deadline: varies, [official URL](https://www.deutschlandstipendium.de).
### Rotary Peace Fellowship
The Rotary Peace Fellowship provides full funding for postgraduate studies. Eligible applicants include asylum seekers and refugees who are applying for a master's degree in a field related to peace and conflict resolution. Deadline: May 31, [official URL](https://www.rotary.org/en/peace-fellowships).
