The University of Toronto's Scholars at Risk Scholarship, which qualifies Syrian refugees, offers a funding amount of up to $10,000. This scholarship is one of the many [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) available to refugees in Canada. 

## Who Qualifies
Syrian refugees who have been granted one of the following visa categories are eligible to apply for these scholarships: Protected Person, Convention Refugee, or Humanitarian Protected Person. Students with a [PhD scholarship](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) or master's degree can also apply. 

## Available Scholarships
### University of Toronto Scholars at Risk Scholarship
The University of Toronto Scholars at Risk Scholarship offers up to $10,000, is open to Syrian refugees, and has a deadline that varies. Check the [official website](https://www.utoronto.ca/) for more information.
### Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program
The Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program provides full funding, is open to refugees, and has a deadline of December 10. Apply on the [official website](https://mastercardfdn.org/).
### Ontario Graduate Scholarship
The Ontario Graduate Scholarship offers up to $15,000, is open to Syrian refugees, and has a deadline of April 30. Apply on the [official website](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-graduate-scholarship).
### Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarship
The Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarship provides up to $60,000, is open to refugees, and has a deadline of December 21. Apply on the [official website](https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/).
### Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship
The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship offers up to $50,000, is open to Syrian refugees, and has a deadline of November 1. Apply on the [official website](https://vanier.gc.ca/).
