## Introduction to DAAD Scholarships for International Students from Turkey in Germany
If you're a migrant student from Turkey looking to study in Germany, you're likely wondering what scholarship options are available to you. The DAAD (Deutscher Akademischer Austauschdienst) offers a range of scholarships to international students from Turkey, covering various fields of study and degree levels. These scholarships aim to promote international student mobility and foster academic exchange between Turkey and Germany.

## Who Qualifies for DAAD Scholarships
To be eligible for DAAD scholarships, you must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* Student visa (Visum für Studienzwecke)
* Language course visa (Visum für Sprachkurs)
* Internship visa (Visum für Praktika)
* Research visa (Visum für Forschung)
* Academic staff visa (Visum für wissenschaftliches Personal)

## Available DAAD Scholarships for International Students from Turkey
### Development-Related Postgraduate Courses (Epos)
The Epos scholarship covers €750 per month for master's students, with an additional €1,500 per month for PhD students. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree and at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is September 2026. For more information, visit [www.daad.de/epos](http://www.daad.de/epos).

### Helmut-Schmidt-Programme (HSP)
The HSP scholarship offers €750 per month for master's students, with an additional €1,500 per month for PhD students. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree and at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is July 2026. For more information, visit [www.daad.de/hsp](http://www.daad.de/hsp).

### Study Scholarships for International Graduates in the Field of the Humanities
This scholarship covers €750 per month for master's students, with an additional €1,500 per month for PhD students. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree in the humanities and a strong academic record. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit [www.daad.de/humanities](http://www.daad.de/humanities).

### Research Grants for Doctoral Candidates and Young Academics and Scientists
This grant offers €1,200 per month for doctoral candidates, with an additional €1,500 per month for young academics and scientists. Eligible applicants must have a master's degree and a strong research proposal. The deadline for application is August 2026. For more information, visit [www.daad.de/research-grants](http://www.daad.de/research-grants).

### Re-Invitation Programme for Former Scholarship Holders
This programme offers €2,000 per month for former scholarship holders, with an additional €1,500 per month for accompanying family members. Eligible applicants must have previously held a DAAD scholarship and have a strong academic or research record. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [www.daad.de/re-invitation](http://www.daad.de/re-invitation).

## Application Steps for DAAD Scholarships
1. Check your eligibility for the desired scholarship programme.
2. Review the scholarship requirements and application deadlines.
3. Prepare your application documents, including your CV, transcripts, and language proficiency certificates.
4. Submit your application through the DAAD online portal.
5. Wait for the application review process to be completed.
6. Receive an email notification with the result of your application.
7. If selected, apply for a student visa at the German embassy or consulate in Turkey.
8. Register at the German university and begin your studies.
