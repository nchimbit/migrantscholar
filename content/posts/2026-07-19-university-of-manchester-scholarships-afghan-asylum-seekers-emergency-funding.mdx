# UK Aid
The University of Manchester Scholarships qualify Afghan asylum seekers for emergency funding of up to £22,000 per year. 
The University of Manchester offers various scholarships for asylum seekers, and one such scholarship is the [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) for students pursuing undergraduate or postgraduate studies. 
These scholarships provide essential funding for students who are unable to access student finance due to their immigration status.

## Who Qualifies
The University of Manchester Scholarships are available to Afghan asylum seekers with a pending asylum claim or those who have been granted discretionary leave to remain in the UK. 
This includes individuals with the following visa categories: 
- Discretionary Leave to Remain 
- Limited Leave to Remain 
- Humanitarian Protection 
- Refugee Status 
- Unaccompanied Asylum-Seeking Children

## Application Steps
To apply for these scholarships, Afghan asylum seekers should follow these steps:
1. Check the eligibility criteria for each scholarship
2. Choose the scholarship that best fits their needs and qualifications
3. Review the course options and choose the course they wish to pursue
4. Gather all required documents, including proof of immigration status and academic qualifications
5. Submit the application form before the deadline
6. Provide a personal statement outlining their background and reasons for applying
7. Provide a letter of recommendation from a teacher, academic advisor, or community leader
8. Follow up with the university to confirm receipt of the application

## Documents Checklist
The following documents are required for scholarship applications:
* Proof of immigration status, such as a biometric residence permit or a letter from the Home Office
* Academic qualifications, such as diplomas or certificates
* Transcripts or academic records
* Personal statement outlining background and reasons for applying
* Letter of recommendation from a teacher, academic advisor, or community leader
* English language proficiency test results, such as IELTS or TOEFL
