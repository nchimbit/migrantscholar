The Helena Kennedy Foundation Scholarship exists for refugee women in the UK, providing £2,000 to support their education.

## Who Qualifies
Refugee women with a visa category of Humanitarian Protection, Refugee Status, or Limited Leave to Remain are eligible for emergency funding in the UK. They must be pursuing higher education and facing financial difficulties. Visa categories that qualify include:
- Humanitarian Protection
- Refugee Status
- Limited Leave to Remain
- Discretionary Leave to Remain
- Leave outside the rules

## Available Scholarships
### The Helena Kennedy Foundation Scholarship
- Amount: £2,000
- Eligibility: Refugee women in the UK
- Deadline: September 30th
- Official URL: https://www.hkf.org.uk/our-work/scholarships
### The Allan and Nesta Ferguson Charitable Trust Scholarship
- Amount: £10,000
- Eligibility: Refugee students in the UK
- Deadline: July 31st
- Official URL: https://www.fergusontrust.co.uk/scholarships
### The UNHCR DAFI Scholarship
- Amount: £5,000
- Eligibility: Refugee students in the UK
- Deadline: May 31st
- Official URL: https://www.unhcr.org/dafi-scholarship-programme
### The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships and Leadership Programme
- Amount: Full tuition fee
- Eligibility: Refugee students in the UK
- Deadline: January 31st
- Official URL: https://www.ox.ac.uk/admissions/graduate/fees-and-funding/graduate-scholarships/weidenfeld-hoffmann
### The University of London Refugee Scholarship
- Amount: £10,000
- Eligibility: Refugee students in the UK
- Deadline: June 30th
- Official URL: https://london.ac.uk/study-at-london/fees-and-funding/scholarships/university-london-refugee-scholarship
