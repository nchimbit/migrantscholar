## Turkey and Australia Government Scholarships for International Students
The Turkish Government Scholarship and Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship are available for international students, including migrants and refugees, to pursue higher education and research in Turkey and Australia.

## Who Qualifies
The following international students qualify for these scholarships:
* Visa subclasses 500, 570, 571, 572, 573, 574, and 575 for Australia
* Visa categories for study in Turkey as specified by the Turkish Government

## Scholarship Opportunities
### Turkish Government Scholarship
The Turkish Government Scholarship covers full tuition fees, a monthly stipend of 1,100 Turkish Lira for undergraduate students and 1,600 Turkish Lira for graduate students, and other expenses. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be under 25 years old for undergraduate studies or under 30 years old for graduate studies. The deadline for application is February 2027. For more information, visit https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/.

### Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship
The Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship covers full tuition fees and a living allowance of AUD 28,597 per annum. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and meet the English language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit https://www.education.gov.au/.

### Fulbright Scholarship
The Fulbright Scholarship covers full tuition fees, a monthly stipend, and other expenses. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be a citizen of a participating country. The deadline for application is August 2026. For more information, visit https://www.fulbright.gov.au/.

### Endeavour Scholarship
The Endeavour Scholarship covers full tuition fees, a monthly stipend of AUD 3,000, and other expenses. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be a citizen of a participating country. The deadline for application is June 2026. For more information, visit https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/.

### Jean Monnet Scholarship
The Jean Monnet Scholarship covers full tuition fees, a monthly stipend of 1,100 Turkish Lira for undergraduate students and 1,600 Turkish Lira for graduate students, and other expenses. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be under 25 years old for undergraduate studies or under 30 years old for graduate studies. The deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, visit https://www.jeanmonnet.org.tr/.

## Application Steps
1. Check the eligibility criteria and requirements for each scholarship.
2. Choose the scholarship and university that best fit your academic goals and interests.
3. Prepare and gather the required documents, including transcripts, certificates, and language proficiency test scores.
4. Submit your application through the official website or online portal.
5. Wait for the application to be processed and reviewed.
6. Receive and review the offer letter, if your application is successful.
7. Accept the offer and confirm your enrollment.
8. Apply for a student visa and make travel arrangements to Turkey or Australia.
