# Turkey Scholar
Pakistani students in Australia are eligible for the Turkish Government Scholarship, which provides funding of up to $750 per month. The Turkish Government Scholarship is a [fully funded scholarship](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) that aims to support international students, including those from Pakistan, in pursuing higher education in Turkey.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Turkish Government Scholarship, Pakistani students in Australia must hold a valid visa, such as a student visa, tourist visa, or work visa. The following visa categories are eligible: Subclass 500, Subclass 600, Subclass 801, and Subclass 820. Students with [scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) status are also encouraged to apply.
