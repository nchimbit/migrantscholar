## Introduction to DAAD Fully Funded PhD Scholarships in Germany
If you are an international student looking to pursue a PhD in Germany, the DAAD Fully Funded PhD Scholarships are an excellent opportunity to consider. These scholarships provide full funding for international students to conduct research in Germany, covering all expenses including tuition, living costs, and travel. The DAAD scholarships are offered by the German Academic Exchange Service, a non-profit organization that aims to promote international academic exchange and cooperation.

## Who Qualifies for DAAD Fully Funded PhD Scholarships
To be eligible for the DAAD Fully Funded PhD Scholarships, you must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* Visa for study purposes (student visa)
* Visa for research purposes (research visa)
* Visa for employment purposes (work visa)
* Visa for family reunification purposes (family reunification visa)
* Visa for humanitarian purposes (humanitarian visa)
* Blue Card EU (highly skilled workers)
* EU Blue Card (highly skilled workers)
* Residence permit for other purposes (other purposes)

## Available Scholarships
The following are five DAAD Fully Funded PhD Scholarships available for international students in Germany:
### PhD Scholarship in Engineering
The PhD Scholarship in Engineering offers a coverage amount of EUR 1,200 per month for 36 months, and is eligible for international students with a master's degree in engineering. The deadline for application is October 15, 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.daad.de/en/.

The PhD Scholarship in Natural Sciences offers a coverage amount of EUR 1,200 per month for 36 months, and is eligible for international students with a master's degree in natural sciences. The deadline for application is November 1, 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.daad.de/en/.
### PhD Scholarship in Social Sciences
The PhD Scholarship in Social Sciences offers a coverage amount of EUR 1,200 per month for 36 months, and is eligible for international students with a master's degree in social sciences. The deadline for application is December 1, 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.daad.de/en/.

## Application Steps
To apply for the DAAD Fully Funded PhD Scholarships, follow these steps:
1. Choose your desired PhD program and university in Germany.
2. Check the eligibility criteria for the scholarship and ensure you meet all requirements.
3. Prepare all required documents, including your resume, transcripts, and language proficiency certificates.
4. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline.
5. Pay the application fee, if required.
6. Wait for the application to be processed and reviewed.
7. Receive an email notification of the application outcome.
8. If selected, apply for a student visa and make arrangements to travel to Germany.
