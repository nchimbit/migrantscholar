The Friedrich Ebert Foundation Scholarship is available to Syrian refugees in Germany, providing a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros for undergraduate and graduate studies in STEM fields.

## Who Qualifies
Syrian refugees with a residence permit in Germany, including those with a temporary residence permit (Aufenthaltstitel) or a permanent residence permit (Niederlassungserlaubnis), may be eligible to apply for fully funded STEM scholarships. Additionally, applicants with a humanitarian residence permit or a residence permit for family reunification may also be considered. Visa categories that may be eligible include: 
- Subsidiary protection visa (zB)
- Temporary protection visa (eAT)
- Family reunification visa ( § 29 Abs. 1 und 2 AufenthG)

## Available Scholarships
### Friedrich Ebert Foundation Scholarship
- Amount: 1,200 euros per month
- Eligibility: Syrian refugees with a strong academic record and social commitment
- Deadline: varies, check the [official website](https://www.fes.de/en/scholarship)
- Official URL: https://www.fes.de/en/scholarship

### DAAD Scholarship
- Amount: 750 euros per month
- Eligibility: Syrian refugees with a bachelor's degree and a strong academic record
- Deadline: varies, check the [official website](https://www.daad.de/en/)
- Official URL: https://www.daad.de/en/

### Deutschlandstipendium
- Amount: 300 euros per month
- Eligibility: Syrian refugees with a strong academic record and social commitment
- Deadline: varies, check the [official website](https://www.deutschlandstipendium.de/en/)
- Official URL: https://www.deutschlandstipendium.de/en/

### Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung Scholarship
- Amount: 1,000 euros per month
- Eligibility: Syrian refugees with a strong academic record and social commitment
- Deadline: varies, check the [official website](https://www.kas.de/en/)
- Official URL: https://www.kas.de/en/

### Heinrich Böll Foundation Scholarship
- Amount: 1,000 euros per month
- Eligibility: Syrian refugees with a strong academic record and social commitment
- Deadline: varies, check the [official website](https://www.boell.de/en/scholarships)
- Official URL: https://www.boell.de/en/scholarships
