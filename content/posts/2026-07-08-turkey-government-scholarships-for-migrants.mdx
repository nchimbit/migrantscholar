## Turkey Government Scholarships for Migrant Students: A Guide
Turkey Government Scholarships are available for migrant students, providing them with the opportunity to pursue undergraduate and graduate programs in Turkey, with the aim of promoting cultural exchange and education.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for Turkey Government Scholarships, migrant students must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* Refugee visa
* Humanitarian visa
* Student visa
* Family reunification visa
* Work visa
* Asylum seeker visa
* Temporary protection visa
* Long-term residence visa

## Available Scholarships
### Türkiye Scholarships
The Türkiye Scholarships program provides a monthly stipend of 700-950 Turkish Lira, tuition fees, and other expenses, to undergraduate and graduate students. Eligible students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be under the age of 25 for undergraduate programs and under 30 for graduate programs. The deadline for application is February 20, 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/](https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/).

### Sabancı University Scholarship
The Sabancı University Scholarship offers a full tuition waiver and a monthly stipend of 1,500 Turkish Lira to graduate students. Eligible students must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and be under the age of 30. The deadline for application is June 1, 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.sabanciuniv.edu/en](https://www.sabanciuniv.edu/en).

### Koç University Scholarship
The Koç University Scholarship provides a full tuition waiver and a monthly stipend of 1,800 Turkish Lira to undergraduate and graduate students. Eligible students must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and be under the age of 25 for undergraduate programs and under 30 for graduate programs. The deadline for application is May 15, 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.ku.edu.tr/en](https://www.ku.edu.tr/en).

### Bilkent University Scholarship
The Bilkent University Scholarship offers a full tuition waiver and a monthly stipend of 1,200 Turkish Lira to undergraduate and graduate students. Eligible students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be under the age of 25 for undergraduate programs and under 30 for graduate programs. The deadline for application is April 15, 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.bilkent.edu.tr/en](https://www.bilkent.edu.tr/en).

### Istanbul Technical University Scholarship
The Istanbul Technical University Scholarship provides a full tuition waiver and a monthly stipend of 1,000 Turkish Lira to undergraduate and graduate students. Eligible students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be under the age of 25 for undergraduate programs and under 30 for graduate programs. The deadline for application is March 1, 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.itu.edu.tr/en](https://www.itu.edu.tr/en).

## Application Steps
1. Check the eligibility criteria for the desired scholarship program.
2. Prepare all required documents, including transcripts, diplomas, and language proficiency certificates.
3. Fill out the online application form on the official website of the scholarship program.
4. Upload all required documents to the online application system.
5. Submit the application and receive a confirmation email.
6. Wait for the evaluation and selection process to be completed.
7. Receive the scholarship award letter and other necessary documents.
8. Apply for a student visa and travel to Turkey to start the academic program.
