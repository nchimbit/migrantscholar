## Introduction to Turkey's Fully Funded Graduate Scholarship Program
Are you an international student, migrant, or refugee looking to pursue your master's or PhD studies in Turkey? The Turkish government offers a fully funded graduate scholarship program for international students, providing them with the opportunity to study at prestigious Turkish universities. This program covers tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits, making it an attractive option for students from around the world.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for Turkey's fully funded graduate scholarship program, you must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* Student visa
* Research visa
* Family residence visa
* Humanitarian residence visa
* Refugee residence visa
* Temporary protection visa
* International protection applicant visa

## Available Scholarships
### Türkiye Scholarships
The Türkiye Scholarships program provides a monthly stipend of 950 Turkish Lira for master's students and 1,400 Turkish Lira for PhD students, as well as tuition fees, accommodation, and health insurance. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0 for master's studies and a master's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.5 for PhD studies. The deadline for application is February 20, 2026, and the official website is [https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/](https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/).

### Ibn Haldun University Scholarship
The Ibn Haldun University Scholarship covers 100% of tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 1,200 Turkish Lira for master's students and 1,800 Turkish Lira for PhD students. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0 for master's studies and a master's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.5 for PhD studies. The deadline for application is April 15, 2026, and the official website is [https://www.ihu.edu.tr/](https://www.ihu.edu.tr/).

### Koç University Graduate Scholarship
The Koç University Graduate Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 2,000 Turkish Lira for master's students and 2,500 Turkish Lira for PhD students, as well as tuition fees and accommodation. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0 for master's studies and a master's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.5 for PhD studies. The deadline for application is May 1, 2026, and the official website is [https://www.ku.edu.tr/](https://www.ku.edu.tr/).

### Sabancı University Graduate Scholarship
The Sabancı University Graduate Scholarship covers 100% of tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 1,500 Turkish Lira for master's students and 2,200 Turkish Lira for PhD students. The deadline for application is June 1, 2026, and the official website is [https://www.sabanciuniv.edu/](https://www.sabanciuniv.edu/).

### Yasar University Graduate Scholarship
The Yasar University Graduate Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 1,000 Turkish Lira for master's students and 1,600 Turkish Lira for PhD students, as well as tuition fees and accommodation. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0 for master's studies and a master's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.5 for PhD studies. ## Frequently Asked Questions
What are the benefits of studying in Turkey?
Studying in Turkey provides international students with the opportunity to experience a unique culture, learn a new language, and receive high-quality education at affordable prices. Additionally, Turkey offers a range of scholarship programs for international students, making it an attractive option for those looking to pursue their higher education abroad.

Can I apply for a scholarship if I am already studying in Turkey?
Yes, you can apply for a scholarship if you are already studying in Turkey, but you must meet the eligibility criteria for the scholarship program and apply before the deadline.

What is the language of instruction in Turkish universities?
The language of instruction in Turkish universities is usually Turkish, but some programs are taught in English, especially at the graduate level.

How long does it take to process a scholarship application?
The scholarship application process can take several months to be completed, so it is essential to apply before the deadline and to be patient during the evaluation process.

Can I work part-time while studying in Turkey?
Yes, international students in Turkey can work part-time for up to 24 hours per week, but they must obtain a work permit from the Turkish government.

What are the requirements for a student visa in Turkey?
To apply for a student visa in Turkey, you must have an admission letter from a Turkish university, proof of financial support, and health insurance.

Do I need to take a language proficiency test to study in Turkey?
Yes, most Turkish universities require international students to take a language proficiency test, such as TOEFL or IELTS, to demonstrate their English language skills.

Can I bring my family with me to Turkey?
Yes, international students in Turkey can bring their family members with them, but they must apply for a family residence visa and meet the requirements for family reunification.
