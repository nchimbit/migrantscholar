## Introduction to UK Chevening Scholarship for Migrant Students and Refugees
The UK Chevening Scholarship is a prestigious award that provides opportunities for migrant students and refugees from around the world to pursue Master's degree programs in UK universities. This scholarship is designed to support individuals who have been displaced or have migrated to a new country, and are seeking to further their education and build a better future for themselves. The UK Chevening Scholarship is a fully funded award that covers tuition fees, living expenses, and other costs associated with studying in the UK.

## Who Qualifies for the UK Chevening Scholarship
To be eligible for the UK Chevening Scholarship, applicants must hold a valid visa or immigration status in the UK, including:
* Refugee status
* Humanitarian protection
* Discretionary leave
* Leave outside the rules
* Asylum seeker
* Migrant worker visa
* Student visa
* Tier 2 or 5 visa
