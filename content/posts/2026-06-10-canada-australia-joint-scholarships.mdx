## Introduction to Canada-Australia Joint Scholarships
The Canada-Australia Joint Scholarships for Undergraduate Migrant Students is a prestigious program that offers financial assistance to migrant students who demonstrate exceptional community engagement and academic merit. This scholarship is a great opportunity for migrant students to pursue their undergraduate studies in Canada or Australia. With a focus on community engagement and academic merit, this scholarship aims to support students who have made significant contributions to their communities and have a strong academic record.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Canada-Australia Joint Scholarships, migrant students must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Subclass 500 (Student Visa) in Australia
* Study Permit in Canada
* Subclass 590 (Student Guardian Visa) in Australia
* Temporary Resident Visa (TRV) in Canada
* Bridging Visa E (BVE) in Australia
* Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP) in Canada
* Subclass 485 (Temporary Graduate Visa) in Australia
* Open Work Permit in Canada
