# STEM Aid

This programme is designed for students from developing countries, including Syrian refugees, who are interested in pursuing a Master's degree in a STEM field, which can lead to [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) and [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) in the future.

## Who Qualifies
Syrian refugees who hold a refugee visa or have been granted asylum in Germany are eligible to apply for these STEM scholarships. They must have a Bachelor's degree in a STEM field and meet the language requirements of the programme, which can also lead to [DAAD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/universities/daad). The following visa categories are eligible: 
- Humanitarian visa
- Asylum seeker visa
- Refugee visa
- Subsidiary protection visa
- Residence permit for humanitarian reasons
