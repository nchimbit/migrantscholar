## Introduction to DAAD Fully Funded Scholarship Awards
The German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) offers fully funded scholarship awards to migrant students, including refugees and asylum seekers, for postgraduate studies in German universities. These scholarships provide a unique opportunity for migrant students to pursue their academic goals in Germany, with coverage of tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits. The DAAD fully funded scholarship awards are highly competitive, but they offer a life-changing experience for successful applicants.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the DAAD fully funded scholarship awards, migrant students must hold a valid visa category, including:
* Subsidiary protection visa
* Refugee visa
* Asylum seeker visa
* Temporary residence permit
* Humanitarian visa
* Family reunion visa for refugees or asylum seekers

## Available Scholarships
### DAAD Scholarship for Postgraduate Studies
The DAAD Scholarship for Postgraduate Studies provides a coverage amount of €750 per month, plus tuition fees and health insurance. To be eligible, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree and have at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is October 31st, and the official website URL is https://www.daad.de/en/.

### Friedrich Ebert Foundation Scholarship
The Friedrich Ebert Foundation Scholarship offers a coverage amount of €1,200 per month, plus tuition fees and health insurance. To be eligible, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree and be committed to social democracy. The deadline for application is September 30th, and the official website URL is https://www.fes.de/en/.

### Heinrich Böll Foundation Scholarship
The Heinrich Böll Foundation Scholarship provides a coverage amount of €1,000 per month, plus tuition fees and health insurance. To be eligible, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree and be committed to sustainability and social justice. The deadline for application is September 1st, and the official website URL is https://www.boell.de/en.

### Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung Scholarship
The Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung Scholarship offers a coverage amount of €1,100 per month, plus tuition fees and health insurance. To be eligible, applicants must hold a bachelor's degree and be committed to Christian democratic values. The deadline for application is July 15th, and the official website URL is https://www.kas.de/en/.

### Deutscher Akademischer Austauschdienst (DAAD) Re-Invitation Programme
The DAAD Re-Invitation Programme provides a coverage amount of €2,000 per month, plus travel expenses. To be eligible, applicants must be former DAAD scholarship holders and have completed their studies in Germany. The deadline for application is April 30th, and the official website URL is https://www.daad.de/en/.
