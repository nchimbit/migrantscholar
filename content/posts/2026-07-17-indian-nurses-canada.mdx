The Queen Elizabeth II Diamond Jubilee Scholarship is available to Indian nurses who wish to pursue a master's degree in nursing in Canada, with a funding amount of $10,000. This scholarship is part of the [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada) that aim to attract international talent in the field of healthcare. 

## Who Qualifies
Indian nurses who are interested in applying for the Queen Elizabeth II Diamond Jubilee Scholarship must have a valid visa to enter Canada. The visa categories for Indian nurses include the Temporary Foreign Worker Program, the Permanent Residence Program, and the Express Entry Program. The [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) like the Queen Elizabeth II Diamond Jubilee Scholarship require applicants to have a valid visa and meet the eligibility criteria.

## Scholarships
### Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships
The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships are available to Indian nurses who wish to pursue a PhD in nursing in Canada, with a funding amount of $50,000 per year for three years. The eligibility criteria include being a citizen of India, having a first-class master's degree in nursing, and being enrolled in a PhD program in a Canadian university. The deadline for application is November 1st, and the official URL is https://vanier.gc.ca/en/home-accueil.html.

### Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarship
The Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarship is available to Indian nurses who wish to pursue a PhD in nursing in Canada, with a funding amount of $40,000 per year for three years. The eligibility criteria include being a citizen of India, having a first-class master's degree in nursing, and being enrolled in a PhD program in a Canadian university. The deadline for application is December 1st, and the official URL is https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/en/programs/scholarship.

### CIHR Fellowship Program
The CIHR Fellowship Program is available to Indian nurses who wish to pursue a postdoctoral fellowship in nursing in Canada, with a funding amount of $40,000 per year for two years. The eligibility criteria include being a citizen of India, having a PhD in nursing, and being affiliated with a Canadian university. The deadline for application is October 1st, and the official URL is https://cihr-irsc.gc.ca/e/193.html.

### Canada Graduate Scholarships-Master's Program
The Canada Graduate Scholarships-Master's Program is available to Indian nurses who wish to pursue a master's degree in nursing in Canada, with a funding amount of $17,500 for one year. The eligibility criteria include being a citizen of India, having a first-class bachelor's degree in nursing, and being enrolled in a master's program in a Canadian university. The deadline for application is December 1st, and the official URL is https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PG-CS/CanadaGraduateScholarships-Master.shtml.

### IDRC Research Awards
The IDRC Research Awards are available to Indian nurses who wish to pursue a master's degree in nursing in Canada, with a funding amount of $10,000 for one year. The eligibility criteria include being a citizen of India, having a first-class bachelor's degree in nursing, and being enrolled in a master's program in a Canadian university. The deadline for application is May 1st, and the official URL is https://www.idrc.ca/en/funding/research-awards.
