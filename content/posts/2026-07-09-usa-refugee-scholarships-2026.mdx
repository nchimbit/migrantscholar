## Fully Funded Refugee Scholarships in the USA for 2026
The USA is offering fully funded refugee scholarships to refugee students pursuing undergraduate studies, with the aim of providing opportunities for displaced individuals to access higher education and build a better future for themselves.

## Who Qualifies
Refugee students who hold one of the following visa categories are eligible to apply for these scholarships:
* F-1 visa
* F-2 visa
* J-1 visa
* J-2 visa
* I-94 with a refugee admission stamp
* I-766 Employment Authorization Document (EAD) with a refugee category
* I-551 Permanent Resident Card with a refugee category

## Application Steps

8. Wait for the admission decision and scholarship award notification.
