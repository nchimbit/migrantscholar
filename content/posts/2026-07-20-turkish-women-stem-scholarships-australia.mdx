Turkish women migrants and refugees, including those on a Subclass 500 visa, Subclass 820 visa, or other eligible visa categories, can apply for this scholarship to pursue undergraduate or postgraduate studies in STEM fields at Australian universities.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, Turkish women must hold a valid visa, such as a Subclass 500 visa for student visa holders, Subclass 820 visa for partner visa holders, or other eligible visa categories, including [scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees). They must also meet the specific eligibility criteria for each scholarship, including academic requirements, language proficiency, and field of study.
