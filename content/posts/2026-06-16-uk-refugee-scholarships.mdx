## Introduction to Fully Funded Scholarships for Refugee Students in the UK
If you are a refugee student looking to pursue higher education in the UK, there are several fully funded scholarships available to support your academic journey. These scholarships are designed to help refugee students overcome financial barriers and achieve their academic goals. The UK offers a range of scholarships to refugee students, including those with limited leave to remain, discretionary leave, or humanitarian protection.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, refugee students must meet specific visa categories, including:
* Limited leave to remain
* Discretionary leave
* Humanitarian protection
* Leave outside the rules
* Refugee status
* Asylum seeker
* Unaccompanied asylum-seeking children

## Available Scholarships
### University of Oxford Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships
The University of Oxford Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships offer £26,700 to £30,000 per year to graduate students. To be eligible, students must be from a developing country, have a bachelor's degree, and be applying to a graduate program at the University of Oxford. The deadline for application is January 2027, and the official website is https://www.ox.ac.uk/admissions/graduate/fees-and-funding/graduate-scholarships/university-of-oxford-scholarships/weidenfeld-hoffmann.

### University of West London Scholarship
The University of West London Scholarship offers £5,000 to £15,000 per year to undergraduate and graduate students. To be eligible, students must be from a developing country, have a high school diploma or bachelor's degree, and be applying to an undergraduate or graduate program at the University of West London. The deadline for application is August 2026, and the official website is https://www.uwl.ac.uk/international/fees-and-scholarships/scholarships.

### Refugee Support Scholarship
The Refugee Support Scholarship offers £2,000 to £5,000 per year to undergraduate and graduate students. To be eligible, students must be a refugee or have limited leave to remain, have a high school diploma or bachelor's degree, and be applying to an undergraduate or graduate program at a UK university. The deadline for application is June 2027, and the official website is https://www.universitiesuk.ac.uk/policy-and-analysis/research-policy/refugee-support.

### Commonwealth Shared Scholarship Scheme
The Commonwealth Shared Scholarship Scheme offers £10,000 to £20,000 per year to graduate students. To be eligible, students must be from a Commonwealth country, have a bachelor's degree, and be applying to a graduate program at a UK university. The deadline for application is January 2027, and the official website is https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/scholarships/commonwealth-shared-scholarships.

### Chevening Scholarship
The Chevening Scholarship offers £12,000 to £18,000 per year to graduate students. To be eligible, students must be from a developing country, have a bachelor's degree, and be applying to a graduate program at a UK university. The deadline for application is November 2026, and the official website is https://www.chevening.org/scholarships.
