## Canada's Diverse and Inclusive Scholarship Program for Refugee Students
The Canada scholarship program for refugee students offers a range of opportunities for undergraduate and graduate studies, available to refugee students who hold a Refugee Claimant Document, a Convention Refugee status, or are sponsored as a Refugee under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, refugee students must fall under one of the following visa categories:
* Refugee Claimant Document
* Convention Refugee status
* Sponsored as a Refugee under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act
* Protected Person status
* Asylum Claimant
