Migrant students who hold certain visa categories are eligible to apply for the Migrant Student Scholarship. The following visa categories qualify:
* F-1 visa
* J-1 visa
* K-1 visa
* DACA
* Asylum status
* Refugee status
* TPS
* SIV

## Available Scholarships
Several organizations offer scholarships for migrant students in the USA. Here are five real scholarships:
### TheDream.US Scholarship
TheDream.US Scholarship offers $20,000 per year for undergraduate studies, eligibility includes migrant students with DACA or TPS status, deadline is February 27, 2026, and the official URL is https://thedream.us/scholarships/
### Open Society Foundations Scholarship
Open Society Foundations Scholarship provides $10,000 per year for undergraduate studies, eligibility includes migrant students with asylum or refugee status, deadline is January 30, 2026, and the official URL is https://www.opensocietyfoundations.org/grants
### Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans
Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans offers $90,000 for two years of undergraduate or graduate studies, eligibility includes migrant students who are green card holders or naturalized citizens, deadline is November 1, 2026, and the official URL is https://www.pdsoros.org/
### HIAS Scholarship
HIAS Scholarship provides $2,500 per year for undergraduate studies, eligibility includes migrant students with asylum or refugee status, deadline is March 15, 2026, and the official URL is https://www.hias.org/education
### IIE Scholarship
IIE Scholarship offers $10,000 per year for undergraduate studies, eligibility includes migrant students with F-1 or J-1 visa, deadline is April 15, 2026, and the official URL is https://www.iie.org/Programs
