## Canadian Government Awards for Migrant Students
The Canadian Government Awards are available to migrant students pursuing Master's degrees in the USA, providing them with an opportunity to fund their education and achieve their academic goals.

To be eligible for the Canadian Government Awards, migrant students must hold one of the following visa categories:
* F-1 visa
* J-1 visa
* H-1B visa
* L-1 visa
* O-1 visa
* TN visa
* TD visa
* DACA

## Available Scholarships
The Canadian Government offers several scholarships to support migrant students in their pursuit of higher education. Here are five available scholarships:
### Fulbright Scholarship
The Fulbright Scholarship provides coverage of up to $100,000 per year, and is eligible to migrant students who have a bachelor's degree and a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is October 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.iie.org/Programs/Fulbright-Program.
### Trudeau Scholarship
The Trudeau Scholarship provides coverage of up to $40,000 per year, and is eligible to migrant students who are pursuing a Master's degree in a field related to public policy or international relations. The deadline for application is February 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/en/programs/trudeau-scholarship.
### Vanier Scholarship
The Vanier Scholarship provides coverage of up to $50,000 per year, and is eligible to migrant students who have a master's degree and are pursuing a PhD in a field related to natural sciences or engineering. The deadline for application is November 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://vanier.gc.ca/en/home-accueil.html.
### Banting Scholarship
The Banting Scholarship provides coverage of up to $70,000 per year, and is eligible to migrant students who have a master's degree and are pursuing a PhD in a field related to health sciences or natural sciences. The deadline for application is October 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://banting.fellowships-bourses.gc.ca/en/home-accueil.html.
### Commonwealth Scholarship
The Commonwealth Scholarship provides coverage of up to $30,000 per year, and is eligible to migrant students who are pursuing a Master's degree in a field related to social sciences or humanities. The deadline for application is March 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.commonwealthscholarship.org.uk.
