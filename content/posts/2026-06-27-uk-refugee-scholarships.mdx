## Introduction to Fully Funded Scholarships for Refugee Students in the UK
Fully funded scholarships for refugee students in the UK are designed to provide educational opportunities to those who have been displaced due to conflict, persecution, or natural disasters. If you are a refugee or asylum seeker looking to pursue higher education in the UK, there are several scholarships available that can cover your tuition fees, living costs, and other expenses. These scholarships are offered by various organizations, universities, and government bodies, and can be a vital source of support for refugee students seeking to rebuild their lives.

* Refugee visa

* Section 4(1) of the Immigration and Asylum Act 1999 visa
