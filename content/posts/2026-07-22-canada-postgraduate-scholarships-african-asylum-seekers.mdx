The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship exists and qualifies asylum seekers from Africa who are pursuing a postgraduate degree in Canada, with funding of up to $50,000 per year. As an asylum seeker from Africa, you can apply for various postgraduate scholarships in Canada, including [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) and [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd). 

## Who Qualifies
Asylum seekers from Africa who hold a valid visa under the following categories can apply for postgraduate scholarships in Canada: 
- Protected persons
- Convention refugees
- Permanent residents who were previously asylum seekers
- Temporary residents who are in the process of seeking asylum
