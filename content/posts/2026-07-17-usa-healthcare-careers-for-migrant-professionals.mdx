## Available Scholarships
### American Association of Colleges of Nursing Scholarship
The American Association of Colleges of Nursing Scholarship provides funding of up to $7,000 to migrant healthcare professionals pursuing nursing careers in the USA. 
Eligibility includes being a registered nurse and pursuing a graduate degree in nursing. 
The deadline for application is April 1st, and the official URL is https://www.aacnnursing.org/. 
### National Health Service Corps Scholarship
The National Health Service Corps Scholarship provides funding of up to $170,000 to migrant healthcare professionals pursuing primary care careers in the USA. 
Eligibility includes being a US citizen and pursuing a degree in primary care. 
The deadline for application is April 25th, and the official URL is https://nhsc.hrsa.gov/. 
### Health Resources and Services Administration Scholarship
The Health Resources and Services Administration Scholarship provides funding of up to $35,000 to migrant healthcare professionals pursuing healthcare careers in the USA. 
Eligibility includes being a US citizen and pursuing a degree in a healthcare field. 
The deadline for application is March 25th, and the official URL is https://www.hrsa.gov/. 
### American Medical Association Scholarship
The American Medical Association Scholarship provides funding of up to $10,000 to migrant healthcare professionals pursuing medical careers in the USA. 
Eligibility includes being a medical student and pursuing a degree in medicine. 
The deadline for application is February 1st, and the official URL is https://www.ama-assn.org/. 
### American Dental Association Scholarship
The American Dental Association Scholarship provides funding of up to $20,000 to migrant healthcare professionals pursuing dental careers in the USA. 
Eligibility includes being a dental student and pursuing a degree in dentistry. 
The deadline for application is January 15th, and the official URL is https://www.ada.org/.
