## Available Scholarships
The UK Chevening Scholarships offer a range of programs for migrant students to pursue their Master's degree. Here are five real scholarships available:
### Chevening Scholarship
The Chevening Scholarship is a fully funded program that covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs up to £18,000. To be eligible, migrant students must have a Bachelor's degree, two years of work experience, and meet the English language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is November 1, 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://www.chevening.org/.
### Commonwealth Scholarship
The Commonwealth Scholarship is a fully funded program that covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs up to £15,000. To be eligible, migrant students must be a citizen of a Commonwealth country, have a Bachelor's degree, and meet the English language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is December 1, 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/.
### Gates Cambridge Scholarship
The Gates Cambridge Scholarship is a fully funded program that covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs up to £17,000. To be eligible, migrant students must have a Bachelor's degree, meet the English language proficiency requirements, and be accepted into a Master's program at the University of Cambridge. The deadline for application is October 15, 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://www.gatescambridge.org/.
### Rhodes Scholarship
The Rhodes Scholarship is a fully funded program that covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs up to £14,000. To be eligible, migrant students must have a Bachelor's degree, meet the English language proficiency requirements, and be under the age of 24. The deadline for application is July 1, 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://www.rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk/.
### Marshall Scholarship
The Marshall Scholarship is a fully funded program that covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs up to £16,000. To be eligible, migrant students must have a Bachelor's degree, meet the English language proficiency requirements, and be a citizen of the United States. The deadline for application is October 1, 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://www.marshallscholarship.org/.
