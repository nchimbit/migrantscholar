The [AAUW Emergency Fund for Women and Girls](https://www.aauw.org/resources/programs/emergency-fund/) exists for women pursuing a certificate or degree in the USA, with a funding amount of up to $5,000, open to female migrants who are US citizens, permanent residents, or hold certain visa categories such as F-1, J-1, or those with asylum or refugee status.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for emergency funding as a female migrant in the USA, you must hold a valid visa such as F-1, J-1, H-1B, L-1, O-1, or have a pending asylum or refugee application. You should also be pursuing a degree or certificate in a healthcare-related field. If you're interested in [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) for your education, we recommend exploring those options as well.

## Scholarships
### AAUW Emergency Fund for Women and Girls
* Amount: up to $5,000
* Eligibility: Women pursuing a certificate or degree in the USA, with a valid visa or asylum/refugee status
* Deadline: Ongoing
* URL: https://www.aauw.org/resources/programs/emergency-fund/
### PEO International Peace Scholarship
* Amount: up to $10,000
* Eligibility: Women from other countries pursuing a degree in the USA or Canada, including [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada)
* Deadline: December 15
* URL: https://www.peointernational.org/peace-scholarship
### National Hispanic Health Foundation Scholarship
* Amount: up to $5,000
* Eligibility: Hispanic students pursuing a degree in healthcare, including [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd)
* Deadline: October 25
* URL: https://www.nhmafoundation.org/scholarship-program/
### Zonta International Jane M. Klausman Women in Business Scholarship
* Amount: up to $5,000
* Eligibility: Women pursuing a degree in business, including healthcare management
* Deadline: July 1
* URL: https://www.zonta.org/Web/Programs/Education/WomenInBusiness/Scholarship.aspx
### [Johnson & Johnson](https://nursing.jnj.com/) Scholarship
* Amount: up to $10,000
* Eligibility: Nursing students, including those pursuing [scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees)
