## Fully Funded Scholarship Opportunities for Refugee Students in the UK
Refugee students in the UK with a valid visa under specific categories are eligible for fully funded scholarship opportunities, including those with Humanitarian Protection, Refugee Status, and Limited Leave to Remain.

### Who Qualifies
The following refugees are eligible for these scholarships:
* Humanitarian Protection visa holders
* Refugee Status visa holders
* Limited Leave to Remain visa holders
* Discretionary Leave visa holders
* UASC Leave visa holders
* Section 67 of the Immigration Act 2016 visa holders
* Section 75 of the Immigration Act 2014 visa holders

## Scholarship Opportunities
The following are five fully funded scholarships available to refugee students in the UK:
### The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarship
The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarship covers full tuition fees and a living stipend of £15,609. It is eligible for postgraduate students from developing countries, including refugees. The deadline for application is December 2026, and the official website is https://www.ox.ac.uk/admissions/graduate/fees-and-funding/graduate-scholarships/university-of-oxford-scholarships/weidenfeld-hoffmann-scholarship-and- leadership-programme.
### The University of Sheffield Scholarship
The University of Sheffield Scholarship covers full tuition fees and a living stipend of £9,500. It is eligible for undergraduate and postgraduate students who are asylum seekers or have refugee status. The deadline for application is June 2026, and the official website is https://www.sheffield.ac.uk/international/scholarships/ukvi-scholarships.
### The Sanctuary Scholarship
The Sanctuary Scholarship covers full tuition fees and a living stipend of £11,000. It is eligible for undergraduate and postgraduate students who are asylum seekers or have refugee status. The deadline for application is August 2026, and the official website is https://www.nottingham.ac.uk/studywithus/international-scholarships/sanctuary-scholarship.aspx.
### The Article 26 Scholarship
The Article 26 Scholarship covers full tuition fees and a living stipend of £8,500. It is eligible for undergraduate students who are asylum seekers or have refugee status. The deadline for application is October 2026, and the official website is https://www.universityofbolton.ac.uk/study/undergraduate-fees-and-funding/article-26-scholarship/.
### The Liverpool International College Scholarship
The Liverpool International College Scholarship covers full tuition fees and a living stipend of £10,000. It is eligible for undergraduate and postgraduate students who are asylum seekers or have refugee status. The deadline for application is May 2026, and the official website is https://www.liv.ac.uk/study/international/scholarships-and-financial-support/scholarships/.
