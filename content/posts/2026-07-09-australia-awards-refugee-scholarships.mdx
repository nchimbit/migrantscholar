## Fully Funded Australia Awards Scholarships for Refugee Students
Refugee students who hold a valid visa in one of the specified categories can apply for the Fully Funded Australia Awards Scholarships to pursue postgraduate studies in Australia.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Fully Funded Australia Awards Scholarships, applicants must hold a valid Australian visa in one of the following categories:
* Permanent Humanitarian Visa (subclass 201, 203, 204, or 866)
* Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790)
* Refugee visa (subclass 732 or 733)
* Asylum Seeker Visa

## Available Scholarships
The following are some of the Fully Funded Australia Awards Scholarships available to refugee students:
### Australia Awards Scholarship
The Australia Awards Scholarship covers the full tuition fee, living costs, and other expenses up to $30,000 per year, and is eligible for refugee students pursuing a master's or PhD degree at an Australian university. The deadline for application is April 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.australiaawards.gov.au](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au).
### Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship
The Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship covers the full tuition fee, living costs, and other expenses up to $28,597 per year, and is eligible for refugee students pursuing a research master's or PhD degree at an Australian university. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.education.gov.au](https://www.education.gov.au).
### University of Melbourne Scholarship for Refugee Students
The University of Melbourne Scholarship for Refugee Students covers the full tuition fee, living costs, and other expenses up to $25,000 per year, and is eligible for refugee students pursuing a master's or PhD degree at the University of Melbourne. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.unimelb.edu.au](https://www.unimelb.edu.au).
### Monash University Refugee Scholarship
The Monash University Refugee Scholarship covers the full tuition fee, living costs, and other expenses up to $20,000 per year, and is eligible for refugee students pursuing a master's or PhD degree at Monash University. The deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.monash.edu](https://www.monash.edu).
### Deakin University Scholarship for Refugee Students
The Deakin University Scholarship for Refugee Students covers the full tuition fee, living costs, and other expenses up to $15,000 per year, and is eligible for refugee students pursuing a master's or PhD degree at Deakin University. The deadline for application is May 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.deakin.edu.au](https://www.deakin.edu.au).

## Application Steps
To apply for the Fully Funded Australia Awards Scholarships, follow these steps:
1. Choose the scholarship and university you wish to apply for.
2. Check the eligibility criteria and ensure you meet the requirements.
3. Prepare the required documents, including your academic transcripts, visa documents, and language proficiency test results.
4. Submit your application through the university's online application portal.
5. Wait for the application to be processed and a decision to be made.
6. If your application is successful, you will be required to accept the scholarship offer and enroll in the university.
7. Attend a pre-departure briefing and complete any required paperwork.
8. Travel to Australia and commence your studies.
