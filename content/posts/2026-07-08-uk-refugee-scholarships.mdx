## Fully Funded Scholarships in the UK for Refugee Students
Refugee students with specific visa categories can apply for fully funded scholarships in the UK, covering full tuition fees and living costs, through various programs such as the Chevening Scholarship, Commonwealth Scholarship, and university-funded scholarships.

### Who Qualifies
The following refugee students qualify for fully funded scholarships in the UK:
* Persons with humanitarian protection status
* Persons with limited leave to remain under the Refugee Family Reunion or Refugee Leave to Remain policies
* Persons with discretionary leave to remain
* Persons with leave outside the rules
* Persons with Section 67 leave
* Stateless persons

## Application Steps
To apply for these scholarships, follow these steps:
1. Check your eligibility for the scholarship and visa requirements.
2. Choose the scholarship you want to apply for and read the application guidelines carefully.
3. Prepare all required documents, including academic certificates and English language proficiency test results.

6. Apply for a student visa if your application is successful.
7. Book your flights and accommodation in the UK.
