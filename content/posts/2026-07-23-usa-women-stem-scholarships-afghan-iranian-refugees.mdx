The [Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans](https://www.pdsoros.org/) offers up to $90,000 to Afghan and Iranian refugee women pursuing graduate degrees in STEM fields at top universities in the USA.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, applicants must be Afghan or Iranian refugees with a valid visa category, such as F-1, J-1, or asylum status. They must also demonstrate a strong academic record, leadership skills, and a commitment to their field of study. [Scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) are highly competitive, so applicants must ensure they meet all the eligibility criteria.

## Available Scholarships
### The Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans
* Amount: up to $90,000
* Eligibility: Afghan and Iranian refugee women pursuing graduate degrees in STEM fields
* Deadline: November 1
* Official URL: https://www.pdsoros.org/
### The National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship Program
* Amount: $34,000 per year for three years
* Eligibility: Afghan and Iranian refugee women pursuing graduate degrees in STEM fields
* Deadline: October 24
* Official URL: https://www.nsfgrfp.org/
### The American Association of University Women (AAUW) International Fellowships
* Amount: $18,000 to $30,000
* Eligibility: Afghan and Iranian refugee women pursuing graduate degrees in STEM fields
* Deadline: November 15
* Official URL: https://www.aauw.org/
### The Schlumberger Foundation Faculty for the Future Fellowships
* Amount: up to $50,000 per year for two years
* Eligibility: Afghan and Iranian refugee women pursuing PhDs in STEM fields
* Deadline: November 5
* Official URL: https://www.slb.com/about/foundation/faculty-for-the-future
### The Google Anita Borg Memorial Scholarship
* Amount: $10,000 to $20,000
* Eligibility: Afghan and Iranian refugee women pursuing undergraduate or graduate degrees in STEM fields
* Deadline: December 6
* Official URL: https://www.google.com/anitaborg/

1. Review the eligibility criteria for each scholarship to ensure you qualify.
2. Choose the scholarships that best fit your academic and career goals.
3. Prepare your application materials, including transcripts, letters of recommendation, and personal statements.
4. Submit your applications on time, making sure to meet all deadlines.
