## Introduction to International Student Scholarships in Canada for Students from Turkey
If you are an international student from Turkey looking to pursue higher education in Canada, you may be eligible for various scholarships that can help fund your studies. The Canadian government and several universities offer scholarships to students from Turkey, covering tuition fees, living expenses, and other study-related costs. These scholarships are designed to attract top talent from around the world, including Turkey, and provide opportunities for students to gain valuable educational experiences in Canada.

## Who Qualifies for International Student Scholarships in Canada
To be eligible for international student scholarships in Canada, students from Turkey must hold a valid visa, such as:
* Study Permit (SP)
* Temporary Resident Visa (TRV)
* Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA)
* Permanent Resident Visa (PRV)
* Diplomatic Visa
* Official Visa
* Temporary Resident Permit (TRP)

## Application Steps for International Student Scholarships in Canada
1. Research and choose the scholarship you want to apply for.
2. Review the eligibility criteria and ensure you meet the requirements.
3. Prepare all required documents, including transcripts, letters of recommendation, and language proficiency test scores.
4. Submit your application online or by mail, depending on the scholarship requirements.
5. Pay the application fee, if applicable.
6. Complete any additional requirements, such as essays or interviews.
7. Wait for the selection committee to review your application.
8. Receive notification of the scholarship decision and follow up on any additional requirements.
