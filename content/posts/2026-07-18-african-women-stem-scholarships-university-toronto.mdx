# STEM Scholar
The Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program at the University of Toronto, Canada qualifies African women for up to $50,000 per year in funding for STEM fields.
The University of Toronto is one of the top universities in Canada, offering [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada) to international students, including African women. Women from Africa can apply for [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) to pursue their dreams in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields.

## Who Qualifies
African women who are refugees, immigrants, or international students can apply for these scholarships. The eligible visa categories include study permits, temporary resident visas, and permanent resident visas. To be eligible, applicants must meet the admission requirements of the University of Toronto and demonstrate financial need.

## Scholarships
### Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program
* Amount: up to $50,000 per year
* Eligibility: African women pursuing undergraduate or graduate studies in STEM fields
* Deadline: varies
* Official URL: https://www(mastercardfdn.org/scholars-program/)
### Ontario Graduate Scholarship
* Amount: $15,000 per year
* Eligibility: International students, including African women, pursuing graduate studies in STEM fields
* Deadline: varies
* Official URL: https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-graduate-scholarship
### NSERC Postgraduate Scholarships
* Amount: $17,500 per year
* Eligibility: Canadian citizens, permanent residents, and international students, including African women, pursuing graduate studies in STEM fields
* Deadline: October 15
* Official URL: https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PG-CS/index_eng.asp
### University of Toronto Scholarship
* Amount: up to $10,000 per year
* Eligibility: International students, including African women, pursuing undergraduate or graduate studies in STEM fields
* Deadline: varies
* Official URL: https://www.utsc.utoronto.ca/future-students/scholarships
### Canada Graduate Scholarships
* Amount: $17,500 per year
* Eligibility: Canadian citizens, permanent residents, and international students, including African women, pursuing graduate studies in STEM fields
* Deadline: December 1
* Official URL: https://www.cihr-irsc.gc.ca/e/49307.html

## Application Steps
1. Research the scholarships and their eligibility criteria
2. Review the admission requirements of the University of Toronto
3. Submit an application to the University of Toronto
4. Apply for the scholarships through the official websites
5. Prepare and submit the required documents, including transcripts and letters of recommendation
6. Write a personal statement and CV
7. Submit a language proficiency test score, if required
