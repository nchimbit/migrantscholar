## Fully Funded German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) Scholarships for Migrant Students in Germany
The Fully Funded German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) Scholarships are available to migrant students, including refugees and asylum seekers, who wish to pursue higher education in Germany.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the DAAD scholarships, migrant students must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* Subsidiary protection
* Refugee status
* Asylum seeker
* Deportation ban
* Pending asylum application
* Temporary residence permit
* Humanitarian residence permit

## Available Scholarships
Migrant students can apply for the following fully funded DAAD scholarships:
### DAAD Scholarship for Master's Studies
The DAAD Scholarship for Master's Studies offers a monthly stipend of 861 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel costs. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree and meet the language requirements of the host university. The deadline for application is November 2026, and the official website can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### DAAD Scholarship for PhD Studies
The DAAD Scholarship for PhD Studies offers a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel costs. To be eligible, applicants must have a master's degree and meet the language requirements of the host university. The deadline for application is January 2027, and the official website can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### DAAD Scholarship for Research Grants
The DAAD Scholarship for Research Grants offers a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel costs. To be eligible, applicants must have a master's degree and meet the language requirements of the host university. The deadline for application is March 2027, and the official website can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### DAAD Scholarship for Language Courses
The DAAD Scholarship for Language Courses offers a monthly stipend of 750 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel costs. To be eligible, applicants must have a high school diploma and meet the language requirements of the host university. The deadline for application is May 2027, and the official website can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### DAAD Scholarship for Internships
The DAAD Scholarship for Internships offers a monthly stipend of 650 euros, plus additional benefits such as health insurance and travel costs. To be eligible, applicants must have a high school diploma and meet the language requirements of the host university. The deadline for application is July 2027, and the official website can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.

## Application Process
To apply for the DAAD scholarships, migrant students must follow these steps:
1. Choose the relevant scholarship and check the eligibility criteria.
2. Prepare the required documents, including transcripts, language certificates, and passport copies.
3. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline.
4. Upload the required documents to the application portal.
5. Wait for the confirmation email and follow the instructions.
6. Take the language proficiency test, if required.
7. Prepare for the interview, if required.
8. Receive the scholarship award and start the academic program.

## Frequently Asked Questions
What are the language requirements for the DAAD scholarships?
The language requirements for the DAAD scholarships vary depending on the host university and the academic program. Generally, migrant students must have a good command of German or English, depending on the program. Some programs may require additional language tests, such as IELTS or TOEFL.
How can I apply for the DAAD scholarships if I don't have a valid visa?
Migrant students can apply for the DAAD scholarships even if they don't have a valid visa. However, they must meet the eligibility criteria and provide all the required documents, including a pending asylum application or a deportation ban.
What are the benefits of the DAAD scholarships?
The DAAD scholarships offer a range of benefits, including a monthly stipend, health insurance, and travel costs. Migrant students can also access additional benefits, such as language courses and research grants.
Can I apply for multiple DAAD scholarships at the same time?
No, migrant students can only apply for one DAAD scholarship at a time. However, they can apply for different scholarships in subsequent years.
How long does it take to receive the scholarship award?
The scholarship award is usually announced within a few weeks after the application deadline. Migrant students will receive a confirmation email with the scholarship details and instructions on how to proceed.
What are the requirements for the language proficiency test?
The language proficiency test requirements vary depending on the host university and the academic program. Generally, migrant students must have a good command of German or English, depending on the program.
Can I apply for the DAAD scholarships if I have a temporary residence permit?
Yes, migrant students with a temporary residence permit can apply for the DAAD scholarships. However, they must meet the eligibility criteria and provide all the required documents.
How can I contact the DAAD for more information?
Migrant students can contact the DAAD through their official website or through the DAAD office in their home country.
