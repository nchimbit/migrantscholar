Female asylum seekers who have been granted refugee status or are seeking asylum in the UK and are facing financial difficulties can qualify for this scholarship. ## Who Qualifies
To qualify for emergency funding as a female asylum seeker in the UK, you must hold a valid visa under one of the following categories: Refugee, Humanitarian Protection, Discretionary Leave, or a section 67 or 72 of the Immigration Act 2016 leave. These categories are specifically designed to provide protection and support to individuals fleeing persecution, war, or natural disasters. ## Available Scholarships
The following scholarships are available to female asylum seekers in the UK:

### The Helen Bamber Foundation Asylum Seeker Scholarship
* Amount: up to £4,000
* Eligibility: Female asylum seekers who have been granted refugee status or are seeking asylum in the UK
* Deadline: Ongoing
* Official URL: https://www.helenbamber.org/

### The Refugee Council Scholarship
* Amount: up to £2,000
* Eligibility: Asylum seekers and refugees who are pursuing higher education in the UK
* Deadline: 31st August 2026
* Official URL: https://www.refugeecouncil.org.uk/

### The Evelyn Oldfield Unit Scholarship
* Amount: up to £1,500
* Eligibility: Migrant and refugee women who are pursuing training or education in the UK
* Deadline: 30th June 2026
* Official URL: https://www.evelynoldfield.co.uk/

### The Asha Scholarship
* Amount: up to £3,000
* Eligibility: Female asylum seekers and refugees who are pursuing higher education in the UK
* Deadline: 28th February 2026
* Official URL: https://www.ashacharity.org/

### The Women for Refugee Women Scholarship
* Amount: up to £2,500
* Eligibility: Female asylum seekers and refugees who are pursuing higher education in the UK
