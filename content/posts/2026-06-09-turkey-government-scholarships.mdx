Yes, the Turkish government offers scholarships to asylum seekers, providing them with the opportunity to pursue their higher education goals in Turkey. To be eligible, asylum seekers must meet certain criteria, including being registered with the Turkish Directorate General of Migration Management and having a valid residence permit. Who Qualifies: Asylum seekers who have been granted refugee status or are under temporary protection in Turkey can apply for these scholarships. Available Scholarships: 1. Turkey Scholarships for Asylum Seekers: This scholarship covers tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 800 TL. Deadline: February 20, 2026. 2. Istanbul University Scholarship for Asylum Seekers: This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a monthly stipend of 1,000 TL. Deadline: March 15, 2026. 3. Ankara University Scholarship for Asylum Seekers: This scholarship covers tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 900 TL. Deadline: April 10, 2026. 4. Turkish Language Scholarship for Asylum Seekers: This scholarship covers Turkish language courses and provides a monthly stipend of 600 TL. Deadline: May 1, 2026. 5. Graduate Scholarship Program for Asylum Seekers: This scholarship covers tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 1,200 TL. Deadline: June 1, 2026. Step-by-Step Application Process: 1. Check the eligibility criteria and deadlines for each scholarship. 2. Prepare the required documents, including a valid passport, residence permit, and academic transcripts. 3. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline. 4. Wait for the evaluation process, which may take several weeks. Documents Checklist: 1. Valid passport 2. Residence permit 3. Academic transcripts 4. Diploma or certificate 5. Language proficiency certificate (if required) 6. Proof of registration with the Turkish Directorate General of Migration Management. Frequently Asked Questions: 1. Q: Can I apply for multiple scholarships at the same time? A: Yes, you can apply for multiple scholarships, but you will only be awarded one. 2. Q: Do I need to have a language proficiency certificate to apply? A: It depends on the scholarship and the program you are applying for. 3. Q: Can I apply for a scholarship if I am still in the process of getting my residence permit? A: No, you need to have a valid residence permit to apply. 4. Q: How long does the evaluation process take? A: The evaluation process can take several weeks to several months. 5. Q: Can I apply for a scholarship if I have already started my studies in Turkey? A: It depends on the scholarship, but most scholarships are available for new students only. 6. Q: Do I need to have a certain GPA to apply for a scholarship? A: It depends on the scholarship, but most scholarships require a minimum GPA of 2.5 or 3.0.
