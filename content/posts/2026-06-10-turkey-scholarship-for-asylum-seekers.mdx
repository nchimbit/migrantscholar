## Introduction to Turkey Government Scholarship for Asylum Seekers in STEM Fields
The Turkey Government Scholarship for asylum seekers in STEM fields is a prestigious award that provides financial support to master's and PhD students who are seeking asylum in Turkey. This scholarship is specifically designed for asylum seekers who are pursuing studies in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields. As of 2026, the Turkish government offers various scholarships to asylum seekers, with the aim of promoting education and research in STEM fields.

## Who Qualifies for the Scholarship
To be eligible for the Turkey Government Scholarship, asylum seekers must belong to one of the following visa categories:
*Conditional Refugee
*Family Residence Permit for Refugees
*Humanitarian Residence Permit
*Refugee
*Secondary Protection
*Subsidiary Protection
*Temporary Protection

## Available Scholarships for Asylum Seekers in STEM Fields
### 1. Türkiye Scholarships for Asylum Seekers
The Türkiye Scholarships for asylum seekers provides a coverage amount of 1,500 TL per month for master's students and 2,000 TL per month for PhD students. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and be under the age of 30 for master's and 35 for PhD. The deadline for application is February 20, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/.

The Research Scholarship for asylum seekers in STEM fields provides a coverage amount of 2,500 TL per month for PhD students. The Higher Education Scholarship for asylum seekers provides a coverage amount of 1,000 TL per month for undergraduate students and 1,500 TL per month for master's students. The Scientific and Technological Research Scholarship for asylum seekers provides a coverage amount of 2,500 TL per month for PhD students. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and be under the age of 40. The deadline for application is May 15, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.bilkent.edu.tr/.
