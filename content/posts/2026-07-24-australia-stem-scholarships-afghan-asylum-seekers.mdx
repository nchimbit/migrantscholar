# STEM Scholarships
The Australia Awards Scholarship, which provides up to AUD 118,500 per year, is available to Afghan asylum seekers in Australia who are pursuing higher education in STEM fields. 

## Who Qualifies
Afghan asylum seekers who hold a valid Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785), Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790), or Permanent Protection Visa (subclass 866) are eligible to apply for STEM scholarships in Australia. They must also meet the eligibility requirements of the specific scholarship program, which may include language proficiency, academic qualifications, and field of study.
