The Turkish Government Scholarship exists and qualifies Pakistani migrants in STEM fields with funding amount of up to $750 per month to study at top universities in Turkey. Pakistani migrants who are holders of a residence permit, work permit, or are asylum seekers can apply for this scholarship to pursue their higher education in Turkey. This is a [fully funded scholarship](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) that covers tuition fees, accommodation, and living expenses.

## Who Qualifies
The Turkish Government Scholarship is open to Pakistani migrants who are holders of a residence permit, work permit, or are asylum seekers. They must have a strong academic background and meet the eligibility criteria set by the Turkish Government. The visa categories that are eligible for this scholarship include work visa, student visa, and residency visa. Pakistani migrants who are [refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) can also apply for this scholarship.
