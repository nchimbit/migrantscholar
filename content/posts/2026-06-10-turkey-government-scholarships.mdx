## Introduction to Turkey's Government Scholarships

The scholarships offered by the Turkish government provide a chance for students to study in a diverse and vibrant environment, with access to top-notch universities and research facilities. By providing these scholarships, the Turkish government aims to foster global understanding and cooperation, while also promoting its own cultural heritage and academic institutions.

## Available Scholarships
### Turkish Government Scholarship
The Turkish Government Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 800 TL for undergraduate students, 950 TL for master's students, and 1,100 TL for PhD students, as well as tuition fees, accommodation, and health insurance. To be eligible, applicants must be under the age of 25 for undergraduate studies, under 30 for master's studies, and under 35 for PhD studies. The deadline for applications is typically in February, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/
### İbrahim Bodur Scholarship
The İbrahim Bodur Scholarship offers a monthly stipend of 500 TL for undergraduate students, 700 TL for master's students, and 900 TL for PhD students, as well as tuition fees and accommodation. Eligible applicants must be enrolled in a Turkish university and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for applications is typically in October, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.boduvakfi.org.tr/
### Yunus Emre Scholarship
The Yunus Emre Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 600 TL for undergraduate students, 800 TL for master's students, and 1,000 TL for PhD students, as well as tuition fees, accommodation, and health insurance. To be eligible, applicants must be studying Turkish language and literature, and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for applications is typically in April, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.yee.org.tr/
### Türkiye Scholarships for Refugees
The Türkiye Scholarships for Refugees offers a monthly stipend of 800 TL for undergraduate students, 950 TL for master's students, and 1,100 TL for PhD students, as well as tuition fees, accommodation, and health insurance. Eligible applicants must be refugees or asylum seekers, and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for applications is typically in June, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/
### Research Scholarship by TÜBİTAK
The Research Scholarship by TÜBİTAK provides a monthly stipend of 1,500 TL for PhD students, as well as tuition fees and research funding. To be eligible, applicants must be enrolled in a PhD program in a Turkish university, and have a minimum GPA of 3.5. The deadline for applications is typically in December, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.tubitak.gov.tr/
