## Introduction to Turkey Government Scholarships for Refugee Students in Australia
As a refugee student in Australia, you may be wondering if you are eligible for Turkey Government Scholarships. The answer is yes, refugee students in Australia can apply for these scholarships, which cover undergraduate, master's, and PhD programs. The Turkish government offers these scholarships to promote international student mobility and to support students who are unable to afford higher education in their home countries.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for Turkey Government Scholarships, you must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Permanent Humanitarian Visa (subclass 200-217)
* Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790)
* Temporary Humanitarian Concern Visa (subclass 786)
* Protection Visa (subclass 866)
* Refugee Visa (subclass 800)
