The Commonwealth Scholarship, which qualifies Indian students with a minimum of 60% marks in their undergraduate degree, offers £1,330 per month stipend and return airfare to fund their postgraduate studies in the UK.

## Who Qualifies
To study in London, Indian students must apply for a Tier 4 Visa under the following categories: Tier 4 (General) student visa, Tier 4 (Child) student visa, or Tier 4 (General) student visa for students sponsored by a government or international scholarship agency. ## Available Scholarships
### Commonwealth Scholarship
* Amount: £1,330 per month stipend and return airfare
* Eligibility: Indian students with a minimum of 60% marks in their undergraduate degree
* Deadline: Varies depending on the course
* Official URL: https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk
### Chevening Scholarship
* Amount: Full tuition fees and living expenses
* Eligibility: Indian students with at least two years of work experience
* Deadline: November 1
* Official URL: https://www.chevening.org
### Erasmus Mundus Scholarship
* Amount: €1,000 to €1,400 per month stipend
* Eligibility: Indian students with a bachelor's degree
* Deadline: Varies depending on the course
* Official URL: https://ec.europa.eu/programmes/erasmus-mundus
### Felix Scholarship
* Amount: £12,000 to £17,000 per year
* Eligibility: Indian students with a first-class bachelor's degree
* Deadline: January 20
* Official URL: https://www.felixscholarship.org
### Charles Wallace India Trust Scholarship
* Amount: £700 to £1,000 per month stipend
* Eligibility: Indian students with a postgraduate degree
* Deadline: November 30
* Official URL: https://www.britishcouncil.in

## Application Steps
1. Check the eligibility criteria for the Tier 4 Visa and scholarships.
2. Choose the right course and university in London.
3. Apply for the course and receive an offer letter.
4. Apply for a scholarship, if eligible.
5. Prepare the required documents, including a valid passport and academic certificates.
6. Fill in the online Tier 4 Visa application form.
7. Pay the visa application fee and book an appointment at a Visa Application Centre.
8. Attend the appointment and submit the biometric data.

## Documents Checklist
* Valid passport
* Academic certificates and transcripts
* English language proficiency test results
* Proof of funds
* CAS statement from the university
* Scholarship award letter, if applicable
* Medical certificate, if required
