The University of Melbourne Scholarship for Asylum Seekers provides up to $110,000 in funding for asylum seekers pursuing a PhD in Engineering. 
Asylum seekers in Australia can apply for [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) to pursue higher education in STEM fields, including [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) in Engineering at the University of Melbourne. 
The university offers various [scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) and asylum seekers, including the ones listed below.

## Who Qualifies
The following visa categories qualify for the University of Melbourne Scholarship for Asylum Seekers: 
- Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785)
- Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790)
- Temporary Humanitarian Concern Visa (subclass 786)
- Protection Visa (subclass 866)
Asylum seekers with these visa categories can apply for [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada) or other international scholarships, but it's essential to check the eligibility criteria for each scholarship.

## Scholarships
### Melbourne School of Engineering Scholarship
The Melbourne School of Engineering Scholarship provides up to $110,000 in funding for PhD students in Engineering, eligibility includes asylum seekers, deadline is 31 October, and the official URL is https://eng.unimelb.edu.au/study/scholarships 
### Graduate Research Scholarship
The Graduate Research Scholarship offers up to $32,400 per annum for PhD students, eligibility includes asylum seekers, deadline is 31 October, and the official URL is https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/awards/graduate-research-scholarships 
### Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship
The Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship provides up to $32,400 per annum for PhD students, eligibility includes asylum seekers, deadline is 31 October, and the official URL is https://www.education.gov.au/australian-government-research-training-program 
### University of Melbourne Chancellors Scholarship
The University of Melbourne Chancellors Scholarship offers up to $20,000 per annum for undergraduate and graduate students, eligibility includes asylum seekers, deadline is 31 October, and the official URL is https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/awards/university-of-melbourne-chancellors-scholarship 
### Melbourne Humanitarian Access Scholarship
The Melbourne Humanitarian Access Scholarship provides up to $10,000 per annum for undergraduate and graduate students, eligibility includes asylum seekers, deadline is 31 October, and the official URL is https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/awards/melbourne-humanitarian-access-scholarship
