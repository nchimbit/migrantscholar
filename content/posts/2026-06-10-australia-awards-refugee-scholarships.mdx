## Introduction to Australia Awards Scholarships for Refugee Students
Australia Awards Scholarships are available for refugee students pursuing Master's degrees in Australia, providing them with a life-changing opportunity to gain a high-quality education and build a brighter future. These scholarships are designed to support students who have been displaced due to conflict, persecution, or other humanitarian crises, and are now residing in Australia on a valid visa. By offering these scholarships, the Australian government aims to promote education, economic growth, and social development among refugee communities.

## Who Qualifies for Australia Awards Scholarships
To be eligible for an Australia Awards Scholarship, refugee students must hold a valid Australian visa in one of the following categories:
* Temporary Protection Visa (TPV)
* Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (SHEV)
* Temporary Humanitarian Stay Visa (THS)
* Permanent Protection Visa (PPV)
* Humanitarian Settlement Visa (HSV)
* Refugee Visa (RV)
* Protection Visa (PV)
* Special Humanitarian Visa (SHV)

## Available Scholarships for Refugee Students
### Australia Awards Scholarship
The Australia Awards Scholarship provides full tuition fee coverage, living allowance, and other benefits to refugee students pursuing a Master's degree in Australia. The coverage amount is up to AU$30,000 per year, and the eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 6.0 and proficiency in English language. The deadline for application is 30 April 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/.
### Endeavour Postgraduate Scholarship
The Endeavour Postgraduate Scholarship offers up to AU$140,000 for a Master's degree program, including tuition fees, living allowance, and travel costs. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 6.5 and at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is 15 June 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/endavour.
### Research Training Program (RTP) Scholarship
The Research Training Program (RTP) Scholarship provides a stipend of up to AU$28,597 per year, tuition fee coverage, and other benefits to refugee students pursuing a Master's degree by research in Australia. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 7.0 and a research proposal. The deadline for application is 31 October 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.education.gov.au/research-training-program.
### International Postgraduate Research Scholarship (IPRS)
The International Postgraduate Research Scholarship (IPRS) offers a stipend of up to AU$28,597 per year, tuition fee coverage, and other benefits to refugee students pursuing a Master's degree by research in Australia. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 7.0 and a research proposal. The deadline for application is 31 January 2027, and the official website URL is https://www.education.gov.au/international-postgraduate-research-scholarship.
### Australian Government Research Training Program (AGRTP) Scholarship
The Australian Government Research Training Program (AGRTP) Scholarship provides a stipend of up to AU$28,597 per year, tuition fee coverage, and other benefits to refugee students pursuing a Master's degree by research in Australia. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 7.0 and a research proposal. The deadline for application is 15 March 2027, and the official website URL is https://www.education.gov.au/australian-government-research-training-program.
