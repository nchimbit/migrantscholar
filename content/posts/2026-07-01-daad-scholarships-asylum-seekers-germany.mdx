## Introduction to DAAD Scholarships for Asylum Seekers
The DAAD scholarships for asylum seekers in Germany are designed to support individuals who have fled their home countries and are now residing in Germany as asylum seekers. These scholarships aim to empower the future of asylum seekers by providing them with opportunities for personal and professional growth. As an asylum seeker in Germany, you may be wondering how you can access these scholarships and what benefits they offer.

## Who Qualifies for DAAD Scholarships
To qualify for DAAD scholarships, asylum seekers must hold a specific type of visa or residence permit. The following visa categories are eligible:
* Visa for asylum seekers (§ 24 AufenthG)
* Visa for toleration (§ 60a AufenthG)
* Visa for subsidiary protection (§ 25 Abs. 2 AufenthG)
* Visa for temporary protection (§ 24 AufenthG)
* Residence permit for asylum seekers (§ 55 Abs. 1 AsylG)

## Application Steps
To apply for DAAD scholarships, follow these eight steps:
1. Check your eligibility by reviewing the visa categories and scholarship requirements.
2. Choose the scholarship that best fits your needs and goals.
3. Prepare your application documents, including your CV, motivation letter, and academic certificates.
4. Submit your application through the DAAD online portal.
5. Pay the application fee, if applicable.
6. Wait for the application deadline to pass and the selection process to begin.
7. Receive a notification of the outcome of your application.
8. If selected, confirm your acceptance of the scholarship and begin making arrangements to start your studies in Germany.
