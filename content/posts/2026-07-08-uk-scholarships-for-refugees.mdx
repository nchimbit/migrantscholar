## Fully Funded UK Scholarships for Refugee Students
Refugee students pursuing undergraduate degrees in the UK can apply for fully funded scholarships, such as those offered by universities and charitable organizations, to cover their tuition fees and living expenses.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, refugee students must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Stateless person
* Refugee
* Humanitarian protection
* Limited leave to remain under the Family Life (Article 8) rules
* Discretionary leave
* Leave outside the rules
* A family member of someone with one of the above statuses
Refugee students with these visa categories can apply for the scholarships listed below.

## Available Scholarships
### The University of Manchester Scholarship 
The University of Manchester Scholarship provides full tuition fees and a living stipend of £15,000 per annum, with eligibility limited to refugee students who have been granted asylum in the UK. The deadline for application is November 2026, and further information can be found on the official website: https://www.manchester.ac.uk.

The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships offer full tuition fees and a living stipend of £14,500 per annum, with eligibility open to refugee students from any country. The deadline for application is January 2027, and further information can be found on the official website: https://www.ox.ac.uk.

The Chevening Scholarship provides full tuition fees and a living stipend of £15,000 per annum, with eligibility limited to refugee students who have been granted asylum in the UK and have a strong academic record. The UNHCR Scholarship provides full tuition fees and a living stipend of £12,000 per annum, with eligibility limited to refugee students who have been granted asylum in the UK and have a strong academic record. ## Application Steps
1. Research and choose a university and scholarship program that fits your academic goals and eligibility.
2. Review the eligibility criteria and application requirements for your chosen scholarship.
3. Prepare and gather all necessary documents, including academic transcripts, language proficiency tests, and visa documentation.
4. Complete and submit the online application form before the deadline.
5. Upload all required documents and attachments to the online application portal.
6. Provide a personal statement outlining your academic goals and reasons for applying for the scholarship.
7. Secure two academic references from previous teachers or professors.
8. Submit the application and wait for the review and selection process to be completed.
