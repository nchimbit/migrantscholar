## Introduction to Fully Funded UK Chevening Scholarships for Refugee Students
Are you a refugee student seeking to pursue higher education in the UK? The Fully Funded UK Chevening Scholarships are an excellent opportunity for refugee students to achieve their academic goals without financial burdens. The Chevening Scholarship is a prestigious award that covers tuition fees, living expenses, and other costs, making it an attractive option for migrant students.

## Who Qualifies for the Chevening Scholarships
To be eligible for the Chevening Scholarships, refugee students must fall under one of the following visa categories:
* Refugee status
* Humanitarian protection
* Discretionary leave
* Leave outside the rules
* Section 67 of the Immigration Act 2016 leave
* Other forms of protection leave

## Available Chevening Scholarships for Refugee Students
### Chevening Scholarship
The Chevening Scholarship is a fully funded award that covers up to £18,000 in tuition fees and £1,200 in monthly living expenses. To be eligible, students must have a bachelor's degree, at least two years of work experience, and fluency in English. The application deadline is usually in November, and interested students can visit the official website at https://www.chevening.org/ to apply.

### UK Government Scholarship
The UK Government Scholarship is a fully funded award that covers up to £20,000 in tuition fees and £1,500 in monthly living expenses. To be eligible, students must have a bachelor's degree, at least two years of work experience, and fluency in English. The application deadline is usually in January, and interested students can visit the official website at https://www.gov.uk/ to apply.

### Commonwealth Scholarship
The Commonwealth Scholarship is a fully funded award that covers up to £15,000 in tuition fees and £1,000 in monthly living expenses. To be eligible, students must have a bachelor's degree, at least one year of work experience, and fluency in English. The application deadline is usually in March, and interested students can visit the official website at https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/ to apply.

### Felix Scholarship
The Felix Scholarship is a fully funded award that covers up to £10,000 in tuition fees and £800 in monthly living expenses. To be eligible, students must have a bachelor's degree, at least one year of work experience, and fluency in English. The application deadline is usually in May, and interested students can visit the official website at https://www.felixscholarship.org/ to apply.

### ORSAS Scholarship
The ORSAS Scholarship is a fully funded award that covers up to £8,000 in tuition fees and £600 in monthly living expenses. To be eligible, students must have a bachelor's degree, at least one year of work experience, and fluency in English. The application deadline is usually in July, and interested students can visit the official website at https://www.orsas.ac.uk/ to apply.
