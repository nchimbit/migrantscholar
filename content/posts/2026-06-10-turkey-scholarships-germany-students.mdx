## Available Scholarships for International Students from Germany
### Turkey Scholarship for Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD Studies
The Turkey Scholarship is a fully funded program that covers tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 700-1,100 Turkish Lira. Eligible applicants must have a strong academic record and proficiency in English or Turkish. The deadline for applications is typically in February, and interested students can find more information on the [Official Turkey Scholarship Website](https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/).

### Turkish Government Scholarship for International Research
The Turkish Government Scholarship for International Research provides funding for research projects in various fields, including science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). The scholarship covers a monthly stipend of 2,500 Turkish Lira and a one-time research allowance of 10,000 Turkish Lira. Eligible applicants must have a Master's or PhD degree and a strong research proposal. The deadline for applications is typically in April, and interested students can find more information on the [Official Turkish Government Scholarship Website](https://www.tubitak.gov.tr/).

### YTB Scholarship for Undergraduate and Graduate Studies
The YTB Scholarship is a fully funded program that covers tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 900-1,400 Turkish Lira. Eligible applicants must have a strong academic record and proficiency in English or Turkish. The deadline for applications is typically in March, and interested students can find more information on the [Official YTB Scholarship Website](https://www.ytb.gov.tr/).

### Mevlana Exchange Program for Graduate Students
The Mevlana Exchange Program is a scholarship program that allows graduate students to study in Turkey for one or two semesters. The scholarship covers a monthly stipend of 750-1,000 Turkish Lira and a one-time travel allowance of 1,000 Turkish Lira. Eligible applicants must be enrolled in a graduate program and have a strong academic record. The deadline for applications is typically in February, and interested students can find more information on the [Official Mevlana Exchange Program Website](https://www.mevlana.gov.tr/).

### Istanbul Technical University Scholarship for PhD Studies
The Istanbul Technical University Scholarship for PhD Studies is a fully funded program that covers tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 1,500-2,000 Turkish Lira. Eligible applicants must have a Master's degree and a strong research proposal. The deadline for applications is typically in May, and interested students can find more information on the [Official Istanbul Technical University Website](https://www.itu.edu.tr/).
