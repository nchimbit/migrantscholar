## Introduction to Fully Funded Scholarships for Refugee Students in the UK
If you are a refugee student seeking to pursue higher education in the UK, there are several fully funded scholarships available to support your academic journey. These scholarships are designed to provide financial assistance to refugee students who are unable to afford the costs of studying in the UK. By applying for these scholarships, refugee students can focus on their studies and achieve their academic goals without worrying about financial constraints.

## Who Qualifies for Refugee Scholarships in the UK
To qualify for refugee scholarships in the UK, you must hold a specific visa category, including:
* Humanitarian Protection visa
* Refugee visa
* Family Reunion visa
* Discretionary Leave to Remain visa
* Leave to Remain under the Syrian Vulnerable Persons Resettlement Scheme
* Leave to Remain under the Vulnerable Children's Resettlement Scheme
* Other visa categories that provide protection in the UK

## Available Scholarships for Refugee Students
### The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships
The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships provide full funding for graduate studies at the University of Oxford, covering university fees, living costs, and returns airfare. To be eligible, applicants must be applying for a graduate course at the University of Oxford and must have a strong academic and professional background. The deadline for applications is usually in January, and applicants can find more information on the [University of Oxford website](https://www.ox.ac.uk/admissions/graduate/fees-and-funding/graduate-scholarships/weidenfeld-hoffmann-scholarships).

The Refugee Education UK Scholarships provide full funding for undergraduate and postgraduate studies at participating UK universities, covering university fees, living costs, and returns airfare. To be eligible, applicants must be applying for an undergraduate or postgraduate course at a participating UK university and must have a strong academic and professional background.
