# Women in Healthcare
The [Harvard University Center for AIDS Research Scholarship](https://cvpi.harvard.edu/training/funding-opportunities) exists and qualify women from developing countries pursuing Master's degrees in healthcare at Harvard University with funding amount of up to $50,000.

## Who Qualifies
Women from developing countries who are pursuing Master's degrees in healthcare at Harvard University and hold F-1 or J-1 visa categories are eligible to apply for emergency funding. Additionally, women who are [refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) or have [scholarship deadlines](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/deadlines) approaching may also qualify.

## Available Scholarships
### Harvard University Center for AIDS Research Scholarship
* Amount: up to $50,000
* Eligibility: Women from developing countries pursuing Master's degrees in healthcare
* Deadline: February 15, 2027
* Official URL: https://cvpi.harvard.edu/training/funding-opportunities
### Margaret McClure Scholarship
* Amount: up to $10,000
* Eligibility: Women pursuing Master's degrees in nursing
* Deadline: April 1, 2027
* Official URL: https://www.nursing.harvard.edu/financial-aid/scholarships
### American Association of University Women (AAUW) International Fellowship
* Amount: up to $30,000
* Eligibility: Women from developing countries pursuing Master's or PhD degrees
* Deadline: November 15, 2026
* Official URL: https://www.aauw.org/resources/programs/fellowships-grants/international-fellowships/
### Ford Foundation International Fellowship
* Amount: up to $50,000
* Eligibility: Women from developing countries pursuing Master's or PhD degrees
* Deadline: December 1, 2026
* Official URL: https://www.fordfoundation.org/work/educational-opportunities-and-scholarships/
### Fulbright Foreign Student Program
* Amount: up to $50,000
* Eligibility: Women from developing countries pursuing Master's or PhD degrees
* Deadline: October 1, 2026
* Official URL: https://foreign.fulbrightonline.org/about/foreign-fulbright
