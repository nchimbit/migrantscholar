## Fully Funded USA Fulbright Scholarships for Asylum Seekers
The Fulbright Scholarship is available to asylum seekers for graduate studies in the USA, providing a fully funded opportunity to pursue higher education in a prestigious American university.

## Who Qualifies
Asylum seekers with the following visa categories qualify for the Fulbright Scholarship:
* Refugee status (asylum granted)
* Asylum pending (asylum application submitted and pending)
* Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
* Deferred Enforced Departure (DED)
* Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA)
* Special Immigrant Visa (SIV)
* Afghan or Iraqi Special Immigrant Visa

## Available Scholarships
### Fulbright Foreign Student Program
The Fulbright Foreign Student Program provides up to $100,000 in funding for one academic year, covering tuition, living costs, and travel expenses. Eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and admission to a graduate program in the USA. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.iie.org/programs/fulbright_foreign_student_program.

### Fulbright Visiting Scholar Program
The Fulbright Visiting Scholar Program offers up to $50,000 in funding for one academic year, covering research and living expenses. Eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and admission to a graduate program in the USA. The deadline for application is August 2026. For more information, visit https://www.cies.org/program/fulbright-visiting-scholar-program.

### Fulbright Scholar-in-Residence Program
The Fulbright Scholar-in-Residence Program provides up to $30,000 in funding for one academic year, covering teaching and research expenses. Eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and admission to a graduate program in the USA. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.cies.org/program/fulbright-scholar-in-residence.

### Hubert H. Humphrey Fellowship Program
The Hubert H. Humphrey Fellowship Program offers up to $60,000 in funding for one academic year, covering tuition, living costs, and travel expenses. Eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and admission to a graduate program in the USA. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit https://www.humphreyfellowship.org/.

### Fulbright Teaching Assistantship
The Fulbright Teaching Assistantship provides up to $20,000 in funding for one academic year, covering teaching and living expenses. Eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and admission to a graduate program in the USA. The deadline for application is July 2026. For more information, visit https://www.fulbright.ie/teaching-assistantship.

## Application Steps
1. Review the eligibility criteria and choose the scholarship that best fits your needs.
2. Prepare the required documents, including a resume, personal statement, and transcripts.
3. Submit an online application through the official website.
4. Complete and submit any additional forms or supporting materials.
5. Take the required English language proficiency test (e.g. TOEFL or IELTS).
6. Apply for admission to a graduate program in the USA.
7. Wait for the selection process to be completed and receive notification of the scholarship award.
8. Accept the scholarship award and prepare for travel to the USA.
