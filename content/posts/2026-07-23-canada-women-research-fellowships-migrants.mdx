The [Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships](https://www.canada.ca/en/research-funding-programs/vanier-canada-graduate-scholarships.html) exist for doctoral students, including migrant women, with a funding amount of $50,000 per year for three years. 

## Who Qualifies
Migrant women who hold a valid Canadian temporary resident visa, permanent resident visa, or are protected persons, including refugee claimants, may be eligible for women-only research fellowships in Canada. This includes those with a study permit, work permit, or temporary resident permit. Eligible visa categories include, but are not limited to, the Federal Skilled Worker Program, the Federal Skilled Trades Program, and the Canadian Experience Class.

## Available Scholarships
### Killam Research Fellowships
The Killam Research Fellowships, valued at $70,000 per year, are awarded to outstanding doctoral students, including migrant women, who are pursuing research in a wide range of disciplines. Eligibility includes being a Canadian citizen or permanent resident, with a deadline of May 1st. For more information, visit the official website at [https://www.killamlaureates.ca/](https://www.killamlaureates.ca/).

### Banting Postdoctoral Fellowships
The Banting Postdoctoral Fellowships, valued at $70,000 per year, are awarded to outstanding postdoctoral researchers, including migrant women, who are pursuing research in a wide range of disciplines. Eligibility includes being a Canadian citizen or permanent resident, with a deadline of September 21st. For more information, visit the official website at [https://banting.fellowships-bourses.gc.ca/](https://banting.fellowships-bourses.gc.ca/).

### Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarships
The Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarships, valued at $40,000 per year, are awarded to outstanding doctoral students, including migrant women, who are pursuing research in the social sciences and humanities. Eligibility includes being a Canadian citizen or permanent resident, with a deadline of December 21st. For more information, visit the official website at [https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/](https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/).

### SSHRC Doctoral Fellowships
The SSHRC Doctoral Fellowships, valued at $20,000 per year, are awarded to outstanding doctoral students, including migrant women, who are pursuing research in the social sciences and humanities. Eligibility includes being a Canadian citizen or permanent resident, with a deadline of October 17th. For more information, visit the official website at [https://www.sshrc-crsh.gc.ca/](https://www.sshrc-crsh.gc.ca/).

### NSERC Postgraduate Scholarships
The NSERC Postgraduate Scholarships, valued at $21,000 per year, are awarded to outstanding masters and doctoral students, including migrant women, who are pursuing research in the natural sciences and engineering. Eligibility includes being a Canadian citizen or permanent resident, with a deadline of December 1st. For more information, visit the official website at [https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/](https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/).
