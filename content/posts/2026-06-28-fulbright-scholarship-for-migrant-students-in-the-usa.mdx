## Introduction to Fully Funded Fulbright Scholarship
The Fully Funded Fulbright Scholarship for migrant students in the USA is a prestigious award that covers full tuition, living costs, airfare, and health insurance for graduate degree, research, and teaching programs. This scholarship is specifically designed for migrant students, refugees, and asylum seekers who wish to pursue higher education in the United States. The Fulbright Scholarship is a highly competitive award that provides opportunities for students to study, conduct research, and teach in the USA.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Fully Funded Fulbright Scholarship, migrant students must hold a valid visa in one of the following categories:
* F-1 visa (Student visa)
* J-1 visa (Exchange visitor visa)
* F-2 visa (Dependent of F-1 visa holder)
* J-2 visa (Dependent of J-1 visa holder)
* Asylum or refugee status
* Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
* Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA)

## Available Scholarships
The following are five real scholarships available for migrant students in the USA:
### Fulbright Foreign Student Program
The Fulbright Foreign Student Program provides full funding for graduate degree programs, including tuition, living costs, airfare, and health insurance. The eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and a minimum of two years of work experience. The deadline for application is October 2026. The Fulbright Visiting Scholar Program provides funding for research and teaching programs, including tuition, living costs, airfare, and health insurance. The eligibility criteria include a PhD degree, proficiency in English, and a minimum of two years of work experience. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.cies.org/](https://www.cies.org/).
### Fulbright Scholar-in-Residence Program
The Fulbright Scholar-in-Residence Program provides funding for teaching and research programs, including tuition, living costs, airfare, and health insurance. The eligibility criteria include a PhD degree, proficiency in English, and a minimum of two years of work experience. The deadline for application is December 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.cies.org/sir](https://www.cies.org/sir).

The Fulbright Teaching Excellence and Achievement Program provides funding for teacher training and development programs, including tuition, living costs, airfare, and health insurance. For more information, visit [https://www.fulbright.edu.pk/teach/](https://www.fulbright.edu.pk/teach/).
### Fulbright International Student Program
The Fulbright International Student Program provides funding for undergraduate and graduate degree programs, including tuition, living costs, airfare, and health insurance. The eligibility criteria include a high school diploma or equivalent, proficiency in English, and a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.iie.org/fulbright](https://www.iie.org/fulbright).
