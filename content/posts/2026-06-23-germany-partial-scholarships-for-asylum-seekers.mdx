## Introduction to Partially Funded Scholarships for Asylum Seekers in Germany
As an asylum seeker in Germany, you may be wondering if there are partially funded scholarships available to support your education and vocational training. The good news is that yes, there are several scholarships offered by German institutions and organizations that can help you cover the costs of language courses and vocational training. These scholarships can provide you with the opportunity to improve your language skills, gain professional qualifications, and enhance your employability in the German job market.

## Who Qualifies for Partially Funded Scholarships
To be eligible for partially funded scholarships in Germany, you typically need to hold a specific type of visa or residence permit. The exact visa categories that qualify for these scholarships include:
* Subsidiary protection status (§ 4 Abs. 1 Satz 2 AufenthG)
* Refugee status (§ 3 Abs. 1 AsylG)
* Asylum seeker status (§ 1 AsylG)
* Temporary residence permit (§ 23 Abs. 1 AufenthG)
* Residence permit for humanitarian reasons (§ 24 AufenthG)
