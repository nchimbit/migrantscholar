## Introduction to Fully Funded Undergraduate Awards for Refugee Students in Canada
If you are a refugee student seeking to pursue an undergraduate education in Canada, there are several fully funded awards available to support your academic journey. The Canadian government and various universities offer these scholarships to help refugee students achieve their educational goals without financial burden. These awards not only cover tuition fees but also provide living costs, ensuring that students can focus on their studies.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these fully funded undergraduate awards, refugee students must hold a specific immigration status in Canada. The following visa categories qualify:
* Convention Refugee
* Protected Person
* Refugee Claimant with a positive pre-removal risk assessment
* Person in Need of Protection
* Government-Assisted Refugee
* Privately Sponsored Refugee
* Dependent of any of the above categories
