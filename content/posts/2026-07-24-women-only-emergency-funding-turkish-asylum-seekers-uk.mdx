The Helena Kennedy Foundation Bursary exists and qualifies Turkish asylum seekers in the UK, providing up to £1,500 in emergency funding for education purposes. 

## Who Qualifies
Turkish asylum seekers who hold a UK visa under the categories of asylum, humanitarian protection, or discretionary leave can apply for women-only emergency funding in the UK. This includes those with a pending asylum application, as well as refugees who have already been granted status but require additional financial support for their education and living expenses. 

## Available Scholarships
### The Helena Kennedy Foundation Bursary
- Amount: Up to £1,500
- Eligibility: Asylum seekers, refugees, and individuals with discretionary leave in the UK
- Deadline: Varies, dependant on specific education course application deadlines
- Official URL: https://www.hkf.org.uk/our-work/bursaries/
### The Refugee Support Network Bursary
- Amount: Up to £500
- Eligibility: Refugee and asylum-seeking students in the UK
- Deadline: Rolling deadline, with applications accepted throughout the year
- Official URL: https://www.refugeesupportnetwork.org/our-services/bursaries
### The Unison Foundation Scholarship
- Amount: £1,000
- Eligibility: Asylum seekers, refugees, and migrant workers in the UK
