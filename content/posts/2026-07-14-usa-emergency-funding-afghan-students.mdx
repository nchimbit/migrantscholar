## Available Scholarships
### Afghanistan Scholarship
The [Afghanistan Scholarship](https://www.iie.org/Programs/Afghanistan-Scholarship) provides up to $10,000 in funding per year to Afghan migrant students pursuing higher education in the USA. 
The eligibility criteria include being an Afghan national, holding a valid visa, and demonstrating financial need. 
The deadline for application is March 15th, and the official URL is https://www.iie.org/Programs/Afghanistan-Scholarship.

### Fulbright Scholarship
The [Fulbright Scholarship](https://fulbright.org/) is a prestigious award that provides funding for international students, including Afghan migrants, to pursue higher education in the USA. 
The amount of funding varies, but it typically covers full tuition and living expenses. 
Eligibility criteria include holding a bachelor's degree, having a strong academic record, and demonstrating leadership potential. 
The deadline for application is October 12th, and the official URL is https://fulbright.org/.
