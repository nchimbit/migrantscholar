## Canada's York University Scholarship for Refugee Students: Eligibility and Application
The York University Scholarship is available to refugee students who are looking to pursue undergraduate programs in Canada, providing a unique opportunity for those with specific visa categories to achieve their academic goals. 

## Who Qualifies
Refugee students who hold one of the following visa categories are eligible to apply for the York University Scholarship:
* Convention Refugee Abroad Class (CRAC)
* Country of Asylum Class (COAC)
* Protected Persons in Canada Class (PPCC)
* Government-Assisted Refugee (GAR)
* Privately Sponsored Refugee (PSR)
* Blended Visa Office-Referred Refugee (BVOR)

## Available Scholarships
### The York University International Student Scholarship
The York University International Student Scholarship provides coverage of up to $20,000 per year, and eligibility is based on academic excellence and community service. The deadline for application is March 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://futurestudents.yorku.ca/scholarships.
### The United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) Scholarship
The UNHCR Scholarship offers full tuition coverage and a stipend for living costs, with eligibility limited to students with a UNHCR refugee registration certificate. The deadline for application is November 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.unhcr.org/.
### The Canadian Government's Refugee Scholarship
The Canadian Government's Refugee Scholarship provides up to $30,000 per year for the duration of the undergraduate program, and eligibility is based on financial need and academic merit. The deadline for application is June 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship.html.
### The York University Faculty of Liberal Arts and Professional Studies (LA&PS) Refugee Scholarship
The LA&PS Refugee Scholarship offers up to $15,000 per year, with eligibility based on academic excellence and community service. The deadline for application is April 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://laps.yorku.ca/.
### The Schulich School of Business Refugee Scholarship
The Schulich School of Business Refugee Scholarship provides up to $25,000 per year, with eligibility limited to students enrolled in the Schulich School of Business undergraduate program. The deadline for application is February 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://schulich.yorku.ca/.

1. Review the eligibility criteria for the York University Scholarship and ensure you meet the requirements.
