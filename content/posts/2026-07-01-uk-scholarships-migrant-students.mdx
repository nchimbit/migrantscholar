## Introduction to Fully Funded UK Scholarships
Are you a migrant student looking to pursue higher education in the UK? The UK offers numerous fully funded scholarships to migrant students, covering full tuition fees and living costs, making it an attractive destination for international students. In this blog post, we will explore the various fully funded UK scholarships available to migrant students in 2026, the eligibility criteria, and the application process.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for fully funded UK scholarships, migrant students must hold a valid visa under one of the following categories:
* Tier 4 (General) student visa
* Tier 2 (General) work visa
* Tier 5 (Temporary Worker) visa
* Family visa
* Refugee or humanitarian protection status
* Other visa categories may also be eligible, but it's essential to check the specific scholarship requirements.

## Available Scholarships
### Chevening Scholarship
The Chevening Scholarship offers a coverage amount of up to £18,000 per annum, and eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree and at least two years of work experience. The deadline for applications is November 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.chevening.org/.

### Commonwealth Scholarship
The Commonwealth Scholarship provides a coverage amount of up to £12,000 per annum, and eligible applicants must be from a Commonwealth country and have a bachelor's degree. The deadline for applications is March 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/.

### Gates Cambridge Scholarship
The Gates Cambridge Scholarship offers a coverage amount of up to £18,000 per annum, and eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree and be applying for a postgraduate course at the University of Cambridge. The deadline for applications is December 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.gatescambridge.org/.

### Rhodes Scholarship
The Rhodes Scholarship provides a coverage amount of up to £15,000 per annum, and eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree and be applying for a postgraduate course at the University of Oxford. The deadline for applications is October 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk/.

### Fulbright Scholarship
The Fulbright Scholarship offers a coverage amount of up to £20,000 per annum, and eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree and be applying for a postgraduate course in the US, but some awards are available for study in the UK. The deadline for applications is October 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.fulbright.org.uk/.

## Application Steps
1. Research the available scholarships and their eligibility criteria.
2. Check your visa category and ensure you meet the requirements.
3. Review the scholarship application deadlines and plan accordingly.
4. Prepare your application documents, including transcripts, references, and a personal statement.
5. Submit your application through the official scholarship website or postal address.
6. Track the status of your application and await the scholarship decision.
7. If successful, accept the scholarship offer and complete any additional requirements.
8. Begin your studies in the UK and make the most of the scholarship opportunity.
