# DAAD Scholarship
The DAAD Helmut-Schmidt-Programme for Master's Studies in Germany exists and qualifies students from developing countries for a fully funded opportunity with a monthly stipend of 931 Euros.

## Who Qualifies
The DAAD Helmut-Schmidt-Programme is open to students from developing countries who hold a bachelor's degree and have at least two years of professional experience. The programme also accepts students with refugee status, who can apply under the DAFI scholarship programme. Visa categories for this programme include student visa and job seeker visa.

## Available Scholarships
### DAAD Helmut-Schmidt-Programme
* Amount: 931 Euros per month
* Eligibility: Students from developing countries with a bachelor's degree and at least two years of professional experience
* Deadline: July 31st
* Official URL: https://www.daad.de/en/study-and-research-in-germany/scholarships/daad-scholarships/helmut-schmidt-programme/
### Erasmus Mundus Scholarship
* Amount: 1,400 Euros per month
* Eligibility: Students from any country with a bachelor's degree
* Deadline: December 15th
* Official URL: https://www.erasmusmundus.eu/
### Fulbright Scholarship
* Amount: 1,900 Euros per month
* Eligibility: Students from the United States with a bachelor's degree
* Deadline: October 12th
* Official URL: https://us.fulbrightonline.org/
### Commonwealth Scholarship
* Amount: 1,200 Euros per month
* Eligibility: Students from Commonwealth countries with a bachelor's degree
* Deadline: December 1st
* Official URL: https://www.csss.ukri.org/
### UNICEF Scholarship
* Amount: 1,000 Euros per month
* Eligibility: Students from developing countries with a bachelor's degree and at least two years of professional experience
* Deadline: June 30th
* Official URL: https://www.unicef.org/
