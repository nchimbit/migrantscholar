Canada's Express Entry visa is a popular option for Indian skilled migrants who wish to live and work in Canada. The Express Entry visa is a points-based system that considers factors such as age, education, work experience, and language proficiency. To be eligible for the Express Entry visa, Indian migrants must meet the eligibility criteria for one of the three visa categories: Federal Skilled Worker Program, Federal Skilled Trades Program, or Canadian Experience Class.

## Who Qualifies
The Express Entry visa is open to Indian skilled migrants who meet the eligibility criteria for one of the three visa categories. The Federal Skilled Worker Program is for skilled workers with work experience in a specific occupation, while the Federal Skilled Trades Program is for skilled tradespeople with work experience in a specific trade. The Canadian Experience Class is for individuals with Canadian work experience. To be eligible, Indian migrants must have a minimum of one year of work experience in a skilled occupation, a bachelor's degree or higher, and proficiency in English or French.
