## Introduction to DAAD Scholarships for Refugee Students in Germany
As a refugee student, pursuing graduate studies in Germany can be a daunting task, but the DAAD Scholarships offer a path to higher education, providing financial support and opportunities for academic growth. The DAAD Scholarships are designed to support refugee students in achieving their academic goals, and in this blog post, we will explore the details of these scholarships and the application process. With the right guidance and support, refugee students can overcome the challenges they face and succeed in their academic pursuits in Germany.

## Who Qualifies for DAAD Scholarships
To be eligible for DAAD Scholarships, refugee students must hold a specific visa category, including:
* Visa for humanitarian reasons (§ 22 paragraph 2)
* Visa for political or racial persecution (§ 22 paragraph 2)
* Visa for family reunification (§ 22 paragraph 2)
* Visa for subsidiary protection (§ 24 paragraph 1)
* Visa for temporary protection (§ 24 paragraph 1)
* residence permit for humanitarian reasons (§ 25 paragraph 4 sentence 1)
* residence permit for subsidiary protection (§ 25 paragraph 2 sentence 1 alternate 2)

## Application Steps for DAAD Scholarships
To apply for DAAD Scholarships, refugee students must follow these steps:
1. Choose the scholarship programme that best fits their academic goals and eligibility criteria.
2. Review the official website of the DAAD scholarship programme and read the application guidelines carefully.
3. Prepare all required documents, including academic transcripts, language proficiency certificates, and visa documents.
4. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline.
5. Upload all required documents to the online application portal.
6. Submit a letter of motivation and a study plan outlining their academic goals and research interests.
7. Get a letter of recommendation from a academic supervisor or professor.
8. Submit the application and wait for the selection result.

## Documents Checklist
The following documents are required for the DAAD scholarship application:
* Academic transcripts and diplomas
* Language proficiency certificates (English or German)
* Visa documents (passport, visa, residence permit)
* Letter of motivation and study plan
* Letter of recommendation from an academic supervisor or professor
* Proof of financial support (bank statement, scholarship award letter)
* Health insurance certificate
* Certificate of registration at a German university
