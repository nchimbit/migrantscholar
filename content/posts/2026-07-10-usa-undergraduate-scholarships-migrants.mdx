The Migrant Students Scholarship, available to migrant students, asylum seekers, and refugees, offers up to $20,000 in partial funding for undergraduate studies in the USA.
## Overview
The Migrant Students Scholarship is designed to support migrant students pursuing higher education in the United States. This scholarship aims to provide financial assistance to students who have been displaced due to various reasons and are now seeking to build a better future for themselves.
## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Migrant Students Scholarship, applicants must fall under one of the following visa categories:
* F-1 visa holders
* J-1 visa holders
* Asylum seekers with pending applications
* Refugees with valid status
* Special Immigrant Visa holders
* Temporary Protected Status holders
* Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals recipients

## Available Scholarships
### 1. The Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans
The Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans offers $90,000 over two years to migrant students pursuing undergraduate studies in the USA. Eligible applicants must be 30 years old or younger and have a bachelor's degree or be in their final year of undergraduate studies. The deadline for application is November 1, 2026. For more information, visit https://pdsoros.org/
### 2. The Gates Scholarship
The Gates Scholarship provides full funding for undergraduate studies, including tuition, fees, room, and board. Eligible applicants must be migrant students, asylum seekers, or refugees who are currently in their senior year of high school. The deadline for application is September 15, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.thegatesscholarship.org/
### 3. The National Merit Scholarship
The National Merit Scholarship offers $2,500 to $5,000 per year to migrant students pursuing undergraduate studies in the USA. Eligible applicants must be in their senior year of high school and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is October 31, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.nationalmerit.org/
### 4. The Horatio Alger Scholarship
The Horatio Alger Scholarship provides $2,500 to $20,000 to migrant students pursuing undergraduate studies in the USA. Eligible applicants must be in their senior year of high school and have a minimum GPA of 2.0. The deadline for application is October 25, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.scholars.horatioalger.org/
### 5. The Coca-Cola Scholars Program
The Coca-Cola Scholars Program offers $1,000 to $20,000 to migrant students pursuing undergraduate studies in the USA. Eligible applicants must be in their senior year of high school and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is October 31, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.coca-colascholars.org/
