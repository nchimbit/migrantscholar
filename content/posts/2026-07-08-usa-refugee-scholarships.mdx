## Fully Funded Scholarships for Refugee Students in the USA
Refugee students in the USA can apply for fully funded scholarships to pursue higher education, including those with F-1, J-1, asylum, or other protected status visas. 

### Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, applicants must hold one of the following visa categories:
* F-1 student visa
* J-1 exchange visa
* Asylum status
* Refugee status
* Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
* Special Immigrant Visa (SIV)
* Other protected status

There are several fully funded scholarships available for refugee students in the USA, including:

The Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans provides up to $90,000 in funding for two years of graduate study. The Refugee Scholarship Fund offers up to $10,000 per academic year for undergraduate or graduate study. ### The International Student Scholarship
The International Student Scholarship offers up to $20,000 per academic year for undergraduate or graduate study. Eligible applicants must be enrolled in an accredited US institution and demonstrate academic excellence. The deadline for application is June 2027. For more information, visit https://www.internationalstudentscholarship.org/.

The Harvard University Refugee Scholarship provides full tuition and living costs for the duration of an undergraduate or graduate program. ## Application Process
To apply for these scholarships, follow these steps:
1. Review the eligibility criteria for each scholarship.
2. Gather all required documents, including transcripts and visa documents.
3. Submit the application form and supporting documents before the deadline.
4. Prepare for and take any required standardized tests, such as the SAT or GRE.
5. Submit letters of recommendation from academic or professional mentors.
6. Write and submit a personal statement or essay.
7. Verify the application and submit any additional materials.
8. Follow up with the scholarship committee to ensure the application is complete.

## Required Documents
To complete the application process, be sure to have the following documents:
* Passport and visa documents
* Transcripts from previous academic institutions
* Letters of recommendation
