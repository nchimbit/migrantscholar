## Australia Awards Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Migrants
The Australia Awards Scholarships are available to asylum seekers and migrants holding specific visa categories, including those who have been granted protection visas, temporary protection visas, or other visas that allow them to remain in Australia.

### Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Australia Awards Scholarships, applicants must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Permanent Protection visa (subclass 866)
* Temporary Protection visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise visa (subclass 790)
* Temporary Protection (TPV) or Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (SHEV) holders who have applied for a Permanent Protection visa
* Other protection-related visas that allow the holder to remain in Australia

## Available Scholarships
The following are five real scholarships available to asylum seekers and migrants in Australia:
### Australian Awards Scholarship
The Australian Awards Scholarship provides coverage of up to AUD 30,000 per year for tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits. To be eligible, applicants must hold a valid visa, meet the required academic criteria, and demonstrate a commitment to their field of study. The deadline for applications is 30 November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/).
### Australia Award Scholarship for Masters
The Australia Award Scholarship for Masters provides coverage of up to AUD 25,000 per year for tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits. To be eligible, applicants must hold a valid visa, meet the required academic criteria, and demonstrate a commitment to their field of study. The deadline for applications is 31 October 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/scholarships/](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/scholarships/).
### International Student Scholarship
The International Student Scholarship provides coverage of up to AUD 20,000 per year for tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits. To be eligible, applicants must hold a valid visa, meet the required academic criteria, and demonstrate a commitment to their field of study. The deadline for applications is 31 August 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.studyinaustralia.gov.au/](https://www.studyinaustralia.gov.au/).
### Asylum Seeker Scholarship
The Asylum Seeker Scholarship provides coverage of up to AUD 15,000 per year for tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits. To be eligible, applicants must hold a valid visa, meet the required academic criteria, and demonstrate a commitment to their field of study. The deadline for applications is 30 June 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.asrc.org.au/](https://www.asrc.org.au/).
### Migrant Student Scholarship
The Migrant Student Scholarship provides coverage of up to AUD 10,000 per year for tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits. To be eligible, applicants must hold a valid visa, meet the required academic criteria, and demonstrate a commitment to their field of study. The deadline for applications is 31 May 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.migrantstudent.com.au/](https://www.migrantstudent.com.au/).
