## DAAD Scholarships in Germany for International Students
The DAAD Scholarships in Germany are available to international students with outstanding academic achievements, providing them with the opportunity to pursue higher education in Germany. 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the DAAD Scholarships, international students must fall under one of the following visa categories:
* Visa for the purpose of studying (Subtitle III, Chapter 2, Section 2, Paragraph 16b, 1)
* Visa for the purpose of language course (Subtitle III, Chapter 2, Section 2, Paragraph 16b, 2)
* Visa for the purpose of school attendance (Subtitle III, Chapter 2, Section 2, Paragraph 16b, 3)
* Visa for the purpose of vocational training (Subtitle III, Chapter 2, Section 2, Paragraph 16b, 4)
* Visa for the purpose of higher education (Subtitle III, Chapter 2, Section 2, Paragraph 16b, 5)

## Available Scholarships
### Helmut-Schmidt-Programme
The Helmut-Schmidt-Programme offers a monthly stipend of 850 euros, covering tuition fees and living costs, to international students pursuing a master's degree in Germany. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum grade of 2.5 or equivalent. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.daad.de](https://www.daad.de).

### Development-Related Postgraduate Courses
The Development-Related Postgraduate Courses scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 750 euros, covering tuition fees and living costs, to international students pursuing a postgraduate degree in development-related fields in Germany. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum grade of 2.5 or equivalent. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.daad.de](https://www.daad.de).

### Public Policy and Good Governance
The Public Policy and Good Governance scholarship offers a monthly stipend of 750 euros, covering tuition fees and living costs, to international students pursuing a master's degree in public policy and good governance in Germany. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum grade of 2.5 or equivalent. The deadline for application is July 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.daad.de](https://www.daad.de).

### Master's Programmes for International Students
The Master's Programmes for International Students scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 750 euros, covering tuition fees and living costs, to international students pursuing a master's degree in Germany. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum grade of 2.5 or equivalent. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.daad.de](https://www.daad.de).

### Research Grants
The Research Grants scholarship offers a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros, covering living costs, to international students pursuing a research degree in Germany. Eligible applicants must have a master's degree with a minimum grade of 2.5 or equivalent. The deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.daad.de](https://www.daad.de).

## Application Process
To apply for the DAAD Scholarships, international students must follow these steps:
1. Check the eligibility criteria for the chosen scholarship program.
2. Choose the study program and university in Germany.
3. Prepare the required documents.
4. Fill out the application form.
5. Submit the application via the DAAD online portal.
6. Wait for the selection result.
7. Accept the scholarship offer.
8. Prepare for departure to Germany.

## Documents Checklist
The following documents are required for the DAAD Scholarships application:
* Passport copy
* Bachelor's or master's degree certificate
* Transcripts of records
* Language proficiency certificate (e.g. IELTS or TOEFL)
* Letter of motivation
* Recommendation letter
* Study plan
* Proof of financing
