# Germany Visa
The DAAD Scholarship, which qualifies Indian nationals, offers funding of up to €750 per month to pursue higher education in Germany. Indian students can benefit from this scholarship to study in Germany, a country known for its high-quality education and research opportunities. Many Indian students prefer to study in Germany due to its [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) and [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd). To be eligible for a German student visa, Indian nationals must meet specific requirements, including language proficiency and academic qualifications. 

## Who Qualifies
Indian nationals who wish to study in Germany can apply for a German student visa under the following categories: student visa, language course visa, or internship visa. The visa category depends on the purpose of the stay in Germany. To increase their chances of getting a visa, Indian students can also apply for [scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) or [DAAD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/universities/daad).

## Application Steps
1. Check the [scholarship deadlines](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/deadlines) to ensure you apply on time.
2. Choose the correct visa category based on your purpose of stay in Germany.
3. Gather all required documents, including a valid passport, birth certificate, and academic certificates.
4. Fill out the visa application form and sign it.
5. Submit your application and pay the visa fee.
6. Attend a visa interview at the German embassy or consulate in India.
7. Wait for the visa decision and collect your passport with the visa sticker.
8. Register with the local authorities in Germany after arrival.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is the duration of a German student visa?
A German student visa is usually issued for a period of one to two years, depending on the length of the study program.
2. Can I work part-time while studying in Germany?
Yes, international students can work part-time for up to 20 hours per week while studying in Germany.
3. Do I need to know German to study in Germany?
While many programs are taught in English, knowledge of German can be beneficial for everyday life in Germany.
4. Can I apply for a German student visa without a language proficiency certificate?
No, a language proficiency certificate is required for a German student visa application.
5. How long does it take to process a German student visa application?
The processing time for a German student visa application can take several weeks to several months.
6. Can I bring my family members to Germany on a student visa?
Dependents of international students can apply for a family reunification visa to join their family members in Germany.
7. Do I need health insurance to study in Germany?
Yes, health insurance is mandatory for international students in Germany.
8. Can I extend my German student visa?
Yes, a German student visa can be extended if the student is making good progress in their studies.
