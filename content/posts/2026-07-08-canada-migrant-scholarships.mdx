## University of British Columbia Scholarships for Migrant Students in Canada
The University of British Columbia offers various scholarships to migrant students pursuing PhD research in Canada, including those with temporary resident visas, permanent resident visas, or refugee status. 

### Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, migrant students must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Temporary Resident Visa (TRV)
* Permanent Resident Visa (PRV)
* Protected Persons status under section 95 of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act
* Convention Refugees under section 96 of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act
* Persons in Canada with a study permit or work permit

### Available Scholarships
The University of British Columbia offers the following scholarships for migrant students pursuing PhD research:
### Four Year Doctoral Fellowship
The Four Year Doctoral Fellowship provides $18,200 per year for four years, and is awarded to top international students. Eligible students must be starting their PhD program, and must not have already received the award. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.grad.ubc.ca/awards/four-year-doctoral-fellowship.
### Affiliated Doctoral Fellowship
The Affiliated Doctoral Fellowship provides $22,000 per year for two years, and is awarded to top international students. Eligible students must be starting their PhD program, and must not have already received the award. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.grad.ubc.ca/awards/affiliated-fellowships.
### Killam Doctoral Scholarship
The Killam Doctoral Scholarship provides $30,000 per year for two years, and is awarded to top international students. ### President's Academic Excellence Initiative PhD Award
The President's Academic Excellence Initiative PhD Award provides $10,000 for one year, and is awarded to top international students. Eligible students must be starting their PhD program, and must not have already received the award. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.grad.ubc.ca/awards/presidents-academic-excellence-phd-award.
### University Doctoral Fellowship
The University Doctoral Fellowship provides $10,000 per year for four years, and is awarded to top international students. Eligible students must be starting their PhD program, and must not have already received the award. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.grad.ubc.ca/awards/university-doctoral-fellowship.

## Application Process
To apply for these scholarships, migrant students must follow these steps:
1. Review the eligibility criteria and ensure that they meet the requirements.
2. Choose the scholarship they wish to apply for and review the application deadline.
3. Prepare the required documents, including transcripts and letters of recommendation.
4. Submit the application through the University of British Columbia's online application portal.
5. Pay the application fee, if required.
6. Upload the required documents to the online application portal.
7. Submit the application and wait for a decision.
8. If awarded a scholarship, review and accept the terms and conditions.
