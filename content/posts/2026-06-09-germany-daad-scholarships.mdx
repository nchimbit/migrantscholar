If you're an international student looking to pursue your graduate studies in Germany, you're in luck. The German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) offers a range of fully funded scholarships for international students. Who qualifies for these scholarships? International students from around the world who have completed their undergraduate degree and are looking to pursue their master's or Ph.D. in Germany are eligible to apply. The DAAD scholarships are designed to support graduate students who are interested in pursuing their academic dreams in Germany. Some of the fully funded DAAD scholarships for international students in Germany include: Helmut Schmidt Programme (HSP) - This scholarship covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs, with a deadline of July 31st, 2026. Development-Related Postgraduate Courses (EPOS) - This scholarship covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs, with a deadline of October 15th, 2026. Graduate School Scholarship Programme (GSSP) - This scholarship covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs, with a deadline of February 1st, 2027. Research Grants for Doctoral Candidates - This scholarship covers living expenses and travel costs, with a deadline of April 15th, 2027. To apply for these scholarships, follow these step-by-step instructions: Step 1: Choose your scholarship programme and read the eligibility criteria and requirements. Step 2: Prepare your application documents, including your curriculum vitae, motivation letter, and academic certificates. Step 3: Submit your application online through the DAAD portal. Step 4: Wait for the selection process to be completed and receive an email notification. Here is a checklist of documents you'll need to apply: Curriculum vitae, Motivation letter, Academic certificates (Bachelor's and Master's degrees), Proof of language proficiency (English or German), Two letters of recommendation. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs): Q: What is the eligibility criteria for DAAD scholarships? A: International students from around the world who have completed their undergraduate degree and are looking to pursue their master's or Ph.D. in Germany are eligible to apply.
