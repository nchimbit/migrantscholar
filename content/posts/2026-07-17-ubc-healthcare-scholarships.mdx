# UBC Scholar
The University of British Columbia Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program exists and qualifies migrant students from Sub-Saharan Africa, with a funding amount of up to $25,000 per year. This program is one of the many [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada) available to migrant students. The University of British Columbia is a prestigious institution that offers various [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) to students from around the world.

## Who Qualifies
Migrant students who hold a valid Canadian study permit, temporary resident visa, or permanent resident card are eligible to apply for these scholarships. The following visa categories are also eligible: study permit, work permit, temporary resident permit, and permanent resident card. [Scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) are also available for those who have been granted refugee status in Canada.

## Scholarships
The following are some of the real scholarships available to migrant students at the University of British Columbia:
### Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program
* Amount: up to $25,000 per year
* Eligibility: Migrant students from Sub-Saharan Africa
* Deadline: December 11, 2026
* Official URL: https://www.mastercardfdn.org/all/scholars/
### President's Entrance Scholarship
* Amount: up to $20,000
* Eligibility: Migrant students with excellent academic records
* Deadline: January 15, 2027
* Official URL: https://you.ubc.ca/administration/scholarships-awards/presidents-entrance-scholarship/
### International Major Entrance Scholarship
* Amount: up to $40,000
* Eligibility: Migrant students with excellent academic records
* Deadline: January 15, 2027
* Official URL: https://you.ubc.ca/administration/scholarships-awards/international-major-entrance-scholarship/
### Donald A. Wehrung International Student Award
* Amount: up to $50,000
* Eligibility: Migrant students from war-torn countries
* Deadline: January 15, 2027
* Official URL: https://you.ubc.ca/administration/scholarships-awards/donald-a-wehrung-international-student-award/
### Karen McKellin International Leader of Tomorrow Award
* Amount: up to $25,000
* Eligibility: Migrant students with excellent leadership skills
* Deadline: January 15, 2027
* Official URL: https://you.ubc.ca/administration/scholarships-awards/karen-mckellin-international-leader-tomorrow-award/

## Application Steps
To apply for these scholarships, follow these steps:
1. Check the eligibility criteria for each scholarship
2. Review the [scholarship deadlines](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/deadlines) to ensure timely submission
3. Prepare the required documents, including transcripts and language proficiency test results
4. Submit the application through the University of British Columbia's online portal
5. Ensure all required fields are completed and supporting documents are uploaded
6. Review and submit the application
7. Track the application status through the online portal
8. Receive and review the scholarship offer, if selected

## Documents Checklist
The following documents are required for scholarship applications:
* Transcripts from previous academic institutions
* Language proficiency test results (e.g. IELTS, TOEFL)
* Passport and study permit (if applicable)
* Proof of financial support (if applicable)
* Personal statement and/or essay (depending on the scholarship)
