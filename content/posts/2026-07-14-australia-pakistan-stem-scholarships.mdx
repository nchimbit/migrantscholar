# AusScholar
The Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarships are available to Pakistani students in STEM fields at the University of Sydney, with a funding amount of up to AUD 28,597 per annum.

## Who Qualifies
Pakistani students who are applying for a Master's or PhD degree in STEM fields at the University of Sydney may be eligible for the Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarships. To be considered, students must meet the eligibility criteria, which includes holding a valid visa, such as a Student visa (subclass 500) or a Research visa (subclass 402). Students who hold a Temporary Graduate visa (subclass 485) or a Bridging visa may also be eligible.
