## Introduction to Canada's International Student Scholarships
Canada's International Student Scholarships for migrant and refugee students provide a chance to pursue higher education in a welcoming and diverse environment. The Canadian government and various universities offer these scholarships to support undergraduate and postgraduate students from around the world. With a range of scholarship options available, migrant and refugee students can now achieve their academic goals in Canada.

## Who Qualifies
Migrant and refugee students who hold specific visa categories can apply for these scholarships. The qualifying visa categories include:
* Study Permit
* Temporary Resident Visa
* Permanent Resident Visa
* Refugee Claimant
* Protected Person
* Convention Refugee

## Available Scholarships
Canada offers numerous scholarships for migrant and refugee students. Here are five notable ones:
### University of Toronto Scholarship
The University of Toronto Scholarship provides coverage of up to $50,000 per year. To be eligible, students must have a study permit, demonstrate financial need, and maintain a minimum GPA of 3.7. The deadline for application is January 15, 2027. For more information, visit https://www.utoronto.ca.
### McGill University Scholarship
The McGill University Scholarship offers coverage of up to $30,000 per year. To be eligible, students must have a temporary resident visa, demonstrate academic excellence, and participate in community service. The deadline for application is March 1, 2027. For more information, visit https://www.mcgill.ca.
### University of British Columbia Scholarship
The University of British Columbia Scholarship provides coverage of up to $20,000 per year. To be eligible, students must have a study permit, demonstrate financial need, and maintain a minimum GPA of 3.5. The deadline for application is February 15, 2027. For more information, visit https://www.ubc.ca.
### York University Scholarship
The York University Scholarship offers coverage of up to $25,000 per year. To be eligible, students must have a protected person status, demonstrate academic excellence, and participate in community service. The deadline for application is April 1, 2027. For more information, visit https://www.yorku.ca.
### Carleton University Scholarship
The Carleton University Scholarship provides coverage of up to $15,000 per year. To be eligible, students must have a refugee claimant status, demonstrate financial need, and maintain a minimum GPA of 3.3. The deadline for application is May 1, 2027. For more information, visit https://www.carleton.ca.

## Application Steps
To apply for Canada's International Student Scholarships, migrant and refugee students should follow these steps:
1. Research and choose a scholarship that aligns with their academic goals and eligibility criteria.
2. Review the application requirements and deadlines for the chosen scholarship.
3. Prepare all necessary documents, including transcripts, language proficiency test results, and passport copies.
4. Submit the application through the official website or email address.
5. Wait for the application to be processed and reviewed by the scholarship committee.
6. Receive notification of the application outcome, which may include an interview or additional documentation.
7. Accept the scholarship offer and review the terms and conditions.
8. Begin the study program in Canada and maintain the scholarship eligibility criteria.

## Documents Checklist
