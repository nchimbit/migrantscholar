## Who Qualifies
Pakistani migrants who hold a temporary resident visa, a study permit, or a permanent resident visa are eligible to apply for the fully funded master's programs at Canadian universities. The visa categories include, but are not limited to, Federal Skilled Worker Program, Quebec Skilled Worker Program, and Provincial Nominee Program. To qualify, applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0 and meet the language proficiency requirements in English or French.
