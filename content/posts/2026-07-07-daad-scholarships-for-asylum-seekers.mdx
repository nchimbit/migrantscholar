## DAAD Scholarships for Asylum Seekers in Germany
Asylum seekers in Germany can apply for the DAAD Scholarships, which provide opportunities to pursue higher education and language courses in the country. 
The DAAD Scholarships are designed to support asylum seekers who are looking to further their education and build a better future for themselves in Germany.

## Who Qualifies
To qualify for the DAAD Scholarships, asylum seekers must meet certain criteria. The following visa categories are eligible:
* Visa category A (asylum seekers with a positive asylum decision)
* Visa category B (asylum seekers with a temporary residence permit)
* Visa category C (asylum seekers with a subsidiary protection status)
* Visa category D (asylum seekers with a tolerated stay permit)
* Visa category E (asylum seekers with a pending asylum application)

## Available Scholarships
Asylum seekers in Germany can apply for the following DAAD Scholarships:
### Scholarship 1: DAAD Helmut-Schmidt-Programme
The DAAD Helmut-Schmidt-Programme provides a monthly stipend of 750 euros, plus tuition fees and health insurance, to asylum seekers who are pursuing a master's degree in Germany. The eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, English language proficiency, and a strong academic record. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Scholarship 2: DAAD Development-Related Postgraduate Courses
The DAAD Development-Related Postgraduate Courses provide a monthly stipend of 750 euros, plus tuition fees and health insurance, to asylum seekers who are pursuing a postgraduate degree in Germany. The eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, English language proficiency, and a strong academic record. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Scholarship 3: DAAD Research Grants
The DAAD Research Grants provide a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros, plus tuition fees and health insurance, to asylum seekers who are pursuing a research project in Germany. The eligibility criteria include a master's degree, English language proficiency, and a strong academic record. The deadline for application is September 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Scholarship 4: DAAD University Summer Courses
The DAAD University Summer Courses provide a monthly stipend of 500 euros, plus tuition fees and health insurance, to asylum seekers who are pursuing a summer course in Germany. The eligibility criteria include a high school diploma, English language proficiency, and a strong academic record. The deadline for application is May 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Scholarship 5: DAAD Language and Short Courses
The DAAD Language and Short Courses provide a monthly stipend of 500 euros, plus tuition fees and health insurance, to asylum seekers who are pursuing a language or short course in Germany. The eligibility criteria include a high school diploma, English language proficiency, and a strong academic record. The deadline for application is April 2026. For more information, visit the official website at https://www.daad.de/en/.

## Application Steps
To apply for the DAAD Scholarships, asylum seekers must follow these steps:
1. Choose the scholarship program that best fits their needs and qualifications.
2. Check the eligibility criteria and ensure they meet all the requirements.
3. Gather all the necessary documents, including their visa, passport, and academic transcripts.
4. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline.
5. Upload all the required documents, including their language proficiency certificate and academic record.
6. Submit their application and wait for the selection committee to review their application.
7. If selected, asylum seekers will receive an email with instructions on how to proceed with their scholarship.
8. Finalize their scholarship by signing a contract and providing any additional documents required.

## Documents Checklist
Asylum seekers must submit the following documents to apply for the DAAD Scholarships:
* Visa category A, B, C, D, or E
* Passport
* Academic transcripts
* Language proficiency certificate
* Proof of financial need
* Motivation letter
* Recommendation letter
* Proof of admission to a German university
