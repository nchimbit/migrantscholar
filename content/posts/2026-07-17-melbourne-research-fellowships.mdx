The McKenzie Postdoctoral Fellowship Scheme exists for Canadian and US researchers, offering up to AUD 91,333 per year for a maximum of two years to conduct research at the University of Melbourne, Australia.

## Who Qualifies
The McKenzie Postdoctoral Fellowship Scheme is open to international researchers, including those from Canada and the US, who hold a valid visa, such as a Temporary Activity visa (subclass 408) or a Temporary Graduate visa (subclass 485). Researchers from Canada and the US can apply for this fellowship, which is also open to [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) holders.

## Available Scholarships
The following are some of the scholarships available to Canadian and US researchers at the University of Melbourne:
### McKenzie Postdoctoral Fellowship Scheme
* Amount: up to AUD 91,333 per year
* Eligibility: International researchers, including those from Canada and the US
* Deadline: February and August each year
* Official URL: https://academicjobsonline.org/ajo/fellowship/University%20of%20Melbourne
### Melbourne Research Scholarship
* Amount: up to AUD 30,600 per year
* Eligibility: International researchers, including those from Canada and the US
* Deadline: October and May each year
* Official URL: https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/awards/melbourne-research-scholarship
### Australia Awards Scholarship
* Amount: up to AUD 30,000 per year
* Eligibility: International researchers, including those from Canada and the US
* Deadline: April and October each year
* Official URL: https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/
### University of Melbourne Graduate Research Scholarship
* Amount: up to AUD 30,600 per year
* Eligibility: International researchers, including those from Canada and the US
* Deadline: October and May each year
* Official URL: https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/awards/university-of-melbourne-graduate-research-scholarship
### Sir John and Lady Higgins Research Fellowship
* Amount: up to AUD 10,000 per year
* Eligibility: International researchers, including those from Canada and the US
* Deadline: March and September each year
* Official URL: https://higgins-menzies.org/fellowship/
