## Available Scholarships
### Fulbright Scholarship
The Fulbright Scholarship provides up to $100,000 in funding for Indian migrants pursuing graduate studies in the USA. The eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and a minimum of 3 years of work experience. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.iie.org/Programs/Fulbright-US-Student-Program](https://www.iie.org/Programs/Fulbright-US-Student-Program).

### Google Lime Scholarship
The Google Lime Scholarship provides up to $10,000 in funding for Indian migrants pursuing undergraduate or graduate studies in computer science or a related field. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 3.0, proficiency in English, and a disability. The deadline for application is December 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.limeconnect.com/programs/page/google-lime-scholarship](https://www.limeconnect.com/programs/page/google-lime-scholarship).

### Microsoft Scholarship
The Microsoft Scholarship provides up to $20,000 in funding for Indian migrants pursuing undergraduate or graduate studies in computer science or a related field. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 3.0, proficiency in English, and a demonstrated interest in technology. The deadline for application is January 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/diversity/programs/microsoft-scholarships](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/diversity/programs/microsoft-scholarships).

### Intel Scholarship
The Intel Scholarship provides up to $10,000 in funding for Indian migrants pursuing undergraduate or graduate studies in computer science or a related field. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 3.0, proficiency in English, and a demonstrated interest in technology. The deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/education/intel-scholarship.html](https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/education/intel-scholarship.html).

### Tata Scholarship
The Tata Scholarship provides up to $25,000 in funding for Indian migrants pursuing graduate studies at Cornell University. The eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and a minimum of 3 years of work experience. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.cornell.edu/admissions/financial-aid/scholarships/tata-scholarship](https://www.cornell.edu/admissions/financial-aid/scholarships/tata-scholarship).
