## Who Qualifies
International students from various countries are eligible to apply for graduate scholarships in Turkey, including those with visa categories such as student visa, research visa, or cultural exchange visa. The eligibility criteria may vary depending on the specific scholarship programme and the institution offering the scholarship. Students can check the official website of the scholarship provider or the Turkish embassy in their home country for more information on eligibility criteria and visa requirements.
