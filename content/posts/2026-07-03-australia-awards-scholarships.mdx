## Introduction to Australia Awards Scholarships
The Australia Awards Scholarships are a great opportunity for refugee and asylum seeker students from Germany and other countries to study in Australia. These scholarships are designed to support students who are unable to access higher education in their home country due to conflict or other circumstances. By providing full tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits, the Australia Awards Scholarships enable refugee and asylum seeker students to pursue their academic goals in a safe and supportive environment.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Australia Awards Scholarships, you must hold a valid Australian visa in one of the following categories:
* Permanent (Humanitarian) visa (subclass 200, 201, 202, 203, 204, 206, 207, 208, 209, 210, 211, 212, 213, 215, 216, 217, 300)
* Temporary Protection visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise visa (subclass 790)
* Temporary (Humanitarian Concern) visa (subclass 449)
* Bridging visa E (subclass 050 or 051)
