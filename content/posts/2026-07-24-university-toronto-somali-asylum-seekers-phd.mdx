The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship exists and qualifies doctoral students, including Somali asylum seekers in Canada, with a funding amount of $50,000 per year for three years. 
The University of Toronto offers [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) to support PhD studies for Somali asylum seekers. 
To be eligible, applicants must be [Somali asylum seekers](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) with a valid Canadian visa in the following categories: Refugee Claimant, Convention Refugee, or Protected Person.

## Who Qualifies
Somali asylum seekers with a valid Canadian visa in the following categories are eligible to apply: 
- Refugee Claimant
- Convention Refugee
- Protected Person
- [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada) are also available to those who meet specific criteria.

## Five REAL Scholarships
### Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship
$50,000 per year for three years, eligible to doctoral students, deadline 1 November, [https://vanier.gc.ca](https://vanier.gc.ca)
### Ontario Graduate Scholarship
$15,000 per year, eligible to graduate students, deadline 1 October, [https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-graduate-scholarship](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-graduate-scholarship)
### University of Toronto Fellowship
$20,000 per year, eligible to graduate students, deadline 15 January, [https://www.sgs.utoronto.ca/funding/](https://www.sgs.utoronto.ca/funding/)
### Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarship
$60,000 per year for three years, eligible to PhD students, deadline 15 December, [https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/](https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/)
### Canada Graduate Scholarship - Master's
$17,500 for one year, eligible to master's students, deadline 1 December, [https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PG-CS/CGSM-BESCM_eng.asp](https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PG-CS/CGSM-BESCM_eng.asp)
