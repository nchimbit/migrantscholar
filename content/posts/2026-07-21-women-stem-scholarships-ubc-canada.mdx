The University of British Columbia, Canada offers the Affiliated Fellowships Master's Program, which qualifies female international students, including migrants and refugees, pursuing Master's programs in STEM fields, with funding amount of $175 to $31,500.

## Who Qualifies
Female migrants and refugees with a valid study visa, including those under the Student Direct Stream, Student Partnership Program, or with a temporary resident visa, can apply for women-only scholarships at the University of British Columbia, Canada. Eligible visa categories include, but are not limited to, SDV, SPP, TRV, and PGWP.

### Women in STEM Scholarships
The following are five real scholarships available to female migrants and refugees pursuing Master's programs in STEM at the University of British Columbia, Canada:
1. **Google Anita Borg Memorial Scholarship**: Amount $10,000, Eligibility female students in computer science, Deadline varies, [https://www.google.com/anitaborg](https://www.google.com/anitaborg)
2. **L'Oréal-UNESCO Awards for Women in Science**: Amount $40,000, Eligibility female students in life sciences, Deadline varies, [https://www.auf.org.au/](https://www.loreal.com)
3. **IBM Ph.D. Fellowship Awards**: Amount $10,000 to $20,000, Eligibility female students in computer science, Deadline May, [https://www.research.ibm.com/university/awards/fellowships.html](https://www.research.ibm.com/university/awards/fellowships.html)
4. **PEO International Peace Scholarship**: Amount $12,500, Eligibility female international students, Deadline varies, [https://www.peointernational.org/ips-application](https://www.peointernational.org/ips-application)
5. **Microsoft Research Ph.D. Fellowship**: Amount $175,000, Eligibility female students in computer science, Deadline October, [https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/academic-program/microsoft-research-phd-fellowship/](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/academic-program/microsoft-research-phd-fellowship/)
