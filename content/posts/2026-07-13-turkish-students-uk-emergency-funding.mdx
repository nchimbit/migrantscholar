The British Council Scholarship for Turkish students qualifies Turkish nationals who are graduate or undergraduate students with a funding amount of up to £25,000. Turkish students in the UK can apply for emergency funding to support their education in times of financial hardship. The UK offers various hardship funds and scholarships to support students who are facing financial difficulties. 

## Who Qualifies
Turkish students who hold a valid visa, such as a Tier 4 (General) student visa, Tier 4 (Child) student visa, or a Short-term study visa, can apply for emergency funding. Those with a spouse or partner visa, or dependents of a points-based system migrant, may also be eligible. To qualify, students must demonstrate that they are experiencing financial hardship due to unforeseen circumstances, such as illness, bereavement, or loss of sponsor support.

The British Council Scholarship, worth up to £25,000, is available to Turkish graduate or undergraduate students who are experiencing financial hardship. Eligibility includes being a Turkish national, having a valid UK visa, and being enrolled in a UK university. Deadline for application varies. For more information, visit https://www.britishcouncil.org.
