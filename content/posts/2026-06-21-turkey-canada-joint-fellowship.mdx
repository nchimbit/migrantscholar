## Introduction to Turkey and Canada's Joint Fellowship
The Turkey and Canada Joint Fellowship for Migrant and Refugee Researchers is a unique opportunity for postdoctoral researchers who are migrants or refugees to pursue their research interests in Turkey and Canada. This fellowship aims to support researchers who have been displaced due to conflict, persecution, or other circumstances and provide them with a platform to continue their academic pursuits. The fellowship is a result of a collaboration between the governments of Turkey and Canada, and it is designed to promote academic excellence and diversity.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Turkey and Canada Joint Fellowship, applicants must meet specific visa requirements. The following visa categories are eligible to apply:
* Refugee visa
* Asylum visa
* Protection visa
* Humanitarian visa
* Temporary resident visa with a study or work permit
* Permanent resident visa with a study or work permit
