# Canada Aid
The University of Toronto's Lester B. Pearson International Student Scholarship exists and qualifies asylum seekers from Turkey, including those with a study permit under the Student Direct Stream, with funding up to $50,000 per year. 
Asylum seekers in Canada can benefit from various [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada) to support their higher education goals. 
The University of Toronto is one of the premier institutions offering [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) to deserving students.

## Who Qualifies
Asylum seekers from Turkey with a study permit under the Student Direct Stream, as well as those with a visitor visa or a work visa, can apply for Canadian university bursaries. 
Visa categories that may be eligible include:
- Study permit under the Student Direct Stream
- Visitor visa with a study permit
- Work visa with a study permit
- Refugee status or asylum claimant

## University Bursaries
### The University of Toronto's Lester B. Pearson International Student Scholarship
- Amount: $50,000 per year
- Eligibility: Asylum seekers from Turkey with a study permit under the Student Direct Stream
- Deadline: January 15th
- Official URL: https://www.utoronto.ca/academics/awards
### University of British Columbia International Student Scholarship
- Amount: $20,000 per year
- Eligibility: Asylum seekers from Turkey with a study permit under the Student Direct Stream
- Deadline: December 1st
- Official URL: https://you.ubc.ca/financial-planning/scholarships-awards-international-students

- Amount: $60,000 per year
- Eligibility: Asylum seekers from Turkey with a [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) offer

### McGill University's Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program
- Amount: $50,000 per year

### York University's International Student Scholarship
- Amount: $20,000 per year
- Eligibility: Asylum seekers from Turkey with a study permit under the Student Direct Stream
