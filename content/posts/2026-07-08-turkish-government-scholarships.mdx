## Turkish Government Scholarships for Asylum Seekers
The Turkish government offers scholarships to asylum seekers pursuing higher education in Turkey, providing them with the opportunity to access quality education and build a better future.

## Who Qualifies
Asylum seekers who hold certain visa categories are eligible to apply for these scholarships. The qualifying visa categories include:
* Conditional Refugee
* Refugee
* Secondary Protection
* Humanitarian Residence
* Stateless Person

## Available Scholarships
###Turkey Scholarships for Asylum Seekers
The Turkey Scholarships for Asylum Seekers covers full tuition fees, a monthly stipend of 700 TL, and accommodation. To be eligible, applicants must be asylum seekers with a valid visa, have a strong academic record, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is February 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/](https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/).

### Abdullah Gül University Scholarship
The Abdullah Gül University Scholarship provides full tuition fees, a monthly stipend of 1000 TL, and accommodation. To be eligible, applicants must be asylum seekers with a valid visa, have a strong academic record, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://aguniversity.edu.tr/](https://aguniversity.edu.tr/).

### Kadir Has University Scholarship
The Kadir Has University Scholarship covers 50% of tuition fees, a monthly stipend of 500 TL, and accommodation. To be eligible, applicants must be asylum seekers with a valid visa, have a strong academic record, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.khas.edu.tr/](https://www.khas.edu.tr/).

### Yasar University Scholarship
The Yasar University Scholarship provides full tuition fees, a monthly stipend of 800 TL, and accommodation. To be eligible, applicants must be asylum seekers with a valid visa, have a strong academic record, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is January 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.yasar.edu.tr/](https://www.yasar.edu.tr/).

### Izmir University of Economics Scholarship
The Izmir University of Economics Scholarship covers 75% of tuition fees, a monthly stipend of 600 TL, and accommodation. To be eligible, applicants must be asylum seekers with a valid visa, have a strong academic record, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is December 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.ieu.edu.tr/](https://www.ieu.edu.tr/).

## Application Steps
1. Check the eligibility criteria for the scholarship you are interested in.
2. Gather all required documents, including academic transcripts, language proficiency certificates, and a valid visa.
3. Choose the program you want to apply for and check the admission requirements.
4. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline.
5. Pay the application fee, if required.
6. Submit all required documents to the university or institution.
7. Wait for the application result, which may take several weeks or months.
8. If accepted, apply for a student visa and register for the program.
