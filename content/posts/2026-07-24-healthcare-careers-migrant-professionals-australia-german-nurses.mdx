# Germany to Aus
The Australian Government's Department of Home Affairs offers the Skilled Independent visa (subclass 189) to German-trained nurses, providing funding of up to AUD 50,000 to support their migration and career development in Australia.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for healthcare careers in Australia, German-trained nurses must hold a valid visa, including the Skilled Independent visa (subclass 189), Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190), or Skilled Regional (provisional) visa (subclass 489). They must also meet the required standards for nursing practice in Australia, including registration with the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA).

### Endeavour Scholarships and Fellowships
The Endeavour Scholarships and Fellowships offer funding of up to AUD 140,000 for German-trained nurses to pursue a PhD or Master's degree in Australia, with eligibility for international students, a deadline of June 15, and can be found at https://www.australiaawards.gov.au.

The International Postgraduate Research Scholarships (IPRS) offer funding of up to AUD 28,597 per year for German-trained nurses to pursue a PhD in Australia, with eligibility for international students, a deadline of August 31, and can be found at https://www.education.gov.au.
