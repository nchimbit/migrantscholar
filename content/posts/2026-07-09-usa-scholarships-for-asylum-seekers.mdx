## Partially Funded Awards for Undergraduate Studies in the USA
Asylum seekers and refugees in the USA can apply for partially funded awards to pursue their undergraduate studies, with several scholarships available to support their educational pursuits.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these partially funded awards, asylum seekers and refugees must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Asylee (AS)
* Refugee (RE)
* Special Immigrant Visa (SIV)
* Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
* Deferred Enforced Departure (DED)
* Withholding of Removal
* Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA)
