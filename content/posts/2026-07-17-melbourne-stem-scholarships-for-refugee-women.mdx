# STEM Scholarships
The [Australia Awards Scholarships](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/) exist for women refugees, offering up to AUD 30,000 per year for women from refugee backgrounds pursuing STEM fields at the University of Melbourne.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the University of Melbourne STEM Scholarships, women must hold a valid Australian visa, including the Permanent Humanitarian Visa, Safe Haven Enterprise Visa, or Temporary Protection Visa. The scholarships are designed to support women who have been displaced due to conflict, persecution, or natural disasters and are now resides in Australia.

## Available Scholarships
### University of Melbourne Graduate Research Scholarships
The University of Melbourne Graduate Research Scholarships offer up to AUD 30,000 per year for women pursuing a graduate research degree in STEM fields. Eligibility includes women from refugee backgrounds holding a valid Australian visa. The deadline is October 31, 2026, and the official URL is https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/graduate-research-scholarships.

### Australia Awards Scholarships
The Australia Awards Scholarships provide up to AUD 30,000 per year for women from refugee backgrounds pursuing undergraduate or postgraduate studies in STEM fields. Eligibility includes women holding a valid Australian visa, and the deadline is April 30, 2026. The official URL is https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/.

### Westpac Future Leaders Scholarship
The Westpac Future Leaders Scholarship offers up to AUD 120,000 over three years for women pursuing postgraduate studies in STEM fields. Eligibility includes women from refugee backgrounds holding a valid Australian visa, and the deadline is September 5, 2026. The official URL is https://www.westpac.com.au/about-westpac/westpac-foundation/scholarships/future-leaders-scholarship/.

### Women in STEM Scholarship
The Women in STEM Scholarship provides up to AUD 5,000 per year for women pursuing undergraduate or postgraduate studies in STEM fields. Eligibility includes women from refugee backgrounds holding a valid Australian visa, and the deadline is August 15, 2026. The official URL is https://www.unimelb.edu.au/scholarships/women-in-stem-scholarship.

### The General Sir John Monash Foundation Scholarships
The General Sir John Monash Foundation Scholarships offer up to AUD 70,000 per year for women pursuing postgraduate studies in STEM fields. Eligibility includes women from refugee backgrounds holding a valid Australian visa, and the deadline is July 15, 2026. The official URL is https://www.monashfoundation.org/.
