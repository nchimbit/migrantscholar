## Introduction to DAAD Scholarship for Refugee Students in Germany
The DAAD Scholarship for refugee students in Germany is a prestigious award that provides opportunities for refugees to pursue language courses, undergraduate, and graduate studies in Germany. Refugee students who are interested in applying for the DAAD Scholarship can expect to receive full tuition fees plus a monthly living stipend. The scholarship is a great opportunity for refugees to gain access to quality education and build a better future for themselves.

## Who Qualifies for the DAAD Scholarship
To be eligible for the DAAD Scholarship, refugee students must meet certain criteria, including:
* Having a refugee status in Germany with a valid visa category of Aufenthaltstitel (residence permit) pursuant to section 24 or 25 para 1 of the Residence Act
* Having a visa category of Duldung (tolerated stay) pursuant to section 60a para 1 sentence 1 of the Residence Act
* Having a visa category of Aufenthaltsgestattung (temporary suspension of deportation) pursuant to section 60b of the Residence Act
* Meeting the language requirements for the chosen course of study
* Meeting the academic requirements for the chosen course of study
