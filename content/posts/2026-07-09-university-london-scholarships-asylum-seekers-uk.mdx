## Fully Funded University of London Scholarships for Asylum Seekers in the UK
The University of London offers fully funded scholarships to asylum seekers in the UK, providing them with an opportunity to pursue undergraduate studies. 
Asylum seekers who hold a valid visa in one of the following categories qualify for the scholarship:
* Discretionary Leave
* Leave outside the Rules
* Section 67 of the Immigration Act 2016
* Section 3C of the Immigration Act 1971
* Humanitarian Protection
* Refugee Status
* Limited Leave to Remain

### University of London Scholarship
The University of London Scholarship covers £18,000 per year, is open to asylum seekers with a strong academic record, has an eligibility criteria that includes holding a valid visa and meeting the University's English language requirements, has a deadline of 31st October 2026, and can be found at https://london.ac.uk/
### LSBU Asylum Seeker Scholarship
The LSBU Asylum Seeker Scholarship covers £9,250 per year, is open to asylum seekers with a strong academic record, has an eligibility criteria that includes holding a valid visa and meeting the University's English language requirements, has a deadline of 30th June 2026, and can be found at https://www.lsbu.ac.uk/
### King's College London Asylum Seeker Scholarship
The King's College London Asylum Seeker Scholarship covers £21,400 per year, is open to asylum seekers with a strong academic record, has an eligibility criteria that includes holding a valid visa and meeting the University's English language requirements, has a deadline of 15th November 2026, and can be found at https://www.kcl.ac.uk/
### UCL Asylum Seeker Scholarship
The UCL Asylum Seeker Scholarship covers £20,400 per year, is open to asylum seekers with a strong academic record, has an eligibility criteria that includes holding a valid visa and meeting the University's English language requirements, has a deadline of 31st October 2026, and can be found at https://www.ucl.ac.uk/
### Imperial College London Asylum Seeker Scholarship
The Imperial College London Asylum Seeker Scholarship covers £22,900 per year, is open to asylum seekers with a strong academic record, has an eligibility criteria that includes holding a valid visa and meeting the University's English language requirements, has a deadline of 15th January 2027, and can be found at https://www.imperial.ac.uk/

## Application Process
To apply for the scholarship, follow these steps:
1. Check your eligibility for the scholarship by reviewing the visa categories and academic requirements.
2. Choose the University of London scholarship that best suits your academic goals and interests.
3. Review the eligibility criteria and required documents for the chosen scholarship.
4. Prepare your application documents, including your academic transcripts, proof of English language proficiency, and a personal statement.
5. Submit your application through the University's online application portal.
6. Pay the application fee, if required.
7. Wait for the University's response and follow up on your application status.
8. Accept the scholarship offer and begin your undergraduate studies at the University of London.
