## Introduction to Fully Funded UK Chevening Scholarship
The UK Chevening Scholarship is a fully funded opportunity for migrant students, refugees, and asylum seekers to pursue a Master's degree in the United Kingdom. This prestigious scholarship covers full tuition fees, a monthly living allowance, and other expenses for the duration of the program, typically one year. By applying for the Chevening Scholarship, eligible students can achieve their academic goals without financial burden.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the UK Chevening Scholarship, migrant students must hold a valid visa under one of the following categories:
* Tier 4 (General) student visa
* Tier 2 (General) work visa
* Family visa
* Refugee or asylum seeker status
* Humanitarian protection status
* Discretionary leave to remain
* Leave outside the rules

## Available Scholarships
The following five scholarships are available for migrant students to apply:
### Chevening Scholarship
The Chevening Scholarship covers £18,000 to £20,000 per year, depending on the course and institution. Eligible students must have a Bachelor's degree, two years of work experience, and meet the English language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is typically in November of each year. For more information, visit the [official Chevening website](https://www.chevening.org/).
