The Chevening Scholarship is available to Pakistani migrants in the UK, providing full funding of up to £18,000 for a master's degree in a STEM field. 
Pakistani migrants who hold a valid UK visa, including Tier 2, Tier 4, and Tier 5, may be eligible for this scholarship.
This scholarship aims to support the development of future leaders in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) by providing opportunities for master's study in the UK.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Chevening Scholarship and other STEM scholarships in the UK, Pakistani migrants must hold a valid UK visa, including:
* Tier 2: General work visa
* Tier 4: Student visa
* Tier 5: Temporary worker visa
Additionally, applicants must meet the eligibility criteria for each individual scholarship, which may include academic requirements, language proficiency, and relevant work experience.
For [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) in STEM fields, applicants typically need to have a master's degree and research experience.
For [scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees), applicants need to have refugee status in the UK.
