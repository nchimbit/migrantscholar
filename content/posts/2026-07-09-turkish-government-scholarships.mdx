## Turkish Government Scholarships for International Students from Migrant Backgrounds
The Turkish Government Scholarship is available for international students from migrant backgrounds to pursue undergraduate and graduate studies in Turkey.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Turkish Government Scholarship, applicants must be from one of the following visa categories:
* Family residence permit
* Student residence permit
* Humanitarian residence permit
* Refugee residence permit
* Stateless person residence permit
* Other residence permits that are specifically granted for education purposes

## Available Scholarships
### Turkey Scholarships
The Turkey Scholarships program provides full tuition coverage, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 1600 TL for undergraduate students, 1800 TL for master's students, and 2000 TL for PhD students. Eligible applicants must have a strong academic record and meet the language proficiency requirements. The application deadline is February 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/](https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/).

### Kadir Has University Scholarship
The Kadir Has University Scholarship offers a full scholarship covering tuition fees and living expenses, with a monthly stipend of 1500 TL for undergraduate students and 2000 TL for graduate students. Eligible applicants must be international students with a strong academic record and a valid passport. The application deadline is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.khas.edu.tr/en](https://www.khas.edu.tr/en).

### Bilkent University Scholarship
The Bilkent University Scholarship provides a full scholarship covering tuition fees and living expenses, with a monthly stipend of 1200 TL for undergraduate students and 1800 TL for graduate students. Eligible applicants must be international students with a strong academic record and a valid passport. The application deadline is October 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.bilkent.edu.tr/](https://www.bilkent.edu.tr/).

### Ankara University Scholarship
The Ankara University Scholarship offers a partial scholarship covering 50% of tuition fees and living expenses, with a monthly stipend of 1000 TL for undergraduate students and 1500 TL for graduate students. Eligible applicants must be international students with a strong academic record and a valid passport. The application deadline is September 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.ankara.edu.tr/en](https://www.ankara.edu.tr/en).

### Istanbul Aydın University Scholarship
The Istanbul Aydın University Scholarship provides a full scholarship covering tuition fees and living expenses, with a monthly stipend of 1500 TL for undergraduate students and 2000 TL for graduate students. Eligible applicants must be international students with a strong academic record and a valid passport. The application deadline is August 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.aydin.edu.tr/en](https://www.aydin.edu.tr/en).

## Application Steps
1. Research the available scholarships and their eligibility criteria.
2. Check the language proficiency requirements for your chosen program.
3. Prepare the required documents, including transcripts, diplomas, and language proficiency certificates.
4. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline.
5. Pay the application fee, if required.
6. Take the language proficiency test, if required.
