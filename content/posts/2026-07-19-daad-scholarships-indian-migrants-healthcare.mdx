# DAAD Scholar
The DAAD Scholarships for master's studies in healthcare are available to Indian migrants, with a funding amount of up to €750 per month. 

## Who Qualifies
Indian migrants with a degree in a healthcare-related field and holding a student visa, job seeker visa, or blue card visa can apply for these scholarships. They must also demonstrate excellent academic performance and language skills in English or German.

## Available Scholarships
There are several scholarships available to Indian migrants in Germany, including:

Eligibility: Indian migrants with a degree in a healthcare-related field

Amount: €750 per month
Eligibility: Indian migrants with a degree in a healthcare-related field
Deadline: varies depending on the course

Amount: €750 per month
