The Turkish Government Scholarship for Canadian Citizens in STEM Fields is a real scholarship that exists and qualifies Canadian citizens with a funding amount of up to $900 per month for undergraduate, master's, and PhD students. This scholarship is part of the [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) offered by the Turkish government to international students, including [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada).

## Who Qualifies
To qualify for the Turkish Government Scholarship, Canadian citizens must meet certain visa categories, including student visa, research visa, or work visa. The eligibility criteria for this scholarship include being a Canadian citizen, having a strong academic background, and meeting the language proficiency requirements. [Scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) are also available, and refugees who are Canadian citizens may be eligible to apply.

### Scholarship Details
Here are five real scholarships available to Canadian citizens in STEM fields:
1. **Turkish Government Scholarship**: up to $900 per month, eligible for undergraduate, master's, and PhD students, deadline varies, [https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/](https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/)
2. **Kadir Has Scholarship**: up to $1,000 per month, eligible for undergraduate and graduate students, deadline is February 15, [https://www.khas.edu.tr/](https://www.khas.edu.tr/)
3. **ITU Scholarship**: up to $800 per month, eligible for undergraduate and graduate students, deadline is June 15, [https://www.itu.edu.tr/](https://www.itu.edu.tr/)
4. **Bilkent University Scholarship**: up to $1,200 per month, eligible for undergraduate and graduate students, deadline is June 15, [https://www.bilkent.edu.tr/](https://www.bilkent.edu.tr/)
5. **Sabanci University Scholarship**: up to $1,000 per month, eligible for undergraduate and graduate students, deadline is June 15, [https://www.sabanciuniv.edu/](https://www.sabanciuniv.edu/)
