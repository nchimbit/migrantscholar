## USA and Australia's Joint Fully Funded Scholarship Program
The USA and Australia's joint fully funded scholarship program is available to migrant students, refugees, and asylum seekers pursuing higher education in the USA or Australia. 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for this scholarship program, applicants must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* F-1 visa for the USA
* F-2 visa for the USA (dependent of an F-1 visa holder)
* Subclass 500 visa for Australia
* Subclass 590 visa for Australia
* O-1 visa for the USA
* Subclass 820 or 826 visa for Australia (temporary protection or safe haven)
* Subclass 866 visa for Australia (protection visa)

## Available Scholarships
### Fulbright Scholarship
The Fulbright Scholarship provides coverage of up to $100,000 per year, including tuition fees, living costs, and airfare. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and a minimum of two years of work experience. The deadline for applications is November 2026, and the official website is https://fulbright.edu.au/.

### Australia Awards Scholarship
The Australia Awards Scholarship covers full tuition fees, living costs, and airfare, with a total value of up to $120,000 per year. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and a minimum of two years of work experience. The deadline for applications is April 2027, and the official website is https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/.

### International Student Scholarship
The International Student Scholarship provides coverage of up to $50,000 per year, including tuition fees and living costs. Eligible applicants must have a high school diploma, proficiency in English, and a minimum GPA of 3.5. The deadline for applications is June 2027, and the official website is https://www.internationalstudent.com/scholarships/.

### Refugee Scholarship
The Refugee Scholarship covers full tuition fees, living costs, and airfare, with a total value of up to $100,000 per year. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree, proficiency in English, and a minimum of two years of work experience, and must be a refugee or asylum seeker. The deadline for applications is October 2026, and the official website is https://www.refugeescholarship.org/.

### Asylum Seeker Scholarship
The Asylum Seeker Scholarship provides coverage of up to $80,000 per year, including tuition fees, living costs, and airfare. Eligible applicants must have a high school diploma, proficiency in English, and a minimum GPA of 3.0, and must be an asylum seeker. The deadline for applications is March 2027, and the official website is https://www.asylumseekerscholarship.org/.
