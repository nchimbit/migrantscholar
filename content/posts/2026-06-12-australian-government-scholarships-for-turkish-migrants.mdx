## Introduction to Australian Government Fully Funded Scholarships for Turkish Migrant Students
The Australian Government offers fully funded scholarships to Turkish migrant students pursuing Masters and PhD degree programs in Australia. These scholarships provide an excellent opportunity for Turkish migrant students to further their education and career prospects in Australia. By offering full funding, the Australian Government aims to support the educational and professional development of international students, including those from Turkey.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Australian Government fully funded scholarships, Turkish migrant students must hold a valid visa in one of the following categories:
* Skilled Independent visa (subclass 189)
* Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)
* Skilled Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 491)
* Temporary Graduate visa (subclass 485)
* student visa (subclass 500)
Turkish migrant students who meet these visa requirements can apply for the scholarships, which are offered annually.
