# STEM Scholarships
The Melbourne School of Engineering Scholarships exist for female migrant students, offering up to $10,000 per year to pursue STEM fields at the University of Melbourne, Australia.

## Who Qualifies
Eligible applicants for these scholarships include female migrant students who hold a valid Australian visa, such as a Permanent Humanitarian Visa, a Temporary Protection Visa, or a Safe Haven Enterprise Visa, and are commencing their undergraduate or graduate studies in a STEM field at the University of Melbourne.

## Available Scholarships
Here are five real scholarships available to female migrant students pursuing STEM fields at the University of Melbourne:
### Melbourne School of Engineering Scholarships
* Amount: up to $10,000 per year
* Eligibility: female students commencing their undergraduate or graduate studies in a STEM field
* Deadline: varies
* Official URL: https://eng.unimelb.edu.au/study/scholarships
### Women in STEM Scholarship
* Amount: up to $5,000 per year
* Eligibility: female students pursuing a STEM field at the University of Melbourne
* Deadline: 31 March
* Official URL: https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/
### Faculty of Science Scholarships
* Amount: up to $10,000 per year
* Eligibility: students commencing their undergraduate or graduate studies in the Faculty of Science
* Deadline: varies
* Official URL: https://science.unimelb.edu.au/study/scholarships
### Melbourne Graduate Scholarship
* Amount: up to $30,000 per year
* Eligibility: graduate students pursuing a research degree at the University of Melbourne
* Deadline: 31 October
* Official URL: https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/
### Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship
* Amount: up to $28,597 per year
* Eligibility: graduate students pursuing a research degree at the University of Melbourne
* Deadline: 31 October
* Official URL: https://www.education.gov.au/australian-government-research-training-program

## Application Steps
Here are the steps to apply for these scholarships:
1. Choose your desired scholarship from the list of available scholarships.
2. Review the eligibility criteria and ensure you meet the requirements.
3. Prepare your documents, including transcripts, proof of English proficiency, and a personal statement.
4. Submit your application through the University of Melbourne's online portal.
5. Wait for the application to be processed and reviewed.
6. Receive notification of the outcome of your application.
7. Accept the scholarship offer and commence your studies at the University of Melbourne.
8. Maintain a high level of academic achievement to ensure ongoing scholarship funding.

## Documents Checklist
Ensure you have the following documents prepared when applying for these scholarships:
* Transcripts from previous academic institutions
* Proof of English proficiency, such as IELTS or TOEFL scores
* Personal statement outlining your academic and career goals
* Letters of recommendation from academic or professional referees
* Proof of Australian visa status
