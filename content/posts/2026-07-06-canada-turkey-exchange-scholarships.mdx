## Canada-Turkey Exchange Program for Undergraduate Students
The Canada-Turkey Exchange Program offers partially funded awards for refugee students pursuing an undergraduate degree in Canada. Refugee students who hold a valid Canadian study permit under specific visa categories are eligible to apply for these awards.

### Who Qualifies
The following refugee students are eligible to apply for the Canada-Turkey Exchange Program:
* Convention refugees
* Persons in need of protection
* Protected persons
* Refugee claimants
* Temporary resident permit holders
* Other visa categories as defined by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada

### Available Scholarships
Here are five scholarships available to refugee students under the Canada-Turkey Exchange Program:
### University of Toronto Scholarship
The University of Toronto Scholarship provides $10,000 to $20,000 per year for four years. To be eligible, applicants must be admitted to an undergraduate program at the University of Toronto, have a valid Canadian study permit, and demonstrate financial need. The deadline to apply is November 15, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.utoronto.ca/](https://www.utoronto.ca/).
### York University Scholarship
The York University Scholarship offers $5,000 to $10,000 per year for four years. Eligible applicants must be admitted to an undergraduate program at York University, have a valid Canadian study permit, and demonstrate academic excellence. The deadline to apply is October 15, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.yorku.ca/](https://www.yorku.ca/).
### McGill University Scholarship
The McGill University Scholarship provides $8,000 to $15,000 per year for four years. To be eligible, applicants must be admitted to an undergraduate program at McGill University, have a valid Canadian study permit, and demonstrate financial need. The deadline to apply is November 1, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.mcgill.ca/](https://www.mcgill.ca/).
### University of British Columbia Scholarship
The University of British Columbia Scholarship offers $6,000 to $12,000 per year for four years. Eligible applicants must be admitted to an undergraduate program at the University of British Columbia, have a valid Canadian study permit, and demonstrate academic excellence. The deadline to apply is October 31, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.ubc.ca/](https://www.ubc.ca/).
### Carleton University Scholarship
The Carleton University Scholarship provides $4,000 to $8,000 per year for four years. To be eligible, applicants must be admitted to an undergraduate program at Carleton University, have a valid Canadian study permit, and demonstrate financial need. The deadline to apply is November 10, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.carleton.ca/](https://www.carleton.ca/).

## Application Process
To apply for the Canada-Turkey Exchange Program, follow these steps:
1. Check your eligibility for the program by reviewing the visa categories and scholarship requirements.
2. Choose the scholarships you are eligible for and review the application deadlines.
3. Review the application requirements for each scholarship, including the required documents and forms.
4. Prepare your application package, including your personal statement, academic transcripts, and language proficiency test results.
5. Submit your application package to the relevant university or organization by the deadline.
6. Wait for the application to be processed and reviewed by the scholarship committee.
7. Receive notification of the application outcome, including any additional requirements or next steps.
8. Accept the scholarship offer and review the terms and conditions of the award.
