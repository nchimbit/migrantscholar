## Introduction to Fully Funded UK Chevening Scholarship for Refugee Students
The UK Chevening Scholarship is a fully funded program for refugee students who wish to pursue a Master's degree in the UK. This prestigious scholarship aims to support talented individuals who have been displaced due to conflict, persecution, or natural disasters, and are now seeking to rebuild their lives through education. The UK Chevening Scholarship covers all tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs, making it an attractive opportunity for refugee students who want to study in the UK.

## Who Qualifies for the UK Chevening Scholarship
To be eligible for the UK Chevening Scholarship, refugee students must hold a valid visa in one of the following categories:
* Refugee visa
* Humanitarian Protection visa
* Discretionary Leave to Remain visa
* Leave to Remain under Article 8 of the European Convention on Human Rights visa
* Asylum seeker with a pending application
* Stateless person visa
* Family reunion visa
