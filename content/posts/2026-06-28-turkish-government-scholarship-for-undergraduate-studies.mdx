## Introduction to Turkish Government Scholarship
The Turkish Government Scholarship is an excellent opportunity for refugees and asylum seekers to pursue their undergraduate degree in Turkey. This scholarship program is designed to support international students, including those with refugee status, to study at top Turkish universities. By providing full tuition, accommodation, and a monthly stipend, the Turkish Government Scholarship helps students to focus on their academic goals without financial burdens.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Turkish Government Scholarship, applicants must hold a valid visa or residence permit in one of the following categories:
* Conditional refugee
* Refugee
* Subsidiary protection
* Family reunion visa
* Student visa
* Humanitarian residence permit
* Temporary protection

## Available Scholarships
The Turkish Government offers various scholarship programs for undergraduate studies. Here are five notable scholarships:
### Turkey Scholarship
The Turkey Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 750 Turkish Lira, tuition fee waiver, and accommodation support. Eligible applicants must have a high school diploma with a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/](https://turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/).
### Yunus Emre Scholarship
The Yunus Emre Scholarship covers tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 900 Turkish Lira. Applicants must have a high school diploma with a minimum GPA of 3.5. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit [https://yee.org.tr/](https://yee.org.tr/).
### Mevlana Exchange Scholarship
The Mevlana Exchange Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 750 Turkish Lira, tuition fee waiver, and accommodation support. Eligible applicants must have a high school diploma with a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is September 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.mevlana.gov.tr/](https://www.mevlana.gov.tr/).
### Bilkent University Scholarship
The Bilkent University Scholarship covers tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 1,200 Turkish Lira. Applicants must have a high school diploma with a minimum GPA of 3.5. The deadline for application is August 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.bilkent.edu.tr/](https://www.bilkent.edu.tr/).
### Istanbul University Scholarship
The Istanbul University Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 900 Turkish Lira, tuition fee waiver, and accommodation support. Eligible applicants must have a high school diploma with a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is July 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.istanbul.edu.tr/](https://www.istanbul.edu.tr/).

## Application Steps
To apply for the Turkish Government Scholarship, follow these steps:
1. Check the eligibility criteria and deadlines for each scholarship program.
2. Prepare all required documents, including a valid passport, high school diploma, and language proficiency certificate.
3. Fill out the online application form on the official website of the scholarship program.
4. Upload all required documents and submit the application.
5. Pay the application fee, if required.
6. Wait for the application to be reviewed and processed.
7. Receive the scholarship award letter and confirm acceptance.
8. Apply for a student visa and travel to Turkey for the academic program.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is the deadline for applying to the Turkish Government Scholarship?
The deadline for applying to the Turkish Government Scholarship varies depending on the program, but the earliest deadline is July 2026.
2. Can I apply for the Turkish Government Scholarship with a conditional refugee status?
Yes, applicants with conditional refugee status are eligible to apply for the Turkish Government Scholarship.
3. How much is the monthly stipend for the Turkey Scholarship?
The monthly stipend for the Turkey Scholarship is 750 Turkish Lira.
4. Do I need to speak Turkish to apply for the Turkish Government Scholarship?
No, proficiency in English is also accepted, but knowledge of Turkish may be an advantage.
5. Can I apply for the Turkish Government Scholarship if I have already started my undergraduate studies?
No, the Turkish Government Scholarship is only available for new undergraduate students.
6. How long does it take to process the scholarship application?
The processing time for the scholarship application varies, but it can take up to 3-4 months.
7. Can I work part-time while studying in Turkey with the Turkish Government Scholarship?
Yes, international students, including scholarship holders, are allowed to work part-time in Turkey.
8. What happens if I fail to meet the academic requirements of the scholarship program?
If you fail to meet the academic requirements, your scholarship may be terminated, and you may need to return to your home country.
