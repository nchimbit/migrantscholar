## Introduction to USA Graduate Awards for Migrant Students in STEM Fields
As a migrant student looking to pursue a graduate degree in a STEM field in the USA, you may be wondering what financial aid options are available to support your research and innovation endeavors. The USA offers various graduate awards, scholarships, and grants to migrant students in STEM fields, including those with F-1, J-1, and H-1B visas. These awards can help cover tuition fees, living expenses, and research costs, enabling you to focus on your academic and professional goals.

## Who Qualifies for USA Graduate Awards
To be eligible for USA graduate awards, migrant students must hold a valid visa that allows them to study in the USA. The following visa categories qualify:
* F-1 visa: for academic students
* J-1 visa: for exchange visitors, including graduate students
* H-1B visa: for specialty occupations, including STEM fields
* O-1 visa: for individuals with extraordinary ability, including in STEM fields
* TN visa: for Canadian and Mexican citizens working in specific professions, including STEM fields

## Available Scholarships for Migrant Students in STEM Fields
The following scholarships are available to support migrant students pursuing graduate degrees in STEM fields in the USA:
### National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship Program
The National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship Program provides a coverage amount of $34,000 per year for three years, and is eligible to students who are pursuing a research-based master's or doctoral degree in a STEM field. The deadline for application is October 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.nsfgrfp.org.
### Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans
The Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans provides a coverage amount of $90,000 over two years, and is eligible to students who are migrants or children of migrants, and are pursuing a graduate degree in any field, including STEM. The deadline for application is November 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.pdsoros.org.
### The Fulbright Foreign Student Program
The Fulbright Foreign Student Program provides a coverage amount that varies depending on the country of origin and the institution, and is eligible to international students, including migrants, who are pursuing a graduate degree in a STEM field. The deadline for application is October 2026, and the official website URL is https://foreign.fulbrightonline.org.
### The AAUW International Fellowship
The AAUW International Fellowship provides a coverage amount of $18,000 to $30,000 per year, and is eligible to women who are migrants or international students, and are pursuing a graduate degree in a STEM field. The deadline for application is November 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.aauw.org.
### The Microsoft Research PhD Fellowship
The Microsoft Research PhD Fellowship provides a coverage amount of $42,000 per year for two years, and is eligible to students who are pursuing a PhD in a STEM field, including computer science, electrical engineering, and mathematics. The deadline for application is October 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/academic-program/phd-fellowship.

## Application Steps for USA Graduate Awards
To apply for USA graduate awards, follow these steps:
1. Research and identify the awards that you are eligible for, and review the eligibility criteria and application requirements.
2. Review and ensure you meet the visa requirements for studying in the USA.
3. Prepare and submit your application, including all required documents and materials.
4. Take any required standardized tests, such as the GRE or TOEFL.
5. Obtain letters of recommendation from academic or professional references.
6. Submit your application before the deadline.
7. Wait for the application to be reviewed, and receive notification of the decision.
8. Accept the award and review the terms and conditions of the award.

## Documents Checklist for USA Graduate Awards
When applying for USA graduate awards, you will typically need to submit the following documents:
* Passport
* Visa documentation
* Academic transcripts
* Letters of recommendation
* Standardized test scores
* Personal statement or essay
* Resume or CV
* Proof of financial support
* Language proficiency test scores, such as TOEFL or IELTS
