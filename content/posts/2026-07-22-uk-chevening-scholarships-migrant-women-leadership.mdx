# Chevening
The UK Chevening Scholarships are available to migrant women in leadership positions in Germany and Turkey, offering funding of up to £18,000 for a one-year master's degree. 

## Who Qualifies
Migrant women who are currently residing in Germany or Turkey and hold a valid visa such as a residence permit, work permit, or student visa may be eligible to apply for the Chevening Scholarships. Additionally, women with refugee status or asylum seeker status may also be considered. Visa categories that may be eligible include, but are not limited to, family reunion visas, work visas, and student visas. ## Available Scholarships
### Chevening Scholarships
* Amount: up to £18,000
* Eligibility: Migrant women in leadership positions in Germany and Turkey
* Deadline: November 1, 2026
* Official URL: https://www.chevening.org/
### DAAD Helmut-Schmidt-Programme
* Amount: €931 per month
* Eligibility: Migrant women from developing countries
* Deadline: July 31, 2026
* Official URL: https://www.daad.de/
### British Council STEM Scholarships for Women
* Amount: Full tuition fees
* Eligibility: Migrant women in STEM fields
* Deadline: February 28, 2027
* Official URL: https://www.britishcouncil.org/
### Commonwealth Scholarships
* Amount: Full tuition fees
* Eligibility: Migrant women from Commonwealth countries
* Deadline: December 1, 2026
* Official URL: https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/
### WeTech Scholarships
* Amount: up to $10,000
* Eligibility: Migrant women in tech fields
* Deadline: April 15, 2027
* Official URL: https://www.iie.org/
