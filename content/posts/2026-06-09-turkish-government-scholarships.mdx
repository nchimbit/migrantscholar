## Introduction to Turkish Government Scholarships
The Turkish Government offers fully funded scholarships to international students, including migrant, refugee, and asylum seeker students, pursuing undergraduate or graduate studies in Turkey. These scholarships cover various expenses, including tuition fees, accommodation, and living expenses, making it an attractive opportunity for students from around the world. With a wide range of programs available, students can choose from various fields of study, including engineering, humanities, and social sciences.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Turkish Government Scholarships, international students must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* Migrant students with a residence permit
* Refugee students with a refugee ID
* Asylum seeker students with an asylum seeker ID
* International students from countries with which Turkey has a bilateral agreement
* Students who have graduated from a Turkish university and want to pursue further studies

## Available Scholarships
The Turkish Government offers several fully funded scholarships to international students. Here are five real scholarships available:
### Türkiye Scholarships
The Türkiye Scholarships cover a monthly stipend of 800-1,100 Turkish Lira, tuition fees, and accommodation expenses. To be eligible, students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be under the age of 25 for undergraduate studies. The deadline for application is usually in February, and students can apply through the official website: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/
### Anadolu Agency Scholarships
The Anadolu Agency Scholarships cover a monthly stipend of 1,500 Turkish Lira, tuition fees, and accommodation expenses. To be eligible, students must be pursuing a degree in journalism or communications and have a minimum GPA of 3.5. The deadline for application is usually in March, and students can apply through the official website: https://www.aa.com.tr/
### Turkish Language Scholarships
The Turkish Language Scholarships cover a monthly stipend of 600-800 Turkish Lira, tuition fees, and accommodation expenses. To be eligible, students must be pursuing a degree in Turkish language and literature and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is usually in April, and students can apply through the official website: https://www.turkishlanguage.co/
### Research Scholarships
The Research Scholarships cover a monthly stipend of 2,000-3,000 Turkish Lira, tuition fees, and accommodation expenses. To be eligible, students must be pursuing a master's or PhD degree in a research field and have a minimum GPA of 3.5. The deadline for application is usually in May, and students can apply through the official website: https://www.tubitak.gov.tr/
### Islamic Studies Scholarships
The Islamic Studies Scholarships cover a monthly stipend of 1,000-1,500 Turkish Lira, tuition fees, and accommodation expenses. To be eligible, students must be pursuing a degree in Islamic studies and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is usually in June, and students can apply through the official website: https://www.diyanet.gov.tr/

## Application Steps
To apply for the Turkish Government Scholarships, international students must follow these steps:
1. Check the eligibility criteria for the desired scholarship
2. Prepare the required documents, including transcripts, diplomas, and language proficiency certificates
3. Fill out the online application form through the official website
4. Upload the required documents to the online application system
5. Submit the application and wait for the review process
6. Take the Turkish language proficiency exam, if required
7. Participate in an interview, if shortlisted
8. Receive the scholarship award letter and start the visa application process
