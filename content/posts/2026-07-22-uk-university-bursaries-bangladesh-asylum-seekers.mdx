The Article 26 Scholarship qualifies Bangladeshi asylum seekers pursuing undergraduate studies with a funding amount of £30,000. This scholarship is a beacon of hope for those seeking to further their education in the UK. As an asylum seeker, finding the right scholarship can be a daunting task, but there are several UK university bursaries available to support Bangladeshi students.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, Bangladeshi asylum seekers must hold a specific visa category, including those with discretionary leave, limited leave, or humanitarian protection. Additionally, applicants must have a strong academic record and demonstrate a commitment to their field of study.

## Available Scholarships
The following scholarships are available to Bangladeshi asylum seekers pursuing undergraduate studies in the UK:
### The Article 26 Scholarship
* Amount: £30,000
* Eligibility: Bangladeshi asylum seekers with discretionary leave, limited leave, or humanitarian protection
* Deadline: February 2027
* Official URL: https://www.universitiesuk.ac.uk/what-we-do/policy-and-research/topics/widening-participation/article-26-project
### The Sanctuary Scholarship
* Amount: £10,000
* Eligibility: Asylum seekers and refugees with a strong academic record
* Deadline: March 2027
* Official URL: https://www.sanctuaryscholarship.org.uk/
### The University of East London Scholarship
* Amount: £5,000
* Eligibility: Bangladeshi asylum seekers with limited leave or humanitarian protection
* Deadline: April 2027
* Official URL: https://www.uel.ac.uk/international/scholarships
### The Brunel University London Scholarship
* Amount: £3,000
* Eligibility: Asylum seekers and refugees with a commitment to their field of study
* Deadline: May 2027
* Official URL: https://www.brunel.ac.uk/international/scholarships
### The University of Bedfordshire Scholarship
* Amount: £2,000
* Eligibility: Bangladeshi asylum seekers with discretionary leave
* Deadline: June 2027
* Official URL: https://www.beds.ac.uk/international/scholarships
