# STEM Scholarships UK
The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships and Leadership Programme exists for asylum seekers and provides £14,000 funding for graduate-level study in the UK.

## Who Qualifies
Asylum seekers in the UK with a pending application for asylum, or those who have been granted refugee status in the UK, may qualify for these scholarships. This includes individuals with Limited Leave to Remain, Discretionary Leave, or Humanitarian Protection. Visa categories such as the Family Reunion Visa or the Protection Visa may also be eligible.

## Available Scholarships
### The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarships and Leadership Programme
£14,000, graduate-level study, [www.oxfordscholarships.com](http://www.oxfordscholarships.com), deadline varies.
### The Sanctuary Scholarships
£9,000, undergraduate or graduate-level study, [www.city.ac.uk/sanctuary-scholarships](http://www.city.ac.uk/sanctuary-scholarships), deadline 31 March 2026.
### The University of Strathclyde Sanctuary Scholarship
£4,500, undergraduate or graduate-level study, [www.strath.ac.uk/study/fees/scholarships/sanctuaryscholarship](http://www.strath.ac.uk/study/fees/scholarships/sanctuaryscholarship), deadline 31 May 2026.
### The Article 26 Scholarship
£10,000, undergraduate or graduate-level study, [www.universitiesuk.ac.uk/article-26](http://www.universitiesuk.ac.uk/article-26), deadline 30 June 2026.
### The Scholarship for Asylum Seekers
£6,000, graduate-level study, [www.ed.ac.uk/student-funding/undergraduate/scholarships/asylum-seekers](http://www.ed.ac.uk/student-funding/undergraduate/scholarships/asylum-seekers), deadline 31 July 2026.
