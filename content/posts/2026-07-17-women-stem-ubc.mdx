The University of British Columbia's [Women in STEM](https://www.students.ubc.ca/enrolment/finances/scholarships-awards-subsidies) scholarship exists and qualifies female international students in STEM fields, with funding amounts ranging from $5,000 to $10,000.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the University of British Columbia's women-only scholarships in STEM fields, applicants must be female international students holding a valid study permit under the [Student Direct Stream](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/study-canada/study-permit/student-direct-stream.html) or [regular study permit](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/study-canada/study-permit.html) categories, and be enrolled in a STEM field program at the University of British Columbia. This includes students from [Turkey](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Turkey) and [Canada](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada).

## Available Scholarships
The University of British Columbia offers several women-only scholarships in STEM fields, including:
### International Women in STEM Scholarship
* Amount: $10,000
* Eligibility: Female international students in STEM fields
* Deadline: January 15th
* Official URL: https://www.students.ubc.ca/enrolment/finances/scholarships-awards-subsidies
### University of British Columbia Scholarship for Women in Engineering
* Amount: $5,000
* Eligibility: Female international students in engineering programs
* Deadline: February 1st
* Official URL: https://www.engineering.ubc.ca/study ENGINEERING/scholarships-awards.html
### Killam Scholarship for Women in STEM
* Amount: $5,000
* Eligibility: Female international students in STEM fields
* Deadline: March 1st
* Official URL: https://www.students.ubc.ca/enrolment/finances/scholarships-awards-subsidies
### University of British Columbia Scholarship for Women in Computer Science
* Amount: $5,000
* Eligibility: Female international students in computer science programs
* Deadline: April 1st

### International Tuition Award
* Amount: $3,200
* Eligibility: Female international students in all programs, including [STEM fields](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/fields-of-study/science-technology-engineering-and-mathematics)
* Deadline: May 1st
* Official URL: https://www.students.ubc.ca/enrolment/finances/tuition-fees/international-tuition-award

## Application Steps
To apply for the University of British Columbia's women-only scholarships in STEM fields, follow these steps:
1. Review the eligibility criteria and [scholarship deadlines](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/deadlines) for each scholarship.
2. Submit an application to the University of British Columbia through the online portal.
3. Ensure all required documents, including transcripts and [language proficiency tests](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/language-proficiency), are submitted by the deadline.
4. Complete and submit any additional application forms required for specific scholarships.
5. Submit a personal statement outlining academic and career goals.
6. Provide two academic references.
7. Submit proof of [study permit](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/study-canada/study-permit.html) or [student visa](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/study-canada/study-permit/student-visa.html).
8. Review and confirm the application before submission.
