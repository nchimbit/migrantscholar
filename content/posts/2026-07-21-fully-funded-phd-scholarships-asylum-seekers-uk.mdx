# PhD Scholarships
The University of Cambridge's [Gates Cambridge Scholarship](https://www.gatescambridge.org/) exists for asylum seekers, providing full funding of tuition fees and a maintenance allowance of approximately £18,744 per annum, at the University of Cambridge, UK.

## Who Qualifies
Asylum seekers who have been granted refugee status or humanitarian protection in the UK, and have been given permission to stay in the UK, may be eligible for this scholarship. Visa categories that may be eligible include Tier 4, Tier 2, or those with leave to remain. To be eligible, applicants must meet the University's entrance requirements for a PhD, which typically involves a strong academic record, English language proficiency, and a well-developed research proposal. ## Available Scholarships
The following scholarships are available to asylum seekers:
### Gates Cambridge Scholarship
* Amount: Full tuition fees and maintenance allowance of approximately £18,744 per annum
* Eligibility: Asylum seekers who have been granted refugee status or humanitarian protection in the UK
* Deadline: December of each year
* Official URL: https://www.gatescambridge.org/
### Cambridge Trust Scholarship
* Amount: Full tuition fees and maintenance allowance of approximately £18,744 per annum
* Eligibility: Asylum seekers who have been granted refugee status or humanitarian protection in the UK
* Deadline: December of each year
* Official URL: https://www.cambridgetrust.org/
### Chevening Scholarship
* Amount: Full tuition fees and maintenance allowance of approximately £18,744 per annum
* Eligibility: Asylum seekers who have been granted refugee status or humanitarian protection in the UK, and have a strong academic record
* Deadline: November of each year
* Official URL: https://www.chevening.org/
### Commonwealth Scholarship
* Amount: Full tuition fees and maintenance allowance of approximately £18,744 per annum
* Eligibility: Asylum seekers who have been granted refugee status or humanitarian protection in the UK, and are from a Commonwealth country
* Deadline: December of each year
* Official URL: https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/
### DAAD Scholarship
* Amount: Full tuition fees and maintenance allowance of approximately €1,200 per month
* Eligibility: Asylum seekers who have been granted refugee status or humanitarian protection in the UK, and have a strong academic record
* Deadline: October of each year
* Official URL: https://www.daad.de/

## Application Steps
To apply for these scholarships, follow these steps:
1. Research the University of Cambridge's PhD programs and find a supervisor.
2. Check your eligibility for the scholarship and the PhD program.
3. Prepare your research proposal and personal statement.
4. Gather your academic documents, including transcripts and references.
5. Apply for the PhD program through the University's online application system.
6. Submit your scholarship application, if required.
7. Prepare for and take the English language proficiency test, if required.
8. Track your application and wait for the decision.
