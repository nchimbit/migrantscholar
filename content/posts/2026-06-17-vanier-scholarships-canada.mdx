## Introduction to Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Refugees
As an asylum seeker or refugee pursuing doctoral studies in Canada, you may be eligible for the prestigious Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships, which provide financial support for doctoral studies in social sciences and humanities. The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships are designed to attract and retain world-class doctoral students, and asylum seekers and refugees are encouraged to apply. With a focus on doctoral studies in social sciences and humanities, these scholarships can help you achieve your academic goals in Canada.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships, you must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Temporary Resident Visa (TRV)
* Study Permit
* Temporary Resident Permit (TRP)
*Protected Person Status
* Convention Refugee
* Person in Need of Protection

## Available Scholarships
### Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship
The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship provides $50,000 per year for three years, and is open to international students, including asylum seekers and refugees, pursuing doctoral studies in social sciences and humanities. To be eligible, you must have achieved a first-class average in your previous two years of study, and be nominated by a Canadian university. The deadline for application is November 1st, and you can find more information on the [official website](https://vanier.gc.ca).
