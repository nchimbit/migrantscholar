## Turkey Government Scholarships for International Students
The Turkey Government Scholarships are available for international students who wish to pursue their undergraduate studies in Turkey, providing them with full tuition plus living costs and accommodation for the duration of their studies. 
### Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Turkey Government Scholarships, international students must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* Foreign national students
* Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus citizens
* Stateless persons
* Refugees
* Blue cardholders
* Minor or adult foreign nationals who are under the protection of Turkey

## Available Scholarships
Here are five real scholarships available for international students who wish to pursue their undergraduate studies in Turkey:
### Türkiye Scholarships
The Türkiye Scholarships provide a monthly stipend of TRY 2,500, full tuition waiver, and accommodation for the duration of the undergraduate studies, with eligibility limited to students who are under the age of 21. The deadline for application is November 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/.
### Turkish Government Scholarships
The Turkish Government Scholarships provide a monthly stipend of TRY 2,000, full tuition waiver, and accommodation for the duration of the undergraduate studies, with eligibility limited to students who have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is October 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.turkiye.gov.tr/.
### Kadir Has University Scholarships
The Kadir Has University Scholarships provide a full tuition waiver and accommodation for the duration of the undergraduate studies, with eligibility limited to students who have a minimum GPA of 3.5. The deadline for application is September 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.khas.edu.tr/.
### Istanbul Aydın University Scholarships
The Istanbul Aydın University Scholarships provide a monthly stipend of TRY 1,500, full tuition waiver, and accommodation for the duration of the undergraduate studies, with eligibility limited to students who have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is August 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.aydin.edu.tr/.
### YTB Scholarships
The YTB Scholarships provide a monthly stipend of TRY 2,000, full tuition waiver, and accommodation for the duration of the undergraduate studies, with eligibility limited to students who are from certain countries. The deadline for application is November 2026, and the official website URL is https://www.ytb.gov.tr/.
