## Introduction to Turkish Government Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Refugee Students in Australia
The Turkish Government offers various scholarships to asylum seekers and refugee students in Australia, providing them with an opportunity to pursue higher education in Turkey. These scholarships are designed to support undergraduate and postgraduate students who are seeking to improve their educational and career prospects. By offering these scholarships, the Turkish Government aims to promote cultural exchange and understanding between Turkey and Australia, while also supporting the educational aspirations of asylum seekers and refugee students.

## Who Qualifies for Turkish Government Scholarships
Asylum seekers and refugee students in Australia who hold certain visa categories are eligible to apply for Turkish Government scholarships. The following visa categories are eligible:
* Permanent Humanitarian Visa (subclass 200-218)
* Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790)
* Protection Visa (subclass 866)
* Temporary Humanitarian Concern Visa (subclass 786)
* Refugee Visa (subclass 800)
* Special Humanitarian Program Visa (subclass 201-204)
