## Who Qualifies
Refugee students from Canada who hold a valid Canadian visa under the following categories can apply for the Turkey Graduate Scholarship:
* Convention Refugee Abroad Class
* Country of Asylum Class
* Protected Persons in Canada
* Government-Assisted Refugee
* Privately Sponsored Refugee
* Refugee Claimants
* Asylum Seekers

## Application Steps
To apply for the Turkey Graduate Scholarship for Refugee Students from Canada, follow these steps:
1. Review the eligibility criteria to ensure you qualify.
2. Choose your desired program of study and university in Turkey.
3. Prepare all required documents, including transcripts and language proficiency certificates.
4. Submit your application through the online portal.
5. Pay the application fee, if required.
6. Wait for the selection committee to review your application.
7. If selected, apply for a student visa at the Turkish embassy or consulate in Canada.
8. Register at your university in Turkey and begin your studies.
