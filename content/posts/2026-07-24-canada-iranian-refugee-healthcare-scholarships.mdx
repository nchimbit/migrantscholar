## Who Qualifies
Iranian refugee students who hold a valid Canadian permanent resident visa or a temporary resident visa under section 24 of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (Canada) may be eligible for these scholarships. 
This includes visa categories such as Convention Refugees, Protected Persons, or Humanitarian-protected Persons abroad. 
For detailed information on visa categories, visit the official Government of Canada website. 
These students can apply for [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada) in healthcare, which can help them pursue their academic and professional goals.

## Available Scholarships
### Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship
* Amount: $50,000 per year for three years
* Eligibility: Open to Canadian citizens, permanent residents, and international students, including Iranian refugees
* Deadline: November 1st
* Official URL: https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PG-CS/VanierCGS-SMVC/index_eng.asp
### Ontario Graduate Scholarship
* Amount: $15,000 per term for two or three terms
* Eligibility: Open to Canadian citizens, permanent residents, and international students, including Iranian refugees
* Deadline: Varies by institution
* Official URL: https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-graduate-scholarship
### Ontario Trillium Scholarship
* Amount: $40,000 per year for up to four years
* Eligibility: Open to international students, including Iranian refugees
* Deadline: Varies by institution
* Official URL: https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-trillium-scholarship
### CIHR Doctoral Foreign Study Award
* Amount: $30,000 to $50,000 per year for up to two years
* Eligibility: Open to Canadian citizens, permanent residents, and international students, including Iranian refugees
* Deadline: October 15th
* Official URL: https://cihr-irsc.gc.ca/e/49835.html
### Queen Elizabeth II Graduate Scholarship in Science and Technology
* Amount: $15,000 per term for two or three terms
* Eligibility: Open to Canadian citizens, permanent residents, and international students, including Iranian refugees
* Deadline: Varies by institution
* Official URL: https://www.ontario.ca/page/queen-elizabeth-ii-graduate-scholarship-science-and-technology
