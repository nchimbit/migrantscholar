## Introduction to Istanbul University Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Refugees 2026
If you are an asylum seeker or refugee looking to further your education in Turkey, you may be eligible for scholarships offered by Istanbul University. The university provides various opportunities for individuals with refugee or asylum seeker status to pursue their academic goals. These scholarships aim to support the education and community building of asylum seekers and refugees in Turkey.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for Istanbul University scholarships, you must hold one of the following visa categories:
* International Protection Applicant Visa
* International Protection Status Holder Visa
* Temporary Protection Visa
* Refugee Visa
* Asylum Seeker Visa
* Humanitarian Residence Visa

## Available Scholarships
Istanbul University offers several scholarships to support the education of asylum seekers and refugees. Here are five available scholarships:
### Istanbul University Scholarship for Refugees
The Istanbul University Scholarship for Refugees covers 100% of tuition fees, along with a monthly stipend of 1,500 TRY. To be eligible, applicants must be refugee status holders, have a high school diploma or equivalent, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is March 31, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.istanbul.edu.tr/en](https://www.istanbul.edu.tr/en).
### Turkey Government Scholarship for Asylum Seekers
The Turkey Government Scholarship for Asylum Seekers provides a full scholarship, including tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 1,800 TRY. Eligible applicants must be asylum seekers or international protection applicants, have a bachelor's degree or equivalent, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is February 20, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/en](https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/en).
### UNHCR Turkey Scholarship
The UNHCR Turkey Scholarship covers 50% of tuition fees, along with a monthly stipend of 1,000 TRY. To be eligible, applicants must be registered with UNHCR Turkey, have a high school diploma or equivalent, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is April 15, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.unhcr.org/tr](https://www.unhcr.org/tr).
### Istanbul University Asylum Seeker Scholarship
The Istanbul University Asylum Seeker Scholarship covers 75% of tuition fees, along with a monthly stipend of 1,200 TRY. Eligible applicants must be asylum seekers, have a bachelor's degree or equivalent, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is March 15, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.istanbul.edu.tr/en](https://www.istanbul.edu.tr/en).
### Turkish Red Crescent Scholarship for Refugees
The Turkish Red Crescent Scholarship for Refugees provides a full scholarship, including tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of 1,500 TRY. To be eligible, applicants must be refugee status holders, have a high school diploma or equivalent, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The deadline for application is May 1, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.kizilay.org.tr/en](https://www.kizilay.org.tr/en).

## Application Steps
To apply for Istanbul University scholarships, follow these steps:
1. Check your eligibility and choose the scholarship you want to apply for.
2. Prepare all the required documents, including your passport, visa, and academic transcripts.
3. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline.
4. Pay the application fee, if required.
5. Wait for the evaluation process to be completed.
6. If selected, receive and sign the scholarship agreement.
7. Register for your chosen program at Istanbul University.
8. Start your academic journey at Istanbul University.
