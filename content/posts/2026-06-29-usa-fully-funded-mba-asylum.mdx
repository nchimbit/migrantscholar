## Introduction to Fully Funded MBA Programs for Asylum Seekers in the USA

These programs not only cover full tuition but also living costs, ensuring that asylum seekers can focus on their studies and build a better future.

To be eligible for these fully funded MBA programs, asylum seekers must hold a specific visa category. The following are some of the visa categories that qualify:
* Asylee (AS)

* Withholding of Removal (WOR)
