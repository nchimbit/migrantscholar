# Women in Healthcare
The Deutschlandstipendium scholarship exists and qualifies migrant women pursuing Master's programs in healthcare in Germany with a funding amount of up to 300 euros per month. 

## Who Qualifies
Migrant women who hold a residence permit, visa categories such as family reunification, student visa, or asylum seekers with a refugee status, can apply for these scholarships. To be eligible, applicants must demonstrate academic excellence, have a strong command of the German language, and meet the specific requirements of each scholarship program. 

## Scholarships
### DAAD Scholarship for Healthcare Professionals
The DAAD Scholarship for Healthcare Professionals offers up to 750 euros per month for 12-24 months, and is open to migrant women with a Bachelor's degree in a healthcare-related field. Deadline: October 31, 2026. [https://www.daad.de](https://www.daad.de)
### Helmut-Schmidt-Programme 
The Helmut-Schmidt-Programme offers up to 850 euros per month for 12-24 months, and is open to migrant women with a Bachelor's degree in a healthcare-related field. Deadline: July 31, 2026. [https://www.daad.de/go/en/scholarships](https://www.daad.de/go/en/scholarships)
### Friedrich-Ebert-Stiftung Scholarship 
The Friedrich-Ebert-Stiftung Scholarship offers up to 750 euros per month for 12-24 months, and is open to migrant women with a Bachelor's degree in a healthcare-related field. Deadline: November 30, 2026. [https://www.fes.de/studienfoerderung](https://www.fes.de/studienfoerderung)
### Heinrich-Boll-Stiftung Scholarship 
The Heinrich-Boll-Stiftung Scholarship offers up to 750 euros per month for 12-24 months, and is open to migrant women with a Bachelor's degree in a healthcare-related field. Deadline: September 1, 2026. [https://www.boell.de/en/foundation/scholarships](https://www.boell.de/en/foundation/scholarships)
### Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung Scholarship 
The Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung Scholarship offers up to 850 euros per month for 12-24 months, and is open to migrant women with a Bachelor's degree in a healthcare-related field. Deadline: July 15, 2026. [https://www.kas.de/go/scholarships](https://www.kas.de/go/scholarships)

## Application Steps
1. Check the official website of the scholarship programs to review the eligibility criteria and application requirements.
2. Prepare all the necessary documents, including a valid passport, academic transcripts, and language proficiency certificates.
3. Fill out the application form accurately and completely.
4. Write a compelling personal statement that highlights your academic background, career goals, and personal motivations.
5. Secure letters of recommendation from academic or professional mentors.
6. Submit your application before the deadline.
7. Wait for the selection committee to review your application.
8. Receive notification of the scholarship award, if selected.
