The Fulbright-Nehru Master's Fellowship is available to Indian students, providing funding of up to $100,000 to pursue higher education in the USA. 
This prestigious scholarship is a great opportunity for Indian students to experience the American education system and gain valuable knowledge and skills. 
In order to be eligible for this scholarship, Indian students must meet certain requirements, including having a strong academic record and a good understanding of the English language. 
The USA is a popular destination for international students, and Indian students are no exception, with many choosing to pursue higher education in the country each year.

## Who Qualifies
Indian students who wish to pursue higher education in the USA must meet certain visa requirements. 
The USA offers several types of visas for students, including the F-1 visa, which is the most common type of student visa. 
To be eligible for an F-1 visa, Indian students must have been accepted into a full-time course of study at a US university or college. 
They must also demonstrate that they have sufficient funds to support themselves while studying in the USA. 
Additionally, Indian students must meet certain eligibility criteria, including having a valid passport, being proficient in English, and having a good academic record. 
The following visa categories are available to Indian students: F-1, J-1, and M-1.
