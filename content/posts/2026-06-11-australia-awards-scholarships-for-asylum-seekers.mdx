## Introduction to Australia Awards Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Migrant Students
If you are an asylum seeker or migrant student looking to pursue higher education in Australia, you may be eligible for the Australia Awards Scholarships. These scholarships provide financial support to students who are unable to afford the costs of higher education, allowing them to achieve their academic goals. The Australia Awards Scholarships are a great opportunity for asylum seekers and migrant students to study in Australia and gain valuable skills and knowledge.

## Who Qualifies for the Australia Awards Scholarships
To be eligible for the Australia Awards Scholarships, you must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Protection visa (subclass 866)
* Temporary Protection visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise visa (subclass 790)
* Permanent Protection visa (subclass 866)
* Humanitarian Stay (Temporary) visa (subclass 449)
* Temporary Humanitarian Concern visa (subclass 786)
* Bridging visa E (subclass 050 or 051)

## Available Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Migrant Students
The following scholarships are available to asylum seekers and migrant students in Australia:
### Australia Awards Scholarship
The Australia Awards Scholarship provides full tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits to successful applicants. To be eligible, you must be a citizen of a participating country, have a minimum of 18 months of residency in Australia, and meet the specific eligibility criteria of the scholarship. The UNSW Sydney Scholarship for Asylum Seekers provides $10,000 per annum for up to 4 years to successful applicants.
