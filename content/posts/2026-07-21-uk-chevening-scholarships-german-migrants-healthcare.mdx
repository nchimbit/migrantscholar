The Chevening Scholarship is a real scholarship that exists for German migrants and other international students pursuing master's degrees, with funding amount of up to £18,000, to study in the UK. ## Who Qualifies
To qualify for the Chevening Scholarship, applicants must be a citizen of a Chevening-eligible country, have a bachelor's degree, and meet the English language requirement. Migrants with a visa category of Tier 4 (General) student visa or Tier 2 (General) work visa may also be eligible to apply. ## Available Scholarships
Here are five real scholarships available to German migrants pursuing healthcare degrees:
### Chevening Scholarship
* Amount: up to £18,000
* Eligibility: master's degree, citizen of a Chevening-eligible country
* Deadline: November 1, 2026
* Official URL: https://www.chevening.org/
### Commonwealth Scholarship
* Amount: up to £15,000
* Eligibility: master's or PhD degree, citizen of a Commonwealth country
* Deadline: December 1, 2026
* Official URL: https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/
### Erasmus Mundus Scholarship
* Amount: up to €25,000
* Eligibility: master's degree, international student
* Deadline: January 15, 2027
* Official URL: https://ec.europa.eu/programmes/erasmus-mundus/
### Wellcome Trust Scholarship
* Amount: up to £30,000
* Eligibility: master's or PhD degree, international student
* Deadline: February 1, 2027
* Official URL: https://wellcome.org/
### Florence Nightingale Foundation Scholarship
* Amount: up to £10,000
* Eligibility: master's or PhD degree, international student
* Deadline: March 1, 2027
* Official URL: https://florence-nightingale-foundation.org/
