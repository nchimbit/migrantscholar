## Turkey Government Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Refugee Students in Canada
The Turkey Government Scholarships are available for asylum seekers and refugee students in Canada, providing them with the opportunity to pursue their higher education in Turkey. 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Turkey Government Scholarships, asylum seekers and refugee students in Canada must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Convention Refugee
* Protected Person
* Asylum Seeker with a pending claim
* Refugee Claimant
* Person in Need of Protection

## Available Scholarships
### Turkey Scholarship for Asylum Seekers
The Turkey Scholarship for Asylum Seekers covers full tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of $500. To be eligible, applicants must be convention refugees or protected persons and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/

### Turkey Graduate Scholarship Program
The Turkey Graduate Scholarship Program covers full tuition fees and a monthly stipend of $600. Eligible applicants must be refugee students pursuing a master's or PhD degree and have a minimum GPA of 3.5. The deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, visit https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/

### Research Scholarship for Refugees
The Research Scholarship for Refugees covers research expenses, full tuition fees, and a monthly stipend of $700. To be eligible, applicants must be refugee students pursuing research in a Turkish university and have a minimum GPA of 3.8. The deadline for application is June 2027. For more information, visit https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/

### Undergraduate Scholarship for Asylum Seekers
The Undergraduate Scholarship for Asylum Seekers covers full tuition fees, accommodation, and a monthly stipend of $400. Eligible applicants must be asylum seekers with a pending claim and have a minimum GPA of 2.8. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/

### Short-Term Scholarship for Refugee Students

To be eligible, applicants must be refugee students pursuing a short-term program in a Turkish university and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, visit https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/
