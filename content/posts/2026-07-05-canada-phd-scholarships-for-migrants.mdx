## Canada Graduate Scholarships for Migrant Students Pursuing PhD
The Canada Graduate Scholarships for PhD research are available to migrant students pursuing a doctoral degree in Canada, specifically those who hold a temporary resident visa, student visa, or permanent resident visa.

### Who Qualifies
Migrant students who hold one of the following visa categories may be eligible for the Canada Graduate Scholarships:
* Temporary resident visa
* Student visa
* Permanent resident visa
* Protected person status
* Convention refugee
* Humanitarian-protected persons abroad

## Scholarships Available
The following are five real scholarships available to migrant students pursuing a PhD in Canada:
### Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships
The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships provide $50,000 per year for three years, and are available to international students who have achieved a first-class average and are pursuing a PhD in a Canadian university. The eligibility requirements include a research-based doctoral program and a nominated university. The deadline for this scholarship is November 1, 2026. For more information, visit: https://vanier.gc.ca/
### Trudeau Scholarship
The Trudeau Scholarship provides $40,000 per year for three years, and is available to international students who are pursuing a PhD in a Canadian university. The eligibility requirements include a research-based doctoral program, a nominated university, and a demonstrated commitment to public engagement and leadership. The deadline for this scholarship is January 15, 2027. For more information, visit: https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/
### Banting Postdoctoral Fellowships
The Banting Postdoctoral Fellowships provide $70,000 per year for two years, and are available to international students who have completed a PhD and are pursuing postdoctoral research in a Canadian university. The eligibility requirements include a research-based postdoctoral program and a nominated university. The deadline for this scholarship is October 1, 2026. For more information, visit: https://banting.fellowships-bourses.gc.ca/
### Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarship
The Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarship provides $40,000 per year for three years, and is available to international students who are pursuing a PhD in a Canadian university. The eligibility requirements include a research-based doctoral program, a nominated university, and a demonstrated commitment to public engagement and leadership. The deadline for this scholarship is January 15, 2027. For more information, visit: https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/
### Joseph-Armand Bombardier Canada Graduate Scholarships
The Joseph-Armand Bombardier Canada Graduate Scholarships provide $17,500 per year for one year, and are available to Canadian citizens and permanent residents who are pursuing a master's or doctoral degree in a Canadian university. The eligibility requirements include a research-based graduate program and a nominated university. The deadline for this scholarship is December 1, 2026. For more information, visit: https://www.sshrc-crsh.gc.ca/

## Application Steps
To apply for the Canada Graduate Scholarships, migrant students should follow these steps:
1. Check the eligibility requirements for each scholarship.
2. Choose a Canadian university that offers the desired PhD program.
3. Apply to the university and receive an offer of admission.
4. Complete the scholarship application form and upload required documents.
5. Submit the application form and supporting documents before the deadline.
6. Wait for the review process to be completed and receive a decision.
7. Accept the scholarship offer and begin the PhD program.
8. Complete any additional requirements, such as language proficiency tests or medical exams.
