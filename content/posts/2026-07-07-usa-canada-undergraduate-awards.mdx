## Migrant Student Awards for Undergraduate Studies in the USA and Canada
Migrant students holding certain visa categories, including F-1, J-1, and refugee status, are eligible to apply for various undergraduate scholarships in the USA and Canada, covering full tuition, living costs, and other expenses.

### Who Qualifies
To qualify for these undergraduate scholarships, migrant students must hold one of the following visa categories:
* F-1 visa
* J-1 visa
* Refugee status
* Asylum status
* Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
* Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA)

## Available Scholarships
There are numerous scholarships available for migrant students pursuing undergraduate studies in the USA and Canada. Here are five real scholarships:
### The Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans
The Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans provides up to $90,000 in coverage for tuition and living costs. Eligible applicants must be 30 years old or younger and hold a green card or be a naturalized US citizen. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.pdsoros.org/.
### The Gates Scholarship
The Gates Scholarship offers full tuition coverage, plus living costs, for undergraduate studies. Eligible applicants must be a minority student and hold a US passport or green card. The deadline for application is September 2026. For more information, visit https://www.thegatesscholarship.org/.
### The Horatio Alger Scholarship
The Horatio Alger Scholarship provides up to $25,000 in coverage for undergraduate studies. Eligible applicants must demonstrate financial need and hold a US passport or green card. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit https://www.horatioalger.org/.
### The Walmart Dependent Scholarship
The Walmart Dependent Scholarship offers up to $13,000 in coverage for undergraduate studies. Eligible applicants must be a dependent of a Walmart associate and hold a US passport or green card. The deadline for application is April 2027. For more information, visit https://www.walmartfoundation.org/.
### The Coca-Cola Scholars Foundation Scholarship
The Coca-Cola Scholars Foundation Scholarship provides up to $20,000 in coverage for undergraduate studies. Eligible applicants must be a high school senior and hold a US passport or green card. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit https://www.coca-colascholarsfoundation.org/.

## Application Steps
To apply for these undergraduate scholarships, migrant students should follow these steps:
1. Review the eligibility criteria for each scholarship.
2. Prepare all required documents, including transcripts and letters of recommendation.
3. Submit the application form before the deadline.
4. Write a strong personal statement outlining academic and career goals.
5. Prepare for and take required standardized tests, such as the SAT or ACT.
6. Research and apply for additional scholarships to increase chances of receiving funding.
7. Follow up with the scholarship provider to confirm receipt of the application.
8. Review and accept the scholarship award, if selected.
