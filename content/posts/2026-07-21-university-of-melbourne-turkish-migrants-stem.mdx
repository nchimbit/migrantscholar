You must also meet the specific eligibility criteria for each scholarship, including academic requirements, language proficiency, and field of study.
Some scholarships may require you to be a new student, while others may be available for current students.
If you are a Turkish migrant with a strong academic background and a passion for STEM fields, you may be eligible for one of the following scholarships.

Deadline: 31 December 2026
Official URL: https://engineering.unimelb.edu.au/study/scholarships
