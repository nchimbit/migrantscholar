## Fully Funded Turkish Government Scholarships for Refugee Students in Australia
The Turkish Government Scholarships are available to refugee students in Australia, providing a unique opportunity to pursue undergraduate and graduate studies in Turkey with full funding. 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Turkish Government Scholarships, refugee students in Australia must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Permanent Humanitarian Visa (subclass 201)
* Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790)
* Temporary Humanitarian Concern Visa (subclass 449)
* Permanent Protection Visa (subclass 866)

## Available Scholarships
### Turkey Scholarships for Refugees
The Turkey Scholarships for Refugees program offers a coverage amount of $800 per month, and eligible students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is February 20, 2027. For more information, visit the official website: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/

The Turkish Government Graduate Scholarships provide a coverage amount of $1,000 per month, and eligible students must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.5.
