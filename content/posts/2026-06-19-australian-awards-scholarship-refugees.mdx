## Introduction to Fully Funded Australian Awards Scholarship
The Australian government offers a range of fully funded scholarships to asylum seekers and refugees, providing them with the opportunity to pursue vocational education and training in Australia. The Fully Funded Australian Awards Scholarship is a great way for asylum seekers and refugees to gain the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their chosen career. By providing full financial support, these scholarships enable recipients to focus on their studies and achieve their goals.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Fully Funded Australian Awards Scholarship, asylum seekers and refugees must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790)
* Permanent Protection Visa (subclass 866)
* Humanitarian Stay Visa (subclass 449)
* Refugee Visa (subclass 200, 201, 203, 204)

## Available Scholarships
The Australian government offers a range of scholarships to asylum seekers and refugees, including:
### Australian Awards Scholarship
The Australian Awards Scholarship provides full funding for vocational education and training, including tuition fees, living expenses, and other costs.
