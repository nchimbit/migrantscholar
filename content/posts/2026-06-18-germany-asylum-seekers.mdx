## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for fully funded Master's scholarships in Germany as an asylum seeker, you must fall under one of the following visa categories:
* Subsidiary protection
* Refugee status
* Asylum seeker with a temporary residence permit
* Person with a tolerated stay permit
* Person with a humanitarian residence permit
