## Fully Funded Australian Awards for International Students from Turkey
International students from Turkey are eligible to apply for the Fully Funded Australian Awards, which provide opportunities to pursue Bachelors, Masters, and PhD degrees in Australia.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Fully Funded Australian Awards, international students from Turkey must meet the following visa categories:
* SCV (Special Category Visa)
* SHEV (Safe Haven Enterprise Visa)
* TPV (Temporary Protection Visa)
* PV (Protection Visa)
* Refugee Visa
* Humanitarian Stay Visa

The Endeavour Leadership Program offers a coverage amount of up to AUD 272,500, and is eligible for PhD, Masters, and Bachelors students. The deadline for application is June 2027. For more information, visit: https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/

The Australian Awards Scholarships provide a coverage amount of up to AUD 30,000 per year, and are eligible for PhD, Masters, and Bachelors students. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit: https://www.australianawards.gov.au/

The Research Training Program International Scholarship offers a coverage amount of AUD 28,597 per year, and is eligible for PhD and Masters students. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit: https://www.unsw.edu.au/
