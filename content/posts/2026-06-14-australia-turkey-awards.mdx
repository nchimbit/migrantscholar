## Introduction to Australian Awards for Turkey
If you are a Turkish asylum seeker looking to pursue higher education in Australia, you may be eligible for the Australian Awards for Turkey, which offer fully funded scholarships for undergraduate and postgraduate studies in Australian universities. The Australian government provides these scholarships to support the development of Turkey and promote people-to-people links between the two countries. By applying for these scholarships, Turkish asylum seekers can gain access to world-class education and improve their future career prospects.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Australian Awards for Turkey, applicants must hold a valid visa that falls under one of the following categories:
* Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790)
* Permanent Protection Visa (subclass 866)
* Humanitarian Stay Visa (subclass 449)
* Resolution of Status Visa (subclass 851)
* Protection Visa (subclass 866)
Applicants must also meet the specific eligibility criteria for each scholarship, which may include academic performance, language proficiency, and other requirements.

## Available Scholarships
The following scholarships are available to Turkish asylum seekers:
### Australia Awards Scholarships
The Australia Awards Scholarships provide full tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs to successful applicants, with a coverage amount of up to AUD 30,000 per year. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.0, meet the English language requirements, and apply by the deadline of 30 April 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/).
### Endeavour Scholarships and Fellowships
The Endeavour Scholarships and Fellowships offer a coverage amount of up to AUD 140,000 for a two-year master's degree, and AUD 280,000 for a three-year doctoral degree. Eligible applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.5, meet the English language requirements, and apply by the deadline of 15 June 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/Endeavour](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/Endeavour).
### Research Training Program (RTP) Scholarships
The Research Training Program (RTP) Scholarships provide a coverage amount of up to AUD 28,597 per year for a maximum of three years. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.5, meet the English language requirements, and apply by the deadline of 31 October 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.education.gov.au/research-training-program](https://www.education.gov.au/research-training-program).
### University of Melbourne Scholarships
The University of Melbourne Scholarships offer a coverage amount of up to AUD 30,000 per year for a maximum of two years. Eligible applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.0, meet the English language requirements, and apply by the deadline of 31 December 2026. For more information, visit [https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/](https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/).
### Australian National University (ANU) Scholarships
The Australian National University (ANU) Scholarships provide a coverage amount of up to AUD 28,597 per year for a maximum of three years. To be eligible, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 3.5, meet the English language requirements, and apply by the deadline of 15 March 2027. For more information, visit [https://www.anu.edu.au/study/scholarships](https://www.anu.edu.au/study/scholarships).

To apply for the Australian Awards for Turkey, follow these steps:
