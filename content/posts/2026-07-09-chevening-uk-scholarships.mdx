## UK Chevening Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Refugee Leaders
The UK Chevening Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Refugee Leaders are available to individuals holding specific visa categories, including those who have been granted refugee status or are seeking asylum in the UK, to pursue a one-year master's degree in the UK, focusing on leadership development to empower them to create positive change in their communities.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the UK Chevening Scholarships, applicants must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Refugee status
* Humanitarian Protection
* Discretionary Leave
* Leave outside the Immigration Rules
* UASC Leave
* SECTION 67 Leave
* SECTION 4 Leave

## Available Scholarships
The following scholarships are available for asylum seekers and refugee leaders in the UK:
### Chevening Scholarship
The Chevening Scholarship covers a maximum of £18,000, and applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum of 2:1, and at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is usually in November of each year. For more information, please visit the official website at https://www.chevening.org.
### Commonwealth Shared Scholarship
The Commonwealth Shared Scholarship covers full tuition fees and living costs, and applicants must be a citizen of a Commonwealth country and have a minimum of 2:1 bachelor's degree. The deadline for application varies, but it is usually between February and May. For more information, please visit the official website at https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk.
### Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarship
The Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarship covers full tuition fees and living costs, and applicants must be a citizen of a developing country and have a minimum of 2:1 bachelor's degree. The deadline for application is usually in January of each year. For more information, please visit the official website at https://www.graduate.ox.ac.uk/weidenfeld-hoffmann.
### Said Foundation Scholarship
The Said Foundation Scholarship covers full tuition fees and living costs, and applicants must be a citizen of Jordan, Lebanon, Palestine, or Syria, and have a minimum of 2:1 bachelor's degree. The deadline for application varies, but it is usually between December and March. For more information, please visit the official website at https://www.saidfoundation.org.
### Alistair Cooke Scholarship
The Alistair Cooke Scholarship covers full tuition fees and living costs, and applicants must be a US citizen and have a minimum of 2:1 bachelor's degree. The deadline for application is usually in November of each year. For more information, please visit the official website at https://www.marshallscholarship.org.
