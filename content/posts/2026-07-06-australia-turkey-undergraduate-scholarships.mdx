## Australia Awards Scholarships for Undergraduate Studies for Migrant Students from Turkey
The Australia Awards Scholarships for undergraduate studies are available to migrant students from Turkey who hold specific visa categories, providing them with the opportunity to pursue their higher education in Australia.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Australia Awards Scholarships, migrant students from Turkey must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Temporary Graduate visa (subclass 485)
* Skilled Independent visa (subclass 189)
* Skilled Nominated visa (subclass 190)
* Skilled Regional (Provisional) visa (subclass 489)
* Temporary Skilled Migration visa (subclass 457)

## Available Scholarships
### Endeavour Scholarship
The Endeavour Scholarship provides AU$140,000 per year for the duration of the undergraduate degree, and is eligible to students who have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and meet the language requirements. The deadline for application is 30 June 2027. For more information, visit https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/.

### Australia Awards Scholarship
The Australia Awards Scholarship covers full tuition fees and provides a living allowance of AU$30,000 per year for the duration of the undergraduate degree. Eligible students must hold a minimum GPA of 3.0 and meet the language requirements. The deadline for application is 15 April 2027. For more information, visit https://www.dfat.gov.au/australia-awards.

### International Student Scholarship
The International Student Scholarship provides AU$20,000 per year for the duration of the undergraduate degree, and is eligible to students who have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and meet the language requirements. The deadline for application is 31 October 2026. For more information, visit https://www.studyinaustralia.gov.au/.

### Vice-Chancellor's Scholarship
The Vice-Chancellor's Scholarship provides AU$15,000 per year for the duration of the undergraduate degree, and is eligible to students who have a minimum GPA of 2.0 and meet the language requirements. The deadline for application is 30 September 2026. For more information, visit https://www.universityofmelbourne.com.au/.

### Deans Scholarship
The Deans Scholarship provides AU$10,000 per year for the duration of the undergraduate degree, and is eligible to students who have a minimum GPA of 1.5 and meet the language requirements. The deadline for application is 31 August 2026. For more information, visit https://www.anu.edu.au/.

## Application Steps
1. Check the eligibility criteria for the scholarship and ensure that you meet the requirements.
2. Choose the scholarship that you wish to apply for and read the application guidelines carefully.
3. Prepare all the required documents, including academic transcripts, language proficiency test results, and identification documents.
4. Submit your application through the online portal or by mail, depending on the scholarship requirements.
5. Wait for the application to be processed and reviewed by the scholarship committee.
6. If your application is successful, you will receive an offer letter and a scholarship agreement to sign.
7. Review and sign the scholarship agreement, and return it to the scholarship provider.
8. Receive the scholarship payment and commence your undergraduate studies in Australia.
