The Mitacs Globalink Research Internship is a fully funded opportunity for international students in STEM fields, providing a funding amount of $6,000 to cover living expenses, with applicants from Australia, Brazil, China, France, Germany, India, Japan, Mexico, Saudi Arabia, South Korea, Tunisia, Ukraine, United Kingdom, United States, and Vietnam eligible to apply.

## Who Qualifies
The Mitacs Globalink Research Internship is open to international students who are enrolled in a undergraduate or graduate program in a STEM field, with the following visa categories eligible to apply:
- Student visa
- Work visa
- Research visa
Applicants must be proficient in English or French, with a minimum language proficiency score of IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90.

## Available Scholarships
The following are five fully funded scholarships for international students in STEM fields in Canada:
### Mitacs Globalink Research Internship
- Amount: $6,000
- Eligibility: International students enrolled in a undergraduate or graduate program in a STEM field
- Deadline: Varies
- Official URL: https://www.mitacs.ca/en/programs/globalink/globalink-research-internship
### Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship
- Amount: $50,000 per year
- Eligibility: International students pursuing a graduate degree in Canada
- Deadline: November 1
- Official URL: https://www.canada.ca/en/research-funding-programs/funding-opportunities/fellowships/scholarships/vanier.html
### Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council (NSERC) Scholarship
- Amount: $17,500 to $21,000 per year
- Eligibility: International students pursuing a graduate degree in a STEM field in Canada
- Deadline: October 1
- Official URL: https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PG-CS/index_eng.asp
### Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council (SSHRC) Scholarship
- Amount: $20,000 to $50,000 per year
- Eligibility: International students pursuing a graduate degree in a social sciences or humanities field in Canada
- Deadline: October 1
- Official URL: https://www.sshrc-crsh.gc.ca/funding-financement/programs-programmes/scholarships-bourses/index-eng.aspx
### Ontario Graduate Scholarship (OGS) Program
- Amount: $15,000 to $30,000 per year
- Eligibility: International students pursuing a graduate degree in Ontario
- Deadline: Varies
- Official URL: https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-graduate-scholarship-program

## Application Steps
1. Research and select a potential supervisor and research project
2. Review the eligibility criteria and application guidelines
3. Submit an online application through the Mitacs website
4. Upload required documents, including transcripts and language proficiency scores
5. Submit a research proposal and personal statement
6. Provide two letters of reference from academic or professional supervisors
7. Apply for a student visa, if required
8. Confirm acceptance of the internship and make travel arrangements
