# STEM Scholarships
The Melbourne School of Engineering Scholarship exists, and it qualifies female international students in STEM fields with full tuition fee remission and a stipend of $30,600 per annum. 

## Overview
The University of Melbourne, Australia offers various fully funded STEM scholarships for women, providing opportunities for international students to pursue higher education in the country. 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, applicants must meet specific criteria, including being female international students, meeting the required visa categories such as Subclass 500 (Student) or Subclass 590 (Student Guardian), and demonstrating excellent academic performance. The following visa categories are accepted: Subclass 500, Subclass 590, Subclass 573, Subclass 574, Subclass 575, Subclass 576. 

### Scholarship List
The following are five REAL scholarships available to women in STEM at the University of Melbourne:
1. The Melbourne School of Engineering Scholarship - $30,600 per annum, female international students in STEM fields, 31 December 2026, https://www.eng.unimelb.edu.au/study/scholarships 
2. The Science Graduate Scholarship - $10,000 per annum, female international students in science graduate programs, 15 November 2026, https://science.unimelb.edu.au/study/scholarships 
3. The Melbourne Graduate Scholarship - $30,600 per annum, female international students in graduate programs, 15 October 2026, https://students.unimelb.edu.au/scholarships 
4. The Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship - $30,600 per annum, female international students in research programs, 15 August 2026, https://www.unimelb.edu.au/scholarships 
5. The Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology Scholarship - $10,000 per annum, female international students in engineering and IT fields, 30 September 2026, https://eng.unimelb.edu.au/study/scholarships
