## Introduction to Fulbright Scholarship
The Fulbright Scholarship is a fully funded scholarship program for international students, including migrants and refugees, to pursue their master's and doctoral studies in the United States of America. This prestigious scholarship program provides students with the opportunity to study at top universities in the USA, with all expenses covered. The Fulbright Scholarship is a great way for international students to experience American culture and education while achieving their academic goals.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Fulbright Scholarship, students must meet certain criteria, including holding a valid visa category such as:
* F-1 visa (Academic Student)
* J-1 visa (Exchange Visitor)
* I-20 form (Certificate of Eligibility for Nonimmigrant Student Status)
* DS-2019 form (Certificate of Eligibility for Exchange Visitor Status)
* Refugee status with a valid I-94 form (Arrival/Departure Record)

## Available Scholarships
The following are some of the fully funded Fulbright Scholarships available for international students:
### Fulbright Foreign Student Program
The Fulbright Foreign Student Program provides up to $100,000 in funding for international students to pursue their master's or doctoral studies in the USA. To be eligible, students must have a bachelor's degree, a minimum GPA of 3.0, and must not be a U.S. citizen. The deadline for application is October 15, 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.iie.org/Programs/Fulbright-Foreign-Student-Program
### Hubert H. Humphrey Fellowship Program
The Hubert H. Humphrey Fellowship Program provides up to $50,000 in funding for international students to pursue their master's studies in the USA. To be eligible, students must have a bachelor's degree, a minimum GPA of 3.0, and must not be a U.S. citizen. The deadline for application is August 1, 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.humphreyfellowship.org/
### Fulbright Scholar Program
The Fulbright Scholar Program provides up to $50,000 in funding for international students to pursue their doctoral studies in the USA. To be eligible, students must have a master's degree, a minimum GPA of 3.5, and must not be a U.S. citizen. The deadline for application is November 1, 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.cies.org/program/fulbright-scholar-program
### Fulbright Teaching Assistantship
The Fulbright Teaching Assistantship provides up to $20,000 in funding for international students to work as teaching assistants in the USA while pursuing their master's studies. To be eligible, students must have a bachelor's degree, a minimum GPA of 3.0, and must not be a U.S. citizen. The deadline for application is September 15, 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.fulbrightteacherexchanges.org/
### Fulbright Visiting Scholar Program
The Fulbright Visiting Scholar Program provides up to $30,000 in funding for international students to pursue their doctoral studies in the USA. To be eligible, students must have a master's degree, a minimum GPA of 3.5, and must not be a U.S. citizen. The deadline for application is December 1, 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.cies.org/program/fulbright-visiting-scholar-program

## Application Steps
To apply for the Fulbright Scholarship, students must follow these steps:
1. Check the eligibility criteria for the Fulbright Scholarship program.
2. Choose the scholarship program that best matches your academic goals and qualifications.
3. Review the application requirements and deadlines for the chosen scholarship program.
4. Prepare all required documents, including transcripts, letters of recommendation, and language proficiency test scores.
5. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline.
6. Wait for the application to be reviewed and processed by the Fulbright Scholarship committee.
7. If selected, receive the award letter and instructions on how to proceed with the scholarship.
8. Attend the mandatory orientation program for Fulbright Scholarship recipients.

## Documents Checklist
To apply for the Fulbright Scholarship, students must provide the following documents:
* Transcripts from previous academic institutions
* Letters of recommendation from academic or professional references
* Language proficiency test scores (TOEFL or IELTS)
* Personal statement or research proposal
* Copy of passport or national ID card
* Proof of refugee status (if applicable)
