## Germany's DAAD Scholarships for Migrant Students and Refugees
The DAAD Scholarship is available for migrant students and refugees pursuing PhD studies in Germany, providing them with a unique opportunity to further their academic careers in a renowned academic environment. 
### Who Qualifies
To qualify for the DAAD Scholarship, migrant students and refugees must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* Subsidiary protection
* Refugee status
* Asylum seeker
* Temporary permission to stay
* Temporary toleration
* Humanitarian residence permit
* Other categories may apply, and applicants are advised to check with the relevant authorities for the most up-to-date information.

## Available Scholarships
There are several scholarships available for migrant students and refugees in Germany, including:
### Development-Related Postgraduate Courses (DRP)
The Development-Related Postgraduate Courses (DRP) scholarship covers a monthly stipend of 850 euros, plus travel and tuition fees, and is open to applicants with a bachelor's degree and at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is August 2026, and more information can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Master's Scholarships for Public Policy and Good Governance (PPGG)
The Master's Scholarships for Public Policy and Good Governance (PPGG) covers a monthly stipend of 850 euros, plus travel and tuition fees, and is open to applicants with a bachelor's degree and at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is July 2026, and more information can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Helmut-Schmidt-Programme (HSP)
The Helmut-Schmidt-Programme (HSP) covers a monthly stipend of 850 euros, plus travel and tuition fees, and is open to applicants with a bachelor's degree and at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is August 2026, and more information can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Scholarship for PhD in Germany
The Scholarship for PhD in Germany covers a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros, plus travel and tuition fees, and is open to applicants with a master's degree. The deadline for application is November 2026, and more information can be found at https://www.daad.de/en/.
### Research Grant for Doctoral Candidates and Young Academics and Scientists (Bi-national)
The Research Grant for Doctoral Candidates and Young Academics and Scientists (Bi-national) covers a monthly stipend of 1,200 euros, plus travel and tuition fees, and is open to applicants with a master's degree.
