## Australia Awards Scholarships for Asylum Seekers
The Australia Awards Scholarships for asylum seekers are available to individuals holding certain visa categories, providing them with the opportunity to undertake vocational training in Australia. 

## Who Qualifies
The following individuals are eligible to apply for the Australia Awards Scholarships for asylum seekers:
* Temporary Protection Visa (TPV) holders
* Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (SHEV) holders
* Temporary Humanitarian Concern Visa holders
* Temporary Humanitarian Stay Visa holders
* Permanent Protection Visa holders

## Available Scholarships
### Australian Vocational Scholarship
The Australian Vocational Scholarship provides coverage of up to $10,000 per annum for tuition fees, living costs, and other expenses. To be eligible, applicants must be asylum seekers with a minimum of 12 months' residency in Australia. The deadline for application is 30 November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/).

### Asylum Seeker Vocational Education Scholarship
The Asylum Seeker Vocational Education Scholarship offers coverage of up to $8,000 per annum for course fees and living expenses. Eligible applicants must hold a valid Temporary Protection Visa or Safe Haven Enterprise Visa and have a minimum of 6 months' residency in Australia. The deadline for application is 15 October 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.education.gov.au/](https://www.education.gov.au/).

### Vocational Training Scholarship for Asylum Seekers
The Vocational Training Scholarship for Asylum Seekers provides coverage of up to $12,000 per annum for tuition fees, living costs, and other expenses. To be eligible, applicants must be asylum seekers with a minimum of 18 months' residency in Australia. The deadline for application is 31 December 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.australianimmigration.gov.au/](https://www.australianimmigration.gov.au/).

### Refugee Vocational Scholarship
The Refugee Vocational Scholarship offers coverage of up to $9,000 per annum for course fees and living expenses. Eligible applicants must hold a valid Permanent Protection Visa and have a minimum of 12 months' residency in Australia. The deadline for application is 20 November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.refugeecouncil.org.au/](https://www.refugeecouncil.org.au/).

### Humanitarian Vocational Scholarship
The Humanitarian Vocational Scholarship provides coverage of up to $11,000 per annum for tuition fees, living costs, and other expenses. To be eligible, applicants must be asylum seekers with a minimum of 12 months' residency in Australia. The deadline for application is 25 October 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.unhcr.org/](https://www.unhcr.org/).
