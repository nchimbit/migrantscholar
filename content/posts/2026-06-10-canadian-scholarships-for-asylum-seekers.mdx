## Introduction to Canadian Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Refugee Students
As an asylum seeker or refugee student in Canada, you may be wondering if there are any partially funded Canadian scholarships available to support your education. The good news is that yes, there are several scholarships available to help you achieve your academic goals. These scholarships can provide financial assistance to help cover the costs of tuition, living expenses, and other educational-related expenses.

## Who Qualifies for Canadian Scholarships
To qualify for these scholarships, you must be an asylum seeker or refugee student who meets specific visa category requirements. The following visa categories are eligible:
* Asylum Claimant
* Convention Refugee
* Protected Person
* Refugee Claimant
* Temporary Resident Permit Holder
* Permanent Resident under the Refugee Category
* Government-Assisted Refugee
* Privately Sponsored Refugee

## Application Steps for Canadian Scholarships
To apply for these scholarships, follow these steps:
1. Research and identify the scholarships you are eligible for
2. Review the eligibility criteria and application requirements
3. Gather all required documents, including transcripts and language proficiency test results
4. Submit your application online or by mail, depending on the scholarship requirements
5. Ensure you meet the deadline for submission
6. Wait for the scholarship committee to review your application
7. Receive notification of the scholarship decision
8. Accept the scholarship offer and follow any additional instructions

## Documents Checklist for Canadian Scholarships
To apply for these scholarships, you will need to provide the following documents:
* Proof of refugee or asylum seeker status
* Proof of admission to a Canadian university
* Transcripts from previous academic institutions
* Language proficiency test results (e.g. IELTS or TOEFL)
* Personal statement or essay
* Letters of recommendation
* Proof of financial need
