## Fully Funded Chevening Scholarships for Migrant Students to Study in the UK
Migrant students from various countries are eligible for the fully funded Chevening Scholarships to pursue their Master's degree in the UK. 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Chevening Scholarships, migrant students must fall under one of the following visa categories:
* Tier 2 (General) visa
* Tier 2 (Intra-company Transfer) visa
* Tier 4 (General) student visa
* Tier 5 (Temporary Worker) visa
* Family visa
* Ancestry visa
* Other categories may apply, and it is recommended to check the official website for the most up-to-date information

## Available Scholarships
### Chevening Scholarship
The Chevening Scholarship covers a maximum of £18,000, and eligibility includes having at least two years of work experience and a strong academic background. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [www.chevening.org](http://www.chevening.org).
### Felix Scholarship
The Felix Scholarship covers full tuition fees of up to £18,000, and eligibility includes being a citizen of a developing country and having a strong academic record. The deadline for application is January 2027. For more information, visit [www.felix-scholarship.org](http://www.felix-scholarship.org).
### Commonwealth Shared Scholarship
The Commonwealth Shared Scholarship covers full tuition fees of up to £12,000, and eligibility includes being a citizen of a Commonwealth country and having a strong academic record. The deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, visit [https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk](https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk).
### Gates Cambridge Scholarship
The Gates Cambridge Scholarship covers full tuition fees of up to £15,000, and eligibility includes having a strong academic record and being a citizen of any country outside the UK. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit [www.gatescambridge.org](http://www.gatescambridge.org).
### Rhodes Scholarship
The Rhodes Scholarship covers full tuition fees of up to £15,000, and eligibility includes having a strong academic record and being a citizen of certain countries. The deadline for application is October 2026. For more information, visit [www.rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk](http://www.rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk).

## Application Steps
To apply for the Chevening Scholarships, migrant students must follow these steps:
1. Check their eligibility and the eligibility criteria for their chosen course.
