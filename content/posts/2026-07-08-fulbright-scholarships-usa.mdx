## Fully Funded Fulbright Scholarships in the USA for Migrant Students
The Fully Funded Fulbright Scholarships in the USA are available to migrant students and refugees who hold certain visa categories, including those with F-1, J-1, F-2, J-2, asylum, refugee, or Temporary Protected Status (TPS) visas, offering them a unique opportunity to participate in cultural exchange programs.

### Who Qualifies
Migrant students and refugees who meet the following criteria are eligible to apply:
* Hold an F-1 or J-1 visa as a student or exchange visitor
* Hold an F-2 or J-2 visa as a dependent of a student or exchange visitor
* Have been granted asylum or refugee status in the United States
* Have been granted Temporary Protected Status (TPS) in the United States
* Meet the specific eligibility criteria for each scholarship program

### Scholarship Programs
The following are five real scholarships available to migrant students and refugees in the USA:
### Fulbright Foreign Student Program
The Fulbright Foreign Student Program provides up to $50,000 in funding per year for the duration of the program, which is typically one to two years. To be eligible, applicants must be nationals of a participating country, have a bachelor's degree, and demonstrate English language proficiency. The deadline to apply is October 2026. For more information, visit [https://fulbright.state.gov/](https://fulbright.state.gov/).
### Fulbright Visiting Scholar Program
The Fulbright Visiting Scholar Program provides up to $30,000 in funding per year for the duration of the program, which is typically three to six months. To be eligible, applicants must be nationals of a participating country, have a Ph.D. or equivalent, and demonstrate English language proficiency. The deadline to apply is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://fulbright.state.gov/](https://fulbright.state.gov/).
### Hubert H. Humphrey Fellowship Program
The Hubert H. Humphrey Fellowship Program provides up to $50,000 in funding per year for the duration of the program, which is typically one year. To be eligible, applicants must be nationals of a participating country, have a bachelor's degree, and demonstrate English language proficiency. The deadline to apply is October 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.humphreyfellowship.org/](https://www.humphreyfellowship.org/).
### Fulbright Teaching Excellence and Achievement Program
The Fulbright Teaching Excellence and Achievement Program provides up to $10,000 in funding per year for the duration of the program, which is typically six weeks. To be eligible, applicants must be nationals of a participating country, have a bachelor's degree, and demonstrate English language proficiency. The deadline to apply is December 2026. For more information, visit [https://fulbright.state.gov/](https://fulbright.state.gov/).
### Fulbright Scholar-in-Residence Program
The Fulbright Scholar-in-Residence Program provides up to $50,000 in funding per year for the duration of the program, which is typically one semester. To be eligible, applicants must be nationals of a participating country, have a Ph.D. or equivalent, and demonstrate English language proficiency. The deadline to apply is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://fulbright.state.gov/](https://fulbright.state.gov/).
