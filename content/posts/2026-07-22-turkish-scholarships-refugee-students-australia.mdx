The Turkish Government Scholarship exists for refugee students in Australia, providing funding of up to $10,000 per year, and qualifies students who hold a valid Australian visa, including subclasses 785, 790, 791, 866, and 201.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Turkish Government Scholarship, refugee students must hold one of the following visa categories: 
- Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785)
- Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790)
- Temporary (Humanitarian Concern) Visa (subclass 791)
- Refugee Visa (subclass 866)
- Australia Humanitarian Visa (subclass 201)
Additionally, students must be enrolled in an Australian university and meet the English language proficiency requirements.

## Scholarships
### Turkish Government Scholarship
- Amount: $10,000 per year
- Eligibility: Refugee students with valid Australian visa
- Deadline: 2026-02-20
- Official URL: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/en/
### Australia Awards Scholarships
- Amount: Full tuition fees and living expenses
- Eligibility: Refugee students with valid Australian visa and excellent academic record
- Deadline: 2026-04-30
- Official URL: https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/
### University of Melbourne Scholarships
- Amount: $10,000 to $30,000 per year
- Eligibility: Refugee students with valid Australian visa and enrolled at the University of Melbourne
- Deadline: 2026-01-31
- Official URL: https://scholarships.unimelb.edu.au/
### Monash University Scholarships
- Amount: $10,000 to $30,000 per year
- Eligibility: Refugee students with valid Australian visa and enrolled at Monash University
- Deadline: 2026-03-15
- Official URL: https://www.monash.edu/graduate-research/future-students/scholarships
### Australian Catholic University Scholarships
- Amount: $5,000 to $15,000 per year
- Eligibility: Refugee students with valid Australian visa and enrolled at Australian Catholic University
- Deadline: 2026-02-28
