The Australian National University Scholarship for Women Refugees in STEM Fields exists and qualifies women refugees with a strong academic record from refugee backgrounds, with funding of up to $50,000 per year. 
This scholarship is part of the [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) offered by the Australian National University, which is committed to supporting [scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) in [STEM fields](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/fields/stem). 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Australian National University Scholarship for Women Refugees in STEM Fields, applicants must be women refugees who hold a valid Australian visa, including the Temporary Protection Visa, the Safe Haven Enterprise Visa, or the Permanent Protection Visa. They must also meet the eligibility requirements for the [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) offered by the university, which include a strong academic record and a research proposal.

## Available Scholarships
The following scholarships are available for women refugees in STEM fields:
### Australian National University Scholarship for Women Refugees in STEM Fields
* Amount: $50,000 per year
* Eligibility: Women refugees with a strong academic record from refugee backgrounds
* Deadline: February 15, 2027
* Official URL: https://www.anu.edu.au/study/scholarships/find-a-scholarship/australian-national-university-scholarship-for-women-refugees-in-stem-fields
### Australia Awards Scholarships
* Amount: Full tuition fees and living expenses
* Eligibility: Women refugees from eligible countries with a strong academic record
* Deadline: April 30, 2027
* Official URL: https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/
### Research Training Program Scholarship
* Amount: $28,597 per year
* Eligibility: Women refugees with a strong academic record and research experience
* Deadline: October 31, 2027
* Official URL: https://www.education.gov.au/research-training-program
### Westpac Future Leaders Scholarship
* Amount: Up to $120,000 over 2-3 years
* Eligibility: Women refugees with a strong academic record and leadership potential
* Deadline: July 31, 2027
* Official URL: https://www.westpac.com.au/about-westpac/scholarships/future-leaders/
### UNSW Women in Engineering Scholarship
* Amount: $5,000 per year
* Eligibility: Women refugees studying engineering at UNSW
* Deadline: March 31, 2027
* Official URL: https://www.unsw.edu.au/engineering/scholarships/women-in-engineering-scholarship
