# Emergency Aid
The University of Toronto's Emergency Loan Fund exists and qualifies migrant students with a financial crisis, providing up to $2,000 in funding. 

## Who Qualifies
Migrant students with a valid Canadian study permit, including those with a work permit under the Post-Graduation Work Permit Program (PGWP), a work permit under the Temporary Foreign Worker Program (TFWP), a work permit under the International Mobility Program (IMP), those sponsored by their government or an external funding agency, those in Canada as a protected person, and those in the process of claiming refugee status may be eligible for emergency funding. 

## Scholarship Options
### University of Toronto Emergency Loan Fund
Amount: up to $2,000, Eligibility: Migrant students with a valid Canadian study permit and financial crisis, Deadline: Ongoing, [https://www.studentlife.utoronto.ca/careers-employment/emergency-loan-fund](https://www.studentlife.utoronto.ca/careers-employment/emergency-loan-fund)
### Ontario Student Assistance Program (OSAP)
Amount: varies, Eligibility: Migrant students with a valid Canadian study permit and financial need, Deadline: Ongoing, [https://www.ontario.ca/page/osap-ontario-student-assistance-program](https://www.ontario.ca/page/osap-ontario-student-assistance-program)
### University of Toronto Scholars Program
Amount: $7,500, Eligibility: Migrant students with a valid Canadian study permit and academic excellence, Deadline: February 1, [https://future.utoronto.ca/scholarships/university-toronto-scholars-program/](https://future.utoronto.ca/scholarships/university-toronto-scholars-program/)
### Karen McKenna Memorial Scholarship
Amount: $1,000, Eligibility: Migrant students with a valid Canadian study permit and financial need, Deadline: February 15, [https://www.uoft.me/karen-mckenna-memorial-scholarship](https://www.uoft.me/karen-mckenna-memorial-scholarship)
### Canada Graduate Scholarships-Master's Program
Amount: $17,500, Eligibility: Migrant students with a valid Canadian study permit and research-based master's program, Deadline: December 1, [https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/CGSM-BESCM_eng.asp](https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/CGSM-BESCM_eng.asp)
