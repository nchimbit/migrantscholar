## Introduction to Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarships
The Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarships are available for refugee students pursuing doctoral studies in Canada. These scholarships are designed to support students who have been displaced due to conflict, persecution, or natural disasters and are now seeking to continue their education in Canada. The scholarships provide full tuition plus living costs, enabling refugee students to focus on their studies and achieve their academic goals.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarships, refugee students must hold a valid Canadian visa in one of the following categories:
* Protected Person (PP)
* Convention Refugee (CR)
* Country of Asylum (COA)
* Refugee Claimant (RC)
* Permanent Resident (PR) with prior refugee status
* Temporary Resident Permit (TRP) holder with prior refugee status
* other visa categories as specified by the scholarship provider

## Available Scholarships
The following are five scholarships available to refugee students in Canada:
### Trudeau Scholarship
The Trudeau Scholarship provides $40,000 per year for up to three years, covering tuition fees and living costs. Eligible students must be pursuing a doctoral degree at a Canadian university and demonstrate a commitment to social justice and human rights. The deadline for applications is November 15, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/en/programs/scholarships.
### Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship
The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship provides $50,000 per year for up to three years, covering tuition fees and living costs. Eligible students must be pursuing a doctoral degree at a Canadian university and demonstrate academic excellence and research potential. The deadline for applications is November 1, 2026. For more information, visit https://vanier.gc.ca/en/home-accueil.html.
### Joseph-Armand Bombardier Scholarship
The Joseph-Armand Bombardier Scholarship provides $35,000 per year for up to two years, covering tuition fees and living costs. Eligible students must be pursuing a doctoral degree at a Canadian university and demonstrate a commitment to social sciences and humanities research. The deadline for applications is October 15, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.sshrc-crsh.gc.ca/funding-financement/programs-programmes/fellowships/bombardier/index-eng.aspx.
### Ontario Trillium Scholarship
The Ontario Trillium Scholarship provides $40,000 per year for up to four years, covering tuition fees and living costs. Eligible students must be pursuing a doctoral degree at an Ontario university and demonstrate academic excellence and research potential. The deadline for applications is October 1, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-trillium-scholarship.
### University of Toronto Scholarship
The University of Toronto Scholarship provides $30,000 per year for up to four years, covering tuition fees and living costs. Eligible students must be pursuing a doctoral degree at the University of Toronto and demonstrate academic excellence and research potential. The deadline for applications is September 15, 2026. For more information, visit https://www.sgs.utoronto.ca/funding/graduate-funding-competition/.
