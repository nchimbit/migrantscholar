The DAAD Scholarship qualifies refugee women in Turkey pursuing higher education with a funding amount of up to €750 per month. Refugee women in Turkey who have been granted international protection or are holding a residence permit can apply for this scholarship to support their academic pursuits. 

## Who Qualifies
Refugee women who are holders of a residence permit, international protection, or humanitarian residence permit in Turkey are eligible to apply for emergency funding for higher education. The specific visa categories that qualify include:

* International protection
* Humanitarian residence
* Residence permit
* Temporary protection

These categories of refugee women in Turkey can pursue [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) and other funding opportunities to support their academic goals.

## Available Scholarships
### DAAD Scholarship
* Amount: up to €750 per month
* Eligibility: Refugee women in Turkey holding a residence permit or international protection
* Deadline: Varies depending on the course
* Official URL: https://www.daad.de/
### UNHCR DAFI Scholarship
* Amount: Up to $2,200 per year
* Eligibility: Refugee women in Turkey with a valid refugee ID
* Deadline: May 31st
* Official URL: https://www.unhcr.org/
### MasterCard Foundation Scholars Program
* Amount: Full tuition and living expenses
* Eligibility: Refugee women in Turkey with a strong academic record
* Deadline: Varies depending on the university
* Official URL: https://mastercardfdn.org/
### Chevening Scholarship
* Amount: Full tuition and living expenses
* Eligibility: Refugee women in Turkey with a strong academic record and leadership skills
* Deadline: November 1st
* Official URL: https://www.chevening.org/
### Fulbright Scholarship
* Amount: Full tuition and living expenses
* Eligibility: Refugee women in Turkey with a strong academic record and English proficiency
* Deadline: October 15th
* Official URL: https://fulbright.edu.tw/

These scholarships can be a valuable resource for refugee women in Turkey, especially those interested in pursuing [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) or [scholarships for refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees).

## Application Steps
1. Research and select a scholarship that fits your academic goals and eligibility criteria.
2. Review the official URL and application guidelines for the selected scholarship.
3. Gather all the required documents, including transcripts and language proficiency test scores.
4. Fill out the application form and submit it before the specified deadline.
5. Prepare a personal statement or essay as required by the scholarship application.
6. Secure letters of recommendation from academic or professional referees.
7. Submit the application and wait for the review process to be completed.
8. Follow up on the application status and be prepared to provide additional information if required.

## Documents Checklist
* Valid passport
* Residence permit or international protection ID
* Academic transcripts
* Language proficiency test scores (e.g. TOEFL or IELTS)
* Personal statement or essay
* Letters of recommendation
* Proof of financial need (if applicable)
