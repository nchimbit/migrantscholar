## Turkish Government Scholarships for Migrant Students in Turkey
The Turkish Government Scholarships are available to migrant students in Turkey who are pursuing graduate studies, including those with international protection or asylum seeker status, and can provide financial support of up to 1,500 TL per month.

## Who Qualifies
Migrant students who meet the following criteria are eligible to apply for the Turkish Government Scholarships:
* International protection applicants
* Asylum seekers
* Conditional refugees
* Refugees
* Stateless individuals
* Migrant students with a valid residence permit in Turkey

## Available Scholarships
### Turkey Scholarships for Graduate Studies
The Turkey Scholarships for Graduate Studies provides 1,500 TL per month for 12 months, and is open to migrant students who are currently enrolled in a graduate program in Turkey. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 3.0, and the deadline for application is February 2027. For more information, please visit: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/

### Jean Monnet Scholarship Programme
The Jean Monnet Scholarship Programme offers 4,800 TL per month for 12 months, and is available to migrant students who are pursuing a master's or doctoral degree in the field of EU studies. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 3.5, and the deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, please visit: https://www.csb.gov.tr/

### Turkish Language Scholarships
The Turkish Language Scholarships provides 1,000 TL per month for 6 months, and is open to migrant students who want to learn Turkish language and culture. The eligibility criteria include a minimum age of 18, and the deadline for application is June 2027. For more information, please visit: https://www.turkishlanguage.turkey.gov.tr/

### Research Scholarships
The Research Scholarships offers 2,000 TL per month for 12 months, and is available to migrant students who are pursuing a research project in Turkey. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 3.0, and the deadline for application is March 2027. For more information, please visit: https://www.tubitak.gov.tr/

### PhD Scholarships
The PhD Scholarships provides 2,500 TL per month for 48 months, and is open to migrant students who are pursuing a doctoral degree in Turkey. The eligibility criteria include a minimum GPA of 3.5, and the deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, please visit: https://www.csb.gov.tr/
