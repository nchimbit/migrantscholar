Pakistani nationals can apply for various government scholarships to pursue their higher education in Turkey. These scholarships provide a great opportunity for international students to study in a foreign country with a rich history and culture. 

## Who Qualifies
Pakistani nationals with a valid passport can apply for these scholarships. The Turkish government offers various visa categories, including student visas, research visas, and cultural exchange visas. To be eligible, applicants must meet the requirements of the specific scholarship programme they are applying for, which may include academic achievements, language proficiency, and other criteria. ## Available Scholarships
### Türkiye Scholarships
* Amount: up to 1,600 TL per month
* Eligibility: Pakistani nationals with a high school diploma or equivalent
* Deadline: February to March
* Official URL: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/
### Islamic Development Bank Scholarship
* Amount: up to $25,000 per year
* Eligibility: Pakistani nationals with a bachelor's degree or equivalent
* Deadline: January to March
* Official URL: https://www.isdb.org/
### Turkiye Burslari Scholarship
* Amount: up to 1,600 TL per month
* Eligibility: Pakistani nationals with a high school diploma or equivalent
* Deadline: February to March
* Official URL: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/
### Kadir Has University Scholarship
* Amount: up to 100% tuition fee waiver
* Eligibility: Pakistani nationals with a bachelor's degree or equivalent
* Deadline: May to July
* Official URL: https://www.khas.edu.tr/
### YTB Scholarship
* Amount: up to 1,600 TL per month
* Eligibility: Pakistani nationals with a high school diploma or equivalent
* Deadline: February to March
* Official URL: https://www.ytb.gov.tr/

## Application Steps
1. Check the eligibility criteria for the scholarship programme you are applying for.
2. Prepare the required documents, including a valid passport, high school diploma or equivalent, and language proficiency certificate.
3. Fill out the online application form on the official website of the scholarship programme.
4. Upload the required documents and submit the application form.
5. Wait for the application to be reviewed and processed.
6. Receive an email notification with the application result.
7. If selected, apply for a student visa at the Turkish embassy or consulate in your home country.
8. Register at the university and start your academic programme.

## Documents Checklist
* Valid passport
