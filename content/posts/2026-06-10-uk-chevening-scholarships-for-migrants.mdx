The Chevening Scholarship is a fully funded scholarship that covers tuition fees, living costs, and other expenses up to £18,000. ### Gates Cambridge Scholarship
The Gates Cambridge Scholarship is a fully funded scholarship that covers tuition fees, living costs, and other expenses up to £17,000. The Rhodes Scholarship is a fully funded scholarship that covers tuition fees, living costs, and other expenses up to £15,000. ### Marshall Scholarship
The Marshall Scholarship is a fully funded scholarship that covers tuition fees, living costs, and other expenses up to £24,000.
