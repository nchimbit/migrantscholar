## Introduction to Fulbright Scholarships for Migrant Students
The Fulbright Scholarships are fully funded awards that provide migrant students with the opportunity to pursue postgraduate studies in the USA. These scholarships are designed to promote cultural exchange and academic excellence, and they cover all expenses related to studying in the USA, including tuition fees, living costs, and airfare. Migrant students who are interested in applying for these scholarships can find all the necessary information on this page.

## Eligibility Criteria
To be eligible for the Fulbright Scholarships, migrant students must meet certain criteria. The following visa categories are eligible to apply:
* F-1 visa holders
* F-2 visa holders
* J-1 visa holders
* J-2 visa holders
* Refugee status
* Asylee status
* Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
* Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA)

## Available Scholarships
The following are some of the Fulbright Scholarships available to migrant students in the USA:
### Fulbright Foreign Student Program
The Fulbright Foreign Student Program provides up to $100,000 in funding per year for the duration of the study. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree and a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is October 2026, and the official website is https://fulbright.americancouncils.org/.
### Fulbright Teaching Assistantship
The Fulbright Teaching Assistantship provides up to $20,000 in funding per year for the duration of the study. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree and a minimum GPA of 3.0, and they must be proficient in English. The deadline for application is November 2026, and the official website is https://www.fulbright_teacherexchanges.org/.
### Fulbright Research Grant
The Fulbright Research Grant provides up to $50,000 in funding per year for the duration of the study. To be eligible, applicants must have a master's degree and a minimum GPA of 3.5, and they must have a research proposal. The deadline for application is December 2026, and the official website is https://www.cies.org/.
### Fulbright MBA Scholarship
The Fulbright MBA Scholarship provides up to $100,000 in funding per year for the duration of the study. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree and a minimum GPA of 3.0, and they must have a minimum of two years of work experience. The deadline for application is January 2027, and the official website is https://www.usief.org.in/.
### Fulbright Law Scholarship
The Fulbright Law Scholarship provides up to $50,000 in funding per year for the duration of the study. To be eligible, applicants must have a law degree and a minimum GPA of 3.0, and they must have a minimum of two years of work experience. The deadline for application is March 2027, and the official website is https://www.fulbrightonline.org/.

## Application Process
To apply for the Fulbright Scholarships, migrant students must follow these steps:
1. Choose the scholarship program that best fits their needs and interests.
2. Review the eligibility criteria and ensure they meet all the requirements.
3. Prepare all the necessary documents, including transcripts, letters of recommendation, and a personal statement.
4. Fill out the online application form and submit it before the deadline.
5. Take the required standardized tests, such as the TOEFL or IELTS.
6. Prepare for and participate in an interview with the Fulbright committee.
7. Receive and review the admission decision and scholarship award.
8. Accept the scholarship award and prepare to begin studies in the USA.

## Documents Checklist
The following documents are required for the Fulbright Scholarship application:
* Transcripts from all previous academic institutions
* Letters of recommendation from academic or professional references
* Personal statement or research proposal
* English language proficiency test scores (TOEFL or IELTS)
* Standardized test scores (GRE or GMAT)
* Copy of passport or national ID
* Proof of refugee or asylee status (if applicable)
