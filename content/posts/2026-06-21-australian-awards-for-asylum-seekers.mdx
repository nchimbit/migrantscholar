## Fully Funded Australian Awards for Asylum Seekers and Refugees
The Australian government offers fully funded awards to asylum seekers and refugees who wish to pursue higher education in the country. These awards provide financial support to eligible students, covering tuition fees, living expenses, and other study-related costs. As an asylum seeker or refugee, you can apply for these awards to achieve your academic goals and build a better future for yourself.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Australian Awards, you must hold a valid visa that falls under one of the following categories:
* Permanent Protection visa (subclass 866)
* Temporary Protection visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise visa (subclass 790)
* Temporary Humanitarian Stay visa (subclass 449)
* Humanitarian Stay visa (subclass 449)
* Protection visa (subclass 866)
* Refugee visa (subclass 884)
