## Introduction to Fully Funded UK Chevening Scholarships for Refugee Students
If you are a refugee student looking to pursue a Master's degree in the UK, you may be wondering if you are eligible for the fully funded UK Chevening Scholarships. The answer is yes, refugee students can apply for these scholarships, which cover tuition fees, living costs, and other expenses. The UK Chevening Scholarships are a great opportunity for refugee students to study in the UK and achieve their academic goals.

## Who Qualifies for the UK Chevening Scholarships
To be eligible for the UK Chevening Scholarships, you must be a refugee with one of the following visa categories:
* Refugee status
* Humanitarian protection
* Discretionary leave
* Leave outside the rules
* Section 67 of the Immigration Act 2016
* Section 3C of the Immigration Act 1971
* Family reunion visa

## Available Scholarships for Refugee Students
The following are five fully funded UK Chevening Scholarships available to refugee students:
### Chevening Scholarship
The Chevening Scholarship covers up to £18,000 in living costs and £14,750 in tuition fees. To be eligible, you must have a bachelor's degree, be a refugee with one of the eligible visa categories, and have at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is November 1, 2026. For more information, visit [www.chevening.org](http://www.chevening.org).
### Commonwealth Shared Scholarship
The Commonwealth Shared Scholarship covers up to £14,750 in tuition fees and £1,200 in living costs per month. To be eligible, you must be a refugee with one of the eligible visa categories, have a bachelor's degree, and be under the age of 40. The deadline for application is December 1, 2026. For more information, visit [www.cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk](http://www.cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk).
### Scotland's Saltire Scholarship
The Scotland's Saltire Scholarship covers up to £8,000 in tuition fees. To be eligible, you must be a refugee with one of the eligible visa categories, have a bachelor's degree, and be under the age of 35. The deadline for application is May 31, 2026. For more information, visit [www.scotland.org](http://www.scotland.org).
### University of Oxford Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarship
The University of Oxford Weidenfeld-Hoffmann Scholarship covers up to £18,000 in living costs and £14,750 in tuition fees. To be eligible, you must be a refugee with one of the eligible visa categories, have a bachelor's degree, and have at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is January 6, 2027. For more information, visit [www.graduate.ox.ac.uk](http://www.graduate.ox.ac.uk).
### Imperial College London President's PhD Scholarship
The Imperial College London President's PhD Scholarship covers up to £21,000 in living costs and £14,750 in tuition fees. To be eligible, you must be a refugee with one of the eligible visa categories, have a master's degree, and have at least two years of work experience. The deadline for application is March 1, 2027. For more information, visit [www.imperial.ac.uk](http://www.imperial.ac.uk).
