## Introduction to Turkey Government Scholarships
Turkey government scholarships for asylum seekers and migrant students offer a unique opportunity to pursue graduate studies in a foreign country. The Turkish government provides various scholarship programs specifically designed for asylum seekers and migrant students, covering tuition fees, living expenses, and other benefits. These scholarships aim to support the educational and career goals of migrant students, enabling them to contribute to their home countries or integrate into Turkish society.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for Turkey government scholarships, asylum seekers and migrant students must belong to specific visa categories, including:
* Conditional refugee status
* Family residence permit
* Humanitarian residence permit
* Student residence permit
* Temporary protection
* International protection applicant
* Refugee status

## Available Scholarships
### Turkey Scholarship for Graduate Studies
The Turkey Scholarship for Graduate Studies covers 100% of tuition fees, provides a monthly stipend of 1,100 Turkish Lira, and includes health insurance. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0, be under 30 years old, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The application deadline is February 20, 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/

### Türkiye Scholarships for Asylum Seekers
The Türkiye Scholarships for Asylum Seekers program offers a monthly stipend of 1,000 Turkish Lira, 100% tuition fee coverage, and language course fees. Eligible applicants must have a conditional refugee status, be enrolled in a graduate program, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The application deadline is November 15, 2026, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/

### Research Scholarship Program
The Research Scholarship Program provides a monthly stipend of 2,500 Turkish Lira, 100% tuition fee coverage, and research funding of up to 10,000 Turkish Lira. Eligible applicants must have a master's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.5, be under 35 years old, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The application deadline is March 1, 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.tubitak.gov.tr/

### Graduate Scholarship Program for Migrant Students
The Graduate Scholarship Program for Migrant Students offers a monthly stipend of 1,200 Turkish Lira, 100% tuition fee coverage, and language course fees. Eligible applicants must have a bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0, be under 30 years old, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The application deadline is January 10, 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.yok.gov.tr/

### Academic Staff Training Program
The Academic Staff Training Program provides a monthly stipend of 3,000 Turkish Lira, 100% tuition fee coverage, and research funding of up to 15,000 Turkish Lira. Eligible applicants must have a master's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.5, be under 35 years old, and meet the language proficiency requirements. The application deadline is April 1, 2027, and more information can be found on the official website: https://www.tubitak.gov.tr/

## Application Steps
1. Check the eligibility criteria for each scholarship program.
2. Prepare the required documents, including transcripts, diplomas, and language proficiency certificates.
3. Choose the desired graduate program and university.
4. Submit the online application through the official website.
5. Upload the required documents and fill out the application form.
6. Pay the application fee, if required.
7. Wait for the evaluation and selection process.
8. Receive the scholarship award letter and complete the registration process.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. What are the language proficiency requirements for Turkey government scholarships?
The language proficiency requirements vary depending on the scholarship program and the language of instruction. Generally, applicants must provide a valid English or Turkish language proficiency certificate, such as TOEFL or TÖMER.
2. Can I apply for multiple scholarship programs at the same time?
Yes, applicants can apply for multiple scholarship programs, but they must meet the eligibility criteria for each program.
3. How long does the evaluation and selection process take?
The evaluation and selection process typically takes 2-3 months, but it may vary depending on the scholarship program and the number of applicants.
4. Can I change my graduate program or university after receiving the scholarship?
Generally, applicants can change their graduate program or university, but they must obtain approval from the scholarship authority and meet the eligibility criteria for the new program.
5. What is the duration of the Turkey government scholarships?
The duration of the scholarships varies depending on the program, but generally ranges from 1-4 years.
6. Can I bring my family with me to Turkey?
Yes, scholarship holders can bring their family members to Turkey, but they must meet the requirements for family residence permits.
7. How much is the monthly stipend for Turkey government scholarships?
The monthly stipend varies depending on the scholarship program, but generally ranges from 1,000 to 3,000 Turkish Lira.
8. What are the career opportunities after completing my graduate studies in Turkey?
Graduates can pursue various career opportunities in Turkey or their home countries, including academia, research, and industry.
