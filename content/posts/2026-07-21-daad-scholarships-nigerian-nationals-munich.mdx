The German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) Fully Funded Scholarships exists for Nigerian Nationals to study at University of Munich, Germany with funding amount of up to €750 per month. Nigerian nationals who are international students can apply for this scholarship to pursue their higher education in Germany. This scholarship is one of the many [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) available to international students.

## Who Qualifies
The DAAD scholarship is available to Nigerian nationals who have a strong academic background and are interested in pursuing their higher education in Germany. The scholarship is open to students from all fields of study, including [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd) and [masters scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/masters). To be eligible, applicants must have a valid visa category, such as a student visa or a research visa. The following visa categories are eligible for the DAAD scholarship:
* Student visa
* Research visa
* Language course visa
* Internship visa
