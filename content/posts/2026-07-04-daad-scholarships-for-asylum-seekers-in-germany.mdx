## DAAD Fully Funded Scholarships for Asylum Seekers in Germany
The DAAD fully funded scholarships are available to asylum seekers in Germany, providing them with the opportunity to pursue their higher education goals without financial burdens.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the DAAD fully funded scholarships, asylum seekers must fall under one of the following visa categories:
* Subsidiary protection
* Refugee status
* Asylum seeker with a temporary residence permit
* Person with a temporary suspension of deportation
* Person with a permanent residence permit due to asylum reasons

## Available Scholarships
### Scholarship 1: Helmut-Schmidt-Programme
The Helmut-Schmidt-Programme provides a monthly scholarship of 850 euros, covering tuition fees and living costs. Eligible candidates must have a bachelor's degree and be admitted to a master's program in Germany. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.daad.de/en/](https://www.daad.de/en/).

## Application Steps
1. Check the eligibility criteria for the desired scholarship program.
2. Gather all required documents, including academic certificates and language proficiency test results.
3. Fill out the online application form on the DAAD website.
4. Submit the application form and required documents before the deadline.
5. Wait for the application to be processed and reviewed.
6. Receive notification of the application result.
7. Accept the scholarship offer and enroll in the desired program.
8. Begin the academic program and receive the monthly stipend.
