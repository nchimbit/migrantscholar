## Introduction to Australia Awards Scholarships for Migrant Students from Turkey
If you're a migrant student from Turkey looking to pursue postgraduate studies in Australia, the Australia Awards Scholarships are an excellent opportunity to consider. These scholarships, offered by the Australian government, provide financial support to international students, including those from Turkey, to undertake master's or PhD programs at Australian universities. The Australia Awards Scholarships aim to promote knowledge, education links, and enduring ties between Australia and its partner countries, including Turkey.

## Who Qualifies for the Australia Awards Scholarships
To be eligible for the Australia Awards Scholarships, migrant students from Turkey must hold a valid visa that falls under one of the following categories:
* Permanent Resident (subclass 801)
* Temporary Graduate (subclass 485)
* Skilled Independent (subclass 189)
* Skilled Nominated (subclass 190)
* Temporary Skilled Migration visa (subclass 457 or 482)
