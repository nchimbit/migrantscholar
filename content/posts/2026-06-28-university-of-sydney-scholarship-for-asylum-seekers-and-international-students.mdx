## Introduction to University of Sydney Scholarship
The University of Sydney offers various scholarships to asylum seekers in Australia and international students from Canada, covering undergraduate and graduate degrees. These scholarships aim to support students who are facing financial difficulties in pursuing their higher education. The University of Sydney Scholarship for asylum seekers and international students provides an opportunity for students to study at one of the world's top universities.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the University of Sydney Scholarship, asylum seekers in Australia and international students from Canada must meet specific visa requirements. The following visa categories are eligible:
* Asylum seeker visa (subclass 866)
* Temporary Protection visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise visa (subclass 790)
* Permanent Protection visa (subclass 866)
* Student visa (subclass 500) for international students from Canada

## Scholarships Available
The University of Sydney offers several scholarships to support asylum seekers and international students. Here are five available scholarships:
### Sydney Scholars Award
The Sydney Scholars Award provides $6,000 to $10,000 per annum for undergraduate students. To be eligible, students must have achieved an ATAR of 95 or above. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/sydney-scholars-award.html.
### University of Sydney Access Scheme
The University of Sydney Access Scheme offers $2,000 to $5,000 per annum for undergraduate students. Eligible students must be from a low socio-economic background. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.sydney.edu.au/access-scheme.
### Australia Awards Scholarship
The Australia Awards Scholarship provides full tuition fees and living costs for undergraduate and graduate students. To be eligible, students must be from a developing country, including Canada. The deadline for application is April 2027. For more information, visit https://www.australiaawards.gov.au.
### Canada-Australia Student Mobility Program
The Canada-Australia Student Mobility Program offers $5,000 to $10,000 per annum for undergraduate and graduate students. Eligible students must be from Canada and studying at the University of Sydney. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.studyinaustralia.gov.au.
### Humanitarian Scholarship
The Humanitarian Scholarship provides full tuition fees and living costs for undergraduate and graduate students. To be eligible, students must be asylum seekers or refugees. The deadline for application is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.sydney.edu.au/humanitarian-scholarship.
