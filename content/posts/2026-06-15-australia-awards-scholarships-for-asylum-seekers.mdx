## Introduction to Australia Awards Scholarships for Asylum Seekers
The Australia Awards Scholarships are available to asylum seekers who are looking to pursue undergraduate and graduate studies in Australia. These scholarships provide an opportunity for individuals who have been granted asylum or are in the process of seeking asylum to further their education and build a better future. The scholarships cover various fields of study, including but not limited to, engineering, humanities, and social sciences.

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the Australia Awards Scholarships, asylum seekers must hold a valid Australian visa, including:
* Temporary Protection Visa (subclass 785)
* Safe Haven Enterprise Visa (subclass 790)
* Permanent Protection Visa (subclass 866)
* Humanitarian Stay (Temporary) Visa (subclass 449)
* Bridging Visa E (subclass 051)

## Available Scholarships
The following are some of the scholarships available to asylum seekers in Australia:
### Australian Awards Scholarship
The Australian Awards Scholarship provides full tuition fees, living expenses, and airfares to and from Australia, with a coverage amount of up to AUD 30,000 per year. To be eligible, applicants must have a strong academic record and demonstrate a commitment to their field of study. The deadline for application is 30 April 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/).
### International Postgraduate Research Scholarship
The International Postgraduate Research Scholarship provides a stipend of AUD 28,597 per year and a relocation allowance of up to AUD 1,500. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree with honors or a master's degree and be enrolled in a research-based postgraduate program. The deadline for application is 31 August 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.education.gov.au/international-postgraduate-research-scholarship](https://www.education.gov.au/international-postgraduate-research-scholarship).
### Australia Awards Pacific Scholarships
The Australia Awards Pacific Scholarships provide full tuition fees, living expenses, and airfares to and from Australia, with a coverage amount of up to AUD 30,000 per year. To be eligible, applicants must be from a participating Pacific country and have a strong academic record. The deadline for application is 15 January 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/pacific](https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/pacific).
### Endeavour Postgraduate Scholarship
The Endeavour Postgraduate Scholarship provides a stipend of up to AUD 140,000 per year and a travel allowance of up to AUD 3,000. To be eligible, applicants must have a bachelor's degree with honors or a master's degree and be enrolled in a postgraduate program. The deadline for application is 15 November 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.education.gov.au/endeavour-scholarships](https://www.education.gov.au/endeavour-scholarships).
### University of Sydney Scholarship
The University of Sydney Scholarship provides a stipend of up to AUD 35,000 per year and a tuition fee waiver. To be eligible, applicants must have a strong academic record and be enrolled in a research-based postgraduate program at the University of Sydney. The deadline for application is 30 September 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships](https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships).

## Application Steps
To apply for the Australia Awards Scholarships, follow these steps:
1. Check your eligibility and ensure you meet the requirements.
2. Choose the scholarship you wish to apply for and read the eligibility criteria and application process.
3. Prepare your documents, including your academic transcripts, English language proficiency test results, and passport.
4. Submit your application through the online application portal.
5. Pay the application fee, if required.
6. Upload your supporting documents, including your academic transcripts and English language proficiency test results.
7. Submit your application and wait for the outcome.
8. If successful, accept the scholarship offer and begin your studies in Australia.
