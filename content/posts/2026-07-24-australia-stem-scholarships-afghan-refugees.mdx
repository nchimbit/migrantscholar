# Australia STEM

## Who Qualifies
Afghan refugees who hold a valid Australian visa, such as a Permanent Humanitarian Visa, a Temporary Protection Visa, or a Safe Haven Enterprise Visa, are eligible to apply for these scholarships. They must also meet the specific eligibility criteria for each scholarship program, including academic requirements and language proficiency.
