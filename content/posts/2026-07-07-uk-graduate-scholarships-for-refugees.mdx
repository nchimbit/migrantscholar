## Fully Funded Graduate Scholarships for Refugees in the UK
Fully funded graduate scholarships are available for refugees in the UK, providing opportunities for higher education and a chance to rebuild their lives. 
### Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these scholarships, refugees must hold one of the following visa categories:
* Refugee status
* Humanitarian Protection
* Discretionary Leave
* Leave outside the Rules
* Section 67 of the Immigration Act 2016 leave
* Section 3C of the Immigration Act 1971 leave
* Other forms of leave that are not subject to immigration restrictions

## Application Process
To apply for these scholarships, refugees must follow these steps:
1. Check their eligibility for the scholarship
2. Apply for a course at a UK university
3. Receive a conditional offer from the university
4. Prepare the necessary documentation, including proof of refugee status and academic transcripts
5. Submit the scholarship application by the deadline
6. Wait for the scholarship decision
7. Accept the scholarship offer and begin studying
8. Maintain good academic progress and comply with the terms of the scholarship

## Documents Checklist
To apply for these scholarships, refugees will need to provide the following documents:
* Proof of refugee status
* Academic transcripts
* Proof of English language proficiency
* Personal statement
* References
* Passport
* Proof of address
