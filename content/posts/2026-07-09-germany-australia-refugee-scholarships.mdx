## Germany and Australia's Joint Scholarship Initiative for Refugee Students
The Germany and Australia Joint Scholarship Initiative is available to refugee students holding specific visa categories, providing them with the opportunity to pursue higher education in either country. 
Who qualifies for this initiative includes individuals with the following visa categories:
* Humanitarian visa
* Protection visa
* Temporary Protection visa
* Safe Haven Enterprise visa
* Refugee visa
* Asylum seeker visa
* Temporary Humanitarian Concern visa
* Temporary Humanitarian Stay visa

### Deutschland Stipendium
The Deutschland Stipendium offers a coverage amount of €300 per month, with eligibility open to undergraduate and postgraduate students from all disciplines, application deadline is 15th November 2026, and the official website is https://www.daad.de/deutschland-stipendium/.

### Australia Awards Scholarship
The Australia Awards Scholarship provides full tuition fees, living expenses, and a return airfare, with eligibility open to postgraduate students from developing countries, application deadline is 1st May 2027, and the official website is https://www.australiaawards.gov.au/.

### Sydney Scholar Award
The Sydney Scholar Award offers a coverage amount of AUD 40,000 per annum, with eligibility open to undergraduate and postgraduate students, application deadline is 30th September 2026, and the official website is https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/.

### Friedrich Ebert Foundation Scholarship
The Friedrich Ebert Foundation Scholarship provides a coverage amount of €750 per month, with eligibility open to undergraduate and postgraduate students from developing countries, application deadline is 31st October 2026, and the official website is https://www.fes.de/en/scholarship.

### Konrad Adenauer Foundation Scholarship
The Konrad Adenauer Foundation Scholarship offers a coverage amount of €1,200 per month, with eligibility open to postgraduate students from developing countries, application deadline is 15th July 2027, and the official website is https://www.kas.de/en/scholarship.
