## Introduction to Turkish Government Scholarships in Germany
The Turkish Government offers various scholarships to international students in Germany, providing them with the opportunity to pursue their higher education in a foreign country. These scholarships are designed to support migrant students who wish to study in Germany, covering a significant portion of their tuition fees and living expenses. If you are an international student looking to study in Germany, the Turkish Government Scholarships can be a great option for you, with various programs available for the year 2026.

## Who Qualifies for Turkish Government Scholarships
To be eligible for the Turkish Government Scholarships, you must belong to one of the following visa categories:
* Visa for study purposes (Student Visa)
* Visa for research purposes (Research Visa)
* Visa for language course (Language Course Visa)
* Visa for internship purposes (Internship Visa)
* Visa for vocational training (Vocational Training Visa)

## Available Scholarships
The Turkish Government offers several scholarships to international students in Germany, including:
### Turkey Scholarships for International Students
The Turkey Scholarships for International Students is a fully-funded scholarship program that covers tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs, with a coverage amount of €650 per month.
