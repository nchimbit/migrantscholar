The [Google Anita Borg Memorial Scholarship](https://www.google.com/education/scholarships/anita-borg-scholarship/) exists for female refugees in the USA, providing $10,000 in funding for women pursuing degrees in computer science and computer engineering. 
This scholarship aims to encourage women to pursue careers in technology and become active leaders in the field. 
The USA provides a vast array of [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) for refugees, particularly women who want to pursue [STEM fields](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-field/stem). 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for the [Google Anita Borg Memorial Scholarship](https://www.google.com/education/scholarships/anita-borg-scholarship/), female refugees in the USA must hold an F-1 or J-1 visa, or be a green card holder or naturalized citizen. 
Applicants with the following visa categories are also eligible: F-2, J-2, H-4, L-2, or O-3. 
For those interested in other options, such as [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada) or [PhD scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-level/phd), it is essential to review the eligibility requirements for each specific program.
