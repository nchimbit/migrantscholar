## Turkish Government Research Scholarships for International Students
The Turkish Government Research Scholarships are available to international students, including migrants and refugees, who wish to pursue research studies in Turkey. 

## Who Qualifies
The following international students qualify for the Turkish Government Research Scholarships:
* Conditional migrants with a valid residence permit
* Refugees with a valid residence permit
* asylum seekers with a valid residence permit
* Migrants with a valid work permit
* Students with a valid student visa

## Available Scholarships
### Turkey Scholarships Graduate Program
The Turkey Scholarships Graduate Program provides a monthly stipend of 2,500 Turkish Lira, full tuition fees, and health insurance to international students who wish to pursue a master's or PhD degree in Turkey. The eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0, English language proficiency, and a research proposal. The deadline for application is February 2027. For more information, visit https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/.
