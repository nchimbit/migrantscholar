The Fulbright Scholarship exists and qualifies Indian migrant students pursuing higher education in the USA, offering funding of up to $100,000. Indian migrant students can apply for various USA visa options to pursue their higher education dreams. ## Who Qualifies
Indian migrant students who qualify for a USA student visa can apply under different visa categories, including F-1, J-1, and M-1 visas. F-1 visa is for academic studies, J-1 visa is for exchange programs, and M-1 visa is for vocational studies. To be eligible for these visas, Indian migrant students must demonstrate their intention to return to their home country after completing their studies and prove that they have sufficient funds to support themselves during their stay in the USA.

## Application Steps
1. Research and choose a US institution that offers [Canada scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/countries/Canada) or other international scholarships.
2. Check the [scholarship deadlines](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/deadlines) and apply for admission to the chosen institution.
3. Receive an admission letter and I-20 form from the institution.
4. Apply for a USA student visa at the US embassy or consulate in India.
5. Prepare and submit all required documents, including transcripts, diplomas, and test scores.
6. Attend a visa interview at the US embassy or consulate.
7. Receive the visa decision and make travel arrangements to the USA.
8. Register with the US institution and attend orientation programs.
