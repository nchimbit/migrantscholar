The Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung Scholarship exists for Indian asylum seekers, offering a funding amount of up to €1,200 per month for graduate education in Germany.

## Who Qualifies
Indian asylum seekers with a residence permit, in particular, those with a visa category of subsidiary protection or asylum, are eligible to apply for various scholarships in Germany. The following visa categories are also eligible: 
- Subsidiary protection 
- Asylum 
- Humanitarian residence permit 
- Temporary residence permit for humanitarian reasons

## Available Scholarships
### DAAD Scholarship
The DAAD Scholarship offers up to €1,200 per month, and is eligible to Indian asylum seekers with a Bachelor's degree, applying to a Master's program. The deadline is usually in October, and the official URL is https://www.daad.de/en/. 

### Friedrich Ebert Foundation Scholarship
The Friedrich Ebert Foundation Scholarship offers up to €1,200 per month, and is eligible to Indian asylum seekers with a Bachelor's degree, applying to a Master's program, who are committed to social democracy. The deadline is usually in October, and the official URL is https://www.fes.de/en/. 

### Heinrich Böll Foundation Scholarship
The Heinrich Böll Foundation Scholarship offers up to €1,200 per month, and is eligible to Indian asylum seekers with a Bachelor's degree, applying to a Master's program, who are committed to democracy and human rights. The deadline is usually in September, and the official URL is https://www.boell.de/en. 

### Rosa Luxemburg Foundation Scholarship
The Rosa Luxemburg Foundation Scholarship offers up to €1,200 per month, and is eligible to Indian asylum seekers with a Bachelor's degree, applying to a Master's program, who are committed to social justice. The deadline is usually in October, and the official URL is https://www.rosalux.de/en. 

### Hanns Seidel Foundation Scholarship
The Hanns Seidel Foundation Scholarship offers up to €1,200 per month, and is eligible to Indian asylum seekers with a Bachelor's degree, applying to a Master's program, who are committed to social responsibility. The deadline is usually in October, and the official URL is https://www.hss.de/en/.
