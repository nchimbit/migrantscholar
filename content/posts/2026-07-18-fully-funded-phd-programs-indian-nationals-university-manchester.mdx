# UK PhD
The President's Doctoral Scholar Award at the University of Manchester, UK exists and qualifies Indian nationals for a funding amount of full tuition fees and a stipend of £15,609 per annum. Indian nationals can apply for this award, as well as other [fully funded scholarships](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-funding/fully-funded) at the University of Manchester. 

## Who Qualifies
To be eligible for these PhD programs, Indian nationals must meet specific visa categories, including Tier 4 student visa requirements. They must also meet the University's English language proficiency requirements and have a strong academic record. Indian nationals who are [refugees](https://migrantscholar.vercel.app/by-eligibility/refugees) or have been displaced may also be eligible for these programs.

## Scholarships
### President's Doctoral Scholar Award 
* Amount: full tuition fees and a stipend of £15,609 per annum
* Eligibility: Indian nationals with a strong academic record
* Deadline: 1 December 2026
* Official URL: https://www.manchester.ac.uk/study/postgraduate-research/funding/presidents-doctoral-scholar-award/

### Commonwealth Scholarship 
* Amount: full tuition fees and a stipend of £1,130 per month
* Eligibility: Indian nationals who are Commonwealth citizens
* Deadline: 1 November 2026
* Official URL: https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/

### Chevening Scholarship 
* Amount: full tuition fees and a stipend of £1,130 per month
* Eligibility: Indian nationals with a strong academic record and leadership potential
* Deadline: 1 November 2026
* Official URL: https://www.chevening.org/

### Felix Scholarship 
* Amount: full tuition fees and a stipend of £14,777 per annum
* Eligibility: Indian nationals with a strong academic record
* Deadline: 31 January 2027
* Official URL: https://www.felixscholarship.org/

### UoM India Scholarship 
* Amount: 50% of tuition fees
* Eligibility: Indian nationals with a strong academic record
* Deadline: 1 June 2027
* Official URL: https://www.manchester.ac.uk/study/international/country-specific-scholarships/india/
