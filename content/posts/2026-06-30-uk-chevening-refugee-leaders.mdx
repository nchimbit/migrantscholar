## Introduction to UK Chevening Scholarships for Asylum Seekers and Refugee Leaders
The UK Chevening Scholarships are an excellent opportunity for asylum seekers and refugee leaders to pursue a Master's degree in leadership and related fields in the UK. These scholarships aim to support individuals with leadership potential to acquire the knowledge and skills necessary to make a positive impact in their communities. By offering full tuition plus living costs, the UK Chevening Scholarships provide a unique chance for asylum seekers and refugee leaders to achieve their academic and professional goals.

## Who Qualifies for the UK Chevening Scholarships
To be eligible for the UK Chevening Scholarships, applicants must hold a valid visa in one of the following categories:
* Refugee status
* Humanitarian Protection
* Discretionary Leave
* Leave outside the Rules
* UASC Leave
* Section 67 leave
* Section 4(1) of the Immigration Act 1999 (hard cases)

## Available Scholarships
The following scholarships are available for asylum seekers and refugee leaders to pursue a Master's degree in leadership and related fields:
### Chevening Scholarship
The Chevening Scholarship provides full tuition fees and a monthly stipend to cover living costs. Eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, at least two years of work experience, and proficiency in English. The deadline for applications is November 2026. For more information, visit https://www.chevening.org/
### Commonwealth Scholarship
The Commonwealth Scholarship offers full tuition fees, a monthly stipend, and airfare to and from the UK. Eligibility criteria include a bachelor's degree, at least two years of work experience, and proficiency in English. The deadline for applications is December 2026. For more information, visit https://cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk/
