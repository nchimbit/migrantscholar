## Introduction to Canada Government Scholarship for Undergraduate International Students
The Canada government scholarship for undergraduate international students is a great opportunity for migrant students to pursue their higher education in Canada. The Canadian government offers various scholarships to international students, including undergraduate students, to help them cover their tuition fees and living expenses. These scholarships are designed to attract top talent from around the world and provide students with the opportunity to experience Canadian culture and education.

* Study permit holder

* Permanent resident applicant

## Available Scholarships
### Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships
The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships is a prestigious scholarship that provides $50,000 per year for three years to international students pursuing their undergraduate degree in Canada. To be eligible, you must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and be nominated by your university. The deadline for application is November 1st, and you can find more information on the official website: https://www.vanier.gc.ca.

### Canada Graduate Scholarships-Master's Program
The Canada Graduate Scholarships-Master's Program provides $17,500 for 12 months to international students pursuing their undergraduate degree in Canada. To be eligible, you must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be enrolled in a master's program. The deadline for application is December 1st, and you can find more information on the official website: https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca.

### Queen Elizabeth II Diamond Jubilee Scholarships
The Queen Elizabeth II Diamond Jubilee Scholarships provides $7,500 for one year to international students pursuing their undergraduate degree in Canada. To be eligible, you must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be enrolled in a program that focuses on international development. The deadline for application is January 15th, and you can find more information on the official website: https://www.queensjubileescholarships.org.

### Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarship
The Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Scholarship provides $60,000 over three years to international students pursuing their undergraduate degree in Canada. To be eligible, you must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and be enrolled in a program that focuses on social sciences and humanities. The deadline for application is January 20th, and you can find more information on the official website: https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca.

### Emerging Leaders in the Americas Program
The Emerging Leaders in the Americas Program provides $7,200 for one year to international students pursuing their undergraduate degree in Canada. To be eligible, you must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be enrolled in a program that focuses on international development. The deadline for application is February 1st, and you can find more information on the official website: https://www.educanada.ca.
