# Canada Visa
The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship, which awards $50,000 per year to highly qualified international students, including German citizens, pursuing a PhD in Canada, is a prestigious award that recognizes academic excellence. German citizens who are skilled workers can benefit from Canada's visa program, which provides a pathway to permanent residency. To be eligible, German citizens must meet the requirements of one of the visa categories, such as the Federal Skilled Worker Program or the Federal Skilled Trades Program. 

## Who Qualifies
German citizens who are skilled workers can qualify for Canada's visa program through various visa categories, including:
* Federal Skilled Worker Program
* Federal Skilled Trades Program
* Canadian Experience Class
* Quebec Skilled Worker Program
* Provincial Nominee Program
These programs require applicants to have relevant work experience, education, and language proficiency in English or French.

## Application Steps
1. Check eligibility for the visa category and scholarship
2. Gather required documents, including passport, resume, and language proficiency test results
3. Submit an expression of interest to the province or territory
4. Receive an invitation to apply for the visa
5. Submit the visa application, including all required documents
6. Pay the application fee
7. Wait for the application to be processed
8. Receive a decision on the visa application

## Documents Checklist
* Passport
* Resume
* Language proficiency test results (e.g. IELTS, TOEFL, or TCF)
* Transcripts and diplomas
* Proof of work experience
* Police certificate
* Medical certificate
* Proof of funds
