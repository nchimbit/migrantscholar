The Carl-Zeiss-Stiftung Scholarship exists and qualifies international women students for a funding amount of up to 25,000 euros. 

## Who Qualifies
The Carl-Zeiss-Stiftung Scholarship is open to international women students who are pursuing a research fellowship at top universities in Germany. To be eligible, applicants must hold a valid visa, such as a student visa or a research visa, and must meet the specific requirements of the scholarship. The visa categories for international students in Germany include student visa, language course visa, and research visa.
