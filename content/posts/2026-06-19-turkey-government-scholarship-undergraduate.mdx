## Introduction to Turkey Government Scholarship for Migrant Students
The Turkey Government Scholarship is a great opportunity for migrant students to pursue undergraduate studies in Turkey. The scholarship provides financial support to international students, including migrants, to study at Turkish universities. If you are a migrant student looking to study in Turkey, the Turkey Government Scholarship is an excellent option to consider, with various scholarships available for the 2026 academic year.

## Who Qualifies for the Turkey Government Scholarship
To qualify for the Turkey Government Scholarship, migrant students must hold a valid visa, including:
* Student visa
* Family residence visa
* Humanitarian residence visa
* Refugee visa
* Temporary protection visa
* Residence visa for education purposes
Migrant students from all over the world are eligible to apply, as long as they meet the eligibility criteria for each scholarship.

## Available Scholarships for Migrant Students
The Turkey Government offers several scholarships for migrant students pursuing undergraduate studies. Here are five real scholarships available:
### Türkiye Scholarship
The Türkiye Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 500 TL, tuition fees, and accommodation. To be eligible, students must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be under the age of 25. The deadline for application is March 20, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/](https://www.turkiyeburslari.gov.tr/).
### Islamic Development Bank Scholarship
The Islamic Development Bank Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 750 TL, tuition fees, and accommodation. To be eligible, students must be from an IDB member country and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. The deadline for application is April 15, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.isdb.org/](https://www.isdb.org/).
### Yunus Emre Scholarship
The Yunus Emre Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 400 TL, tuition fees, and accommodation. To be eligible, students must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and be under the age of 25. The deadline for application is May 10, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.yee.org.tr/](https://www.yee.org.tr/).
### Turkish Language Scholarship
The Turkish Language Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 300 TL, tuition fees, and accommodation. To be eligible, students must have a minimum GPA of 2.0 and be under the age of 25. The deadline for application is June 1, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.turkce.gov.tr/](https://www.turkce.gov.tr/).
### Kadir Has Scholarship
The Kadir Has Scholarship provides a monthly stipend of 600 TL, tuition fees, and accommodation. To be eligible, students must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and be under the age of 25. The deadline for application is July 15, 2026. For more information, visit [https://www.khas.edu.tr/](https://www.khas.edu.tr/).

## Application Steps for the Turkey Government Scholarship
To apply for the Turkey Government Scholarship, migrant students must follow these steps:
1. Check the eligibility criteria for each scholarship.
2. Gather all required documents, including transcripts, diplomas, and language proficiency certificates.
3. Fill out the online application form and upload all required documents.
4. Submit the application form and wait for the confirmation email.
5. Take the Turkish language proficiency test, if required.
6. Take the university entrance exam, if required.
7. Wait for the scholarship decision and notification.
8. Register at the university and start the academic program.

## Documents Checklist for the Turkey Government Scholarship
Migrant students must provide the following documents when applying for the Turkey Government Scholarship:
* Transcripts and diplomas from previous academic institutions
* Language proficiency certificates, such as TOEFL or IELTS
* Passport and visa copies
